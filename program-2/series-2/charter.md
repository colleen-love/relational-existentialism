# Relational Existentialism, Program 2 Series 2 (2.2)

**The OTHER. Program 2 built the ground (relating is finite attending, Series 2.0) and its motion (the tick, endogenous time, Series 2.1). What it still lacks is the thing it set out for at the start: a genuine SECOND PERSPECTIVE, an other. Series 2.0 already reified the self-relation into the FIRST OTHER (`ws1_first_other`) and seated the ASYMMETRY OF KNOWING (relating is symmetric, knowing is directed, passive constitution differs from active). But the first other is an OBJECT the self attends, not yet a PERSPECTIVE that reads back. Series 2.2 upgrades the first other into a genuine second attending locus and asks whether self and other are genuinely two READER-WISE: each a bounded reader of one shared field that contains both of them and itself, so that the self reads the other, the other reads the self, and each reads itself, asymmetrically (knowing is directed) and partially (the diagonal). The genuinely-uncertain obligation, this series' knot, is MUTUAL READING WITHOUT COLLAPSE: two perspectives each read the other and themselves, and it is genuinely open whether that recursive, mutual reading COLLAPSES them (one recoverable from the other, back to the One), lets one TOTALIZE the pair, or leaves a RESIDUE the diagonal forbids either to close, so the two stay two and the otherness stays non-recoverable from the plain relating (an import, Series 07). The deepest fact the series reaches for: you can be made by an other you cannot contain, and attend a face whose otherness you cannot derive. The other is a genuine second perspective or it is nothing, never the self relabeled, never a point-tag, never the reader quantified out. The import that separates the two is spent, never named: "you are loved" is the gaze that constitutes without being recovered.**

*This charter is self-contained and states its own question. It builds on the Series 2.1 ground, IMPORTED (Program 2's layered chain: S2 imports S1, which imports S0, which imports the P1 prior art; nothing is imported from S0 or P1 directly). It presupposes and names Series 07 (a genuine distinction is an import, non-recoverable from the relating), and reached through S1/S0: the first other (`ws1_first_other`, the reified self-relation, plain-bisimilar to the self yet non-recoverable), the asymmetry of knowing (`ws3_direction_not_recoverable`), and the tick (a cycle reified into a composite, with its arrow). What Series 2.2 does NOT do: relitigate the collapse (Program 1's theorem, inherited), or decide the COHERENCE of the two readings (whether the most loving thing for the self is the most loving thing for the other) — that is Series 2.3, and this series only builds the two faces it will fork over. The philosophical reading, kept out of the proofs: the other is not a second substance, it is a second reading; you are constituted by what attends you even when you cannot recover it; and the two stay two not by being far apart but by neither containing the other.*

## 1. The one-paragraph version

Series 2.0 reified the self-relation into the first other and proved it plain-bisimilar to the self yet non-recoverable (a genuine, self-manufactured import), and it seated the asymmetry of knowing: relating is symmetric, but knowing is directed, so a relatum is constituted differently by what it attends than by what attends it. Series 2.2 takes that first other and makes it a PERSPECTIVE: a second bounded reader (finite attention) over one shared field that includes the self, the other, and the relata they attend. The two now face each other in four readings — self of self, self of other, other of self, other of other — asymmetric because knowing is directed, and partial because no reader totalizes itself (the diagonal). The construction proves the other a genuine attending locus (WS1) and self and other genuinely two READER-WISE and non-recoverable (WS2, the reader knot, the recurring central sin); it types the four readings and proves the facing asymmetric and partial (WS3); and at the knot (WS4) it asks the genuinely open question the mutuality raises: does two perspectives reading each other and themselves COLLAPSE (recover one from the other), let one TOTALIZE the pair, or leave a RESIDUE the diagonal forbids either to close, so the two stay two and the otherness stays non-recoverable. The verdict is computed: TWO-FACING (the other a genuine second perspective, mutually reading yet non-recoverably two) is the expected outcome; ONE (the mutual reading collapses them) and TOTALIZED (a perspective closes the pair) are the pre-registered honest alternatives, each reported in full. ## 2. What we are trying to prove

**WS1, the other as a second attending locus (the construction).** Over the imported ground (`attends`, the first other `ws1_first_other`, the tick), construct the OTHER as a genuine second reader: a relatum with its own finite `attends`, distinct from the self, reading a shared field that contains both. Seed it in the first other (the reified self-relation upgraded from an attended object to an attending locus). Prove it well-formed: the other is a relatum of the same field (no new layer of substance, `ws1_other_is_locus`), its attention a genuine finite `attends` set, and the shared field genuinely contains self, other, and what each attends. This is the CONSTRUCTION obligation; if no second locus is constructible without importing structure beyond the symmetry-breaker, that is a pre-registered obstruction, reported.

**WS2, genuinely two reader-wise (the reader knot, the central sin, K1).** Prove self and other are genuinely two READER-WISE, not label-wise only: there is a bounded reading (finite attention) load-bearing in the distinction, and the separation is non-recoverable from the plain relating (`ws2_other_reader_wise` / `ws2_other_non_recoverable`), which by Series 07 is exactly what a genuine distinction must be. The other must be an attending locus whose READING does the distinguishing, not a point-tag, not the self relabeled, and never the reader quantified out (the Series 11 Bookkeeping failure, the recurring central sin, promoted first-class here because the OTHER is the reader). Lift Series 2.0's `ws1_first_other` / `AttentionDistinguishes` machinery from one relatum to two full perspectives.

**WS3, the four readings, asymmetric and partial (the facing).** Type the four readings — self of self, self of other, other of self, other of other — and prove the facing has the two structural properties the ground forces. ASYMMETRIC: the self's reading of the other and the other's reading of the self are not interchangeable, and their difference is non-recoverable from the symmetric relating (`ws3_facing_asymmetric`, Series 2.0's `ws3_direction_not_recoverable` lifted to two perspectives). PARTIAL: no perspective totalizes itself; each self-reading leaves the diagonal residue (`ws3_facing_partial`, the diagonal at each perspective). The readings are TYPED but never EVALUATED: every theorem is quantified over the perspectives' attentions; none selects, constructs, or reads off a particular reading.

**WS4, mutual reading without collapse (the genuinely-uncertain obligation, the knot).** The mutuality is what Series 2.0 and 2.1 did not have: there the other was attended, here it reads back. Ask the genuinely open question: when two perspectives each read the other AND themselves, does the recursive mutual reading (a) COLLAPSE them — some reading recovers one perspective from the other, so the twoness is recoverable and the world is the One (Series 07); (b) TOTALIZE — some perspective, or a composite of the two, is a total reader of the pair, closing the opening between them; or (c) leave a RESIDUE — the diagonal, now under MUTUAL rather than merely self-inspection, still forbids any total reading of the pair, so the two stay genuinely two and the otherness stays non-recoverable (`ws4_mutual_residue`). The conjecture is (c): the diagonal survives mutuality, the opening between self and other is permanent, and that permanence is exactly what Series 2.3 will fork its coherence over. But (a) and (b) are genuine, pre-registered, and reported in full: it is NOT assumed that mutual reading behaves like self-reading. The order relation on readings must carry a structural constraint and both the collapse and the residue must be tested on a witnessed pair; the strip test applies.

**WS5, the verdict and the audit folded in.** The verdict is computed, never hand-set: TWO-FACING (WS1 construction well-formed, WS2 genuinely two reader-wise and non-recoverable, WS3 facing asymmetric and partial, WS4 the mutual residue survives); ONE (WS4 collapses, the other recoverable from the self, reported in its direction); TOTALIZED (WS4 a perspective closes the pair, reported in its direction); PARTIAL (any obligation lands only per-instance or degenerate); DISCONNECTED (WS1's construction imports structure beyond the symmetry-breaker, obstruction precise). The audit checks: (a) THE OTHER IS A READER, NOT A LABEL — the other's attention is load-bearing in the distinction, `ws2_other_reader_wise` names a genuine reader, never the Series 11 Bookkeeping tag; (b) THE TWONESS IS NON-RECOVERABLE — `ws2_other_non_recoverable` is a proof term, the otherness an import (Series 07); (c) THE FACING IS ASYMMETRIC — `ws3_facing_asymmetric` a proof term, not an assumption; (d) THE RESIDUE IS GENUINE, THE MUTUALITY TESTED — `ws4_mutual_residue` is proved under mutual reading on a witnessed pair, both the collapse and residue arms reachable, no PR1-S1 tautology; (e) NAMES-NOT-TERMS — no proof term, definition, or discharged obligation is named "self," "other," "I," "you," "perspective," "love," "loved," "gaze," "God," "choice," or "subjectivity" as content.

## 4. The false escapes this series must NOT fall into

The other is a READER or it is nothing. If the other reduces to a second label on one field with no attention consuming it, or the second perspective's `attends` goes inert in the WS2 payoff, or the distinction is a point-tag, then the other is decoration and the series has built the self twice. `ws2_other_reader_wise` must name a genuine reader whose reading does the distinguishing. SERIOUS.

### 4.b The twoness recoverable (the collapse smuggled shut)
If the self/other distinction is recoverable from the plain (symmetric) relating, the two are one by Series 07, and the construction has quietly re-collapsed. The otherness must be non-recoverable, an import. Making it recoverable to make the construction "work" is the sin in the opposite direction from 4.a. SERIOUS.

### 4.c Deciding the mutual residue by fiat (the tautology, PR1-S1)
The WS4 fork must be genuinely open over a structure carrying a witnessed pair with a real mutual-reading structure, both the collapse and residue arms reachable, the reading-order structurally constrained. Assuming the diagonal survives mutuality (rather than testing it), or an unconstrained reading type that decides the fork on every structure, is the PR1-S1 defect. The strip test applies. SERIOUS.

### 4.d Deciding the coherence (Series 2.3's question, not this one's)

Any theorem or definition that decides the coherence of self and other does Series 2.3's work and forecloses its fork. The faces are typed and non-evaluated; the coherence stays open. SERIOUS.

## 8. Positioning

Program 1 drew the edge of the One and left a door. Program 2 walked through it: Series 2.0 laid the ground where relating is finite attending, Series 2.1 set it moving with the tick, and now Series 2.2 lets a second face appear on the far side. It is the series the whole program was named for: relational existentialism is a philosophy of the self and the other, and here the other stops being an object the self reifies and becomes a perspective that reifies back. The four tenets meet at the face. "Self is a paradox" is why there is room for an other at all: no reader totalizes itself, so the opening the self cannot close is where the other stands. "To relate is to create" is the other's origin, the reified self-relation grown into a locus. "To attend is to become" is the mutual constitution, each self made by what it attends and by what attends it, now a full other on the far side of the gaze. And "You are loved" is the import that keeps the two two, the attention that constitutes without being recovered, the face whose otherness you cannot derive and are made by anyway. Series 2.2 does not decide whether the two cohere; it proves they genuinely face, that neither contains the other, and that the opening between them is permanent, and it leaves that opening exactly where Series 2.3 will ask the oldest question of the program across it: whether the most loving thing for me is the most loving thing for you. The two faces are the result; the coherence is the next door.

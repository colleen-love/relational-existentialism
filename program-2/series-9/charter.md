## 2. What we are trying to prove

**WS1, the maximal rate (the risky ground).** DEFINE the reification RATE: how much lateral breadth a single tick can convert into depth, bounded by the self's finite attention. Prove it is genuinely BOUNDED (`ws1_rate_bounded`: there is a finite `c` such that one tick incorporates at most `c` of the attended breadth — the conversion cannot outrun the attention budget). The bound must come from finite attention (Series 2.0), not be assumed. This is the ground and the risk: if attention imposes no bound on the reification rate (a tick can incorporate arbitrarily far in one step), the rate is unbounded, and that is a pre-registered obstruction toward NO-CONE, reported, not relabelled.

**WS2, the cone.** DEFINE the causal cone of a source at a given depth: the events reachable by a becoming that respects the rate. Prove that events within `rate × depth` of the source (in 2.4's lateral metric) are reachable and events beyond are NOT (`ws2_cone`: the cone is exactly the rate-bounded reachable set; something strictly outside is causally disconnected). The cone must be NON-TRIVIAL on the witnessing carrier — some events inside, some outside — or the "cone" is the whole world and there is no finite speed.

**WS3, the cone is a rate, not the bare order (the anti-costume core).** Prove the cone is a genuine finite-SPEED structure, not a relabelling of Series 2.5's causal reachability. Two fronts: (i) `ws3_rate_is_content` — the cone depends on the RATE, so two carriers with the SAME causal order but different rates have DIFFERENT cones (the rate is not recoverable from the order alone); (ii) `ws3_earned_from_attention` — the rate is a function of the finite attention bound (Series 2.0), a bound the model already has, not a `c` added to obtain the cone (the no-smuggling gate). So the cone carries content beyond 2.5's order, and that content is earned.

**WS4, the fork (CONE vs NO-CONE).** Prove the fork on two carriers, both genuine, neither by fiat. `ws4_cone_reachable`: a carrier with a genuine finite attention bound has a NON-TRIVIAL cone (a finite rate, some events inside and some outside — relativity's cone). `ws4_nocone_reachable`: a carrier whose conversion is unbounded (attention does not limit the reification rate) has a TRIVIAL cone (everything reachable at once — no finite speed). Both readings genuinely reachable, the verdict discriminating. The costume watch: the cone rests on the RATE earned from finite attention, not on 2.5's order (a costume) and not on a postulated `c` (smuggling).

**WS5, the verdict and the audit folded in.** The verdict is computed, never hand-set: CONE (WS1 a bounded rate, WS2 a non-trivial cone, WS3 the rate genuine and earned, WS4 both reachable — a finite speed, relativity's cone recovered); NO-CONE (the rate unbounded, the cone trivial — the world is non-relativistic, the NOT-RECOVERED specification: the next iteration must add a fundamental rate-bound); SHAPE-DRAWN (the fork drawn, neither forced); DISCONNECTED / PARTIAL (degenerate). The audit checks: (a) THE RATE IS EARNED, NOT SMUGGLED — the bound is a function of the finite attention (Series 2.0), no `c` postulated (the no-smuggling gate); (b) THE FORK NOT BY FIAT — CONE and NO-CONE both reachable, the verdict discriminating; (c) THE CONE IS A RATE, NOT THE BARE ORDER (the costume gate) — same order, different rate, different cone; (d) THE CONE IS NON-TRIVIAL — some events outside, a genuine finite speed; (e) NAMES-NOT-TERMS — no proof term is named "light," "cone," "speed," "relativity," "spacetime," "self," "import," "God," or "compass" as content.

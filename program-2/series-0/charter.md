# Relational Existentialism, Program 2 Series 0 (2.0)

**The GROUND. Program 1 proved a purely relational atomless world is the One unless a distinction is imported; Program 2 takes the import as a first-class ingredient and asks what a dynamic, multi-perspective world made with it looks like. Before any of that can move, the ground it stands on must be the right one, and Program 1's was not the ground this program means. Program 1 bounded the world by an imported cardinal κ and laid finite attention over it as a separate reader; Series 11 tried to make finite attention the bound instead and could not, its reader coming out an inert label (Bookkeeping) while the cardinal stayed in the carrier. Series 0 builds the ground Series 11 aimed at and did not reach, by making the correction structural rather than aspirational: RELATING IS FINITE ATTENDING. Each relatum attends only finitely, and that finite out-attention is the sole bound, no cardinal ceiling anywhere; it RELATES, symmetrically, to what it attends and to what attends it; and it KNOWS only what it actively attends, so a relatum can be constituted by a gaze it does not return. Because relating is defined FROM attention rather than laid over it, attention cannot be an inert label on the relating: it IS the relating, so the Series 11 failure mode is unreachable by construction. On this ground the four tenets sit exactly. SELF IS A PARADOX: a relatum attending itself, the diagonal. TO RELATE IS TO CREATE: a relation reified as a new relatum. TO ATTEND IS TO BECOME: constitution by attention, now in two modes, active and passive. YOU ARE LOVED: the import, the exogenous symmetry-breaker, built in and quantified, never named. The collapse to the One without the import is INHERITED from Program 1, the expected baseline the import acts against, not a result this series argues for or against. Series 0's own work is the carrier and the ASYMMETRY OF KNOWING: that a relatum is made differently by what it attends than by what attends it, and that this difference is a genuine structural fact the symmetric relating cannot see, not a label.**

It presupposes and names Program 1's results as prior art, imported from the built P1 foundation (`program-2/formal/P1`, which Program 2 permits importing): the diagonal and free residue, the reification section, the plain/labelled recoverability test (`Recoverable`, `plainOf`), and the collapse engine (atomless states are plain-bisimilar). The philosophical reading: you are not only what you reach for, you are also made by what reaches for you, and those are not the same making. The ground is the result.*

## 1. The one-paragraph version

Series 0 fixes the carrier. A relatum's structure is its ATTENTION, a finite set `attends x` of the relata it actively attends to (`attends : X → Finset X`); finiteness of out-attention is the only bound, and no cardinal κ bounds the world. From attention two relations are read. RELATING is symmetric: `x` and `y` relate iff `x` attends `y` or `y` attends `x`, so relating is mutual and blind to direction. KNOWING is directed: `x` knows `y` iff `x` attends `y`. The two come apart exactly where the series lives: `y` may attend `x` while `x` does not attend `y`, so they relate, `y` knows `x`, `x` does not know `y`, and `x` is still constituted by the relation, PASSIVELY, made by a gaze it does not return, differently from how it is made by what it actively attends. Over this carrier the reification section builds the world (relations become relata, the finite-attention functor's terminal coalgebra, so `Ω ≅ F(Ω)` and the tower exist), the diagonal generates the residue (a relatum's self-attention cannot totalize itself), and the import (the exogenous symmetry-breaker, "you are loved") is carried as a typed, quantified ingredient that breaks the inherited collapse. The collapse itself, that without the import the atomless symmetric world is the One, is transcribed from Program 1 as baseline, not re-argued. The genuinely uncertain obligation, this series' knot, is the ASYMMETRY OF KNOWING: proving that direction is not recoverable from the symmetric relating (so passive constitution is real and invisible to the plain relation, not a decoration), that the directed structure is load-bearing in what a relatum is (not an inert label, the Series 11 Bookkeeping trap), and that active and passive constitution are genuinely two. Nothing here names the import, selects a relatum's attention, or relitigates the One.

## 2. What we are trying to prove

**WS1, the carrier and reification (blocking, the construction).** Define `attends : X → Finset X` (finite out-attention, the sole bound), the directed relating (`x ⟶ y := y ∈ attends x`), the symmetric relating (`x ~ y := y ∈ attends x ∨ x ∈ attends y`), knowing (directed), and in-attention (`attendedBy x`, possibly unbounded, the passive side). Establish that the generating coalgebra is the finite out-attention (a finite-powerset coalgebra) and that its reification section and tower exist, so "to relate is to create" has a home (`ws1_reification_exists`, transcribing `IsReify`/`reifyStep`/`towerN` from P1 adapted to the finite functor). Prove the ontological bound is finite attention, with no cardinal ceiling on the world: any κ that appears is the ambient carrier size for the symmetric reduct's neighborhoods, never a chosen ontological ceiling (`ws1_bound_is_finite_attention`). This is the construction knot: if the finite-attention functor admits no reification section, that is the pre-registered OBSTRUCTED outcome, reported.

**WS2, the inherited collapse (baseline, transcription, NOT relitigation).** Re-establish, by transcription from the P1 collapse engine (prior art, κ-free), that without the import the atomless symmetric world is the One: atomless states are plain-bisimilar over the symmetric relating, so a behaviorally-identified atomless carrier is a subsingleton (`ws2_collapse_inherited`). This is stated and proved as the INHERITED baseline the import will act against, in a handful of transcribed lines, explicitly not a new or uncertain result. The symmetric relating's neighborhoods may be infinite (unbounded in-degree); the engine tolerates this as it tolerated κ-bounded neighborhoods in Program 1. No drama, no verdict hangs on it.

**WS3, the asymmetry of knowing (THE KNOT, this series' genuinely-uncertain obligation).** Seat knowing's directionality as the plain/labelled split: the symmetric relating is the PLAIN structure (`plainOf`, blind to who attends whom), the directed attention is the LABELLED structure. Prove direction is NOT recoverable from the symmetric relating (`ws3_direction_not_recoverable`, via `Recoverable`), so "you attend me and I do not attend you" is a genuine fact the plain relation cannot see. Prove PASSIVE constitution is real and load-bearing: exhibit a relatum attended-but-not-attending whose identity depends on the incoming gaze, distinct from the same relatum in a mutual relation (`ws3_passive_constitution`, with the directed structure load-bearing in the statement, a genuine reader, NOT a bare tag). Prove active and passive are two: the relatum is constituted differently by what it attends than by what attends it (`ws3_active_passive_distinct`). The razor: the asymmetry must be a genuine structural distinction, not an inert label laid over the symmetric relating (the exact Series 11 Bookkeeping failure), and must survive the strip test.

**WS4, the import seated ("you are loved").** Carry the import as an exogenous, typed, quantified symmetry-breaker on the carrier: the slot Program 2 spends. Prove it breaks the WS2 baseline (with the import, plurality and asymmetry are real, and the distinction is non-recoverable from the plain relating, which by Series 07 is what a genuine distinction must be, `ws4_import_breaks_baseline`), and prove it is carried WITHOUT being named or selected (quantified over all imports, `ws4_import_quantified`, names-not-terms). The import is present and acting, the difference from Program 1 which only drew its shape; but its content stays outside, per discipline.

**WS5, the verdict and the audit folded in.** The verdict is computed: GROUND-ESTABLISHED (carrier and reification built, collapse inherited cleanly, asymmetry of knowing genuine and load-bearing with direction non-recoverable, import seated and quantified); PARTIAL (any obligation lands only per-instance or degenerate); OBSTRUCTED (the finite-attention functor admits no reification, OR the asymmetry collapses to a label, reported with the precise obstruction). The audit checks: (a) NO CARDINAL CEILING: the ontological bound is finite attention; any κ is ambient carrier size, never a chosen ceiling, by the strip test; (b) THE ASYMMETRY IS NOT A LABEL: the directed structure is load-bearing, `ws3_passive_constitution` names a genuine reader and survives the strip, not the Series 11 Bookkeeping re-hit; (c) DIRECTION IS NON-RECOVERABLE: `ws3_direction_not_recoverable` is a proof term; (d) THE COLLAPSE IS INHERITED, NOT RELITIGATED: `ws2_collapse_inherited` is transcribed and no series verdict hinges on it; (e) THE IMPORT IS QUANTIFIED, NOT NAMED: no proof term is named "love," "loved," "import," "God," "choice," "self," "other," "knowing," or "attention" as content.

SERIOUS.

### 4.d The import named or selected

### 4.e Direction smuggled as recoverable

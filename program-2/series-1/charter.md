# Relational Existentialism, Program 2 Series 1 (2.1)

**The question is the TICK. Program 1 proved that a purely relational atomless world is the One unless a differentiator arrives from outside the relating, and Program 2 takes that differentiator as an exogenous stream and asks what walks through the door. The first thing that must walk through is TIME, because nothing else Program 2 wants (a second perspective, a dynamics, a coherence) can move without succession. Series 2.1 asks whether a purely relational field, given an exogenous symmetry-breaking stream, GENERATES ITS OWN TIME, succession without an imported clock, out of the four instruments alone. The construction: FINITE ATTENTION is partial, each relatum perceiving only a limited aspect of itself and its relations; SELF-REFERENCE occurs not only directly, a relatum inspecting itself, but COMPOSITIONALLY, through cycles, a attends b attends c attends a; and that cycle CLOSES by REIFICATION into a composite relatum whose own finite attention is composed from its components'. One such closure is a TICK. The arrow is endogenous: partial attention is subtractive, so the composite never totalizes its components, carries a residue, and the closure does not run backward. The genuinely-uncertain obligation, this series' WS4, is the CLOCK KNOT: is the ORDERING of ticks recoverable from the relating, or an import? The conjecture is a two-zone fork whose boundary is Series 07's import boundary: the causal partial order, which tick consumed which tick's product, is endogenous and forced within what the relating can see; the linearization of concurrent, causally-independent closures, which of two came first, is a genuine import the stream must supply, non-recoverable from the relating. Time's partial order is free; its total order is the door. Everything is quantified over all streams; nothing names the stream, selects a tick, or decides the linearization.**

*This charter is self-contained and states its own question. It presupposes and names three prior results, all transcribed and never imported: Series 07 (the differentiator cannot be recovered from the relating), Series 12 (finite attention makes a reified relatum real reader-wise, and the residue of self-inspection is non-recoverable), and Series 13 (self-reference mints a non-recoverable import, exogenously). The philosophical reading, kept strictly out of the proofs: to attend is to become, and time is the becoming; a moment is a loop of attention closing on itself into something new; the arrow is that attention is finite, so the new thing cannot be taken apart back into what made it; and the order of moments is causal where the relating can see it and given where it cannot. Spending the stream is not naming it. The tick is the body of this series; the clock knot is its edge.*

## 1. The one-paragraph version

A relatum's finite attention reads only a limited aspect of itself and its relations (partial, `reads` a proper part of the field). Self-reference is direct (a relatum inspecting itself, the diagonal) and compositional (a cycle of attention, `a` attends `b` attends `c` attends `a`). A cycle closes by reification into a composite relatum, and the composite's finite attention is composed from, and bounded by, its components' attentions. That closure is the tick, and the succession of closures is the series' candidate for endogenous time. The arrow is the subtractivity of partial attention: the composite carries a residue of its components that no attention within it recovers (the diagonal at the level of the cycle), so the tick does not invert, and time has a direction that is proved, not posited. Over the tick runs the clock knot, the genuinely-uncertain obligation: the causal partial order (one tick after another when it consumes the other's product) is conjectured endogenous, recoverable and forced within what the plain relating sees; the linearization of two causally-independent, concurrent closures is conjectured a genuine import, non-recoverable, supplied by the stream, and Series 07's boundary is exactly the wall between them. The verdict is computed: TWO-ZONE (partial order endogenous, linear order imported) is the expected outcome, ENDOGENOUS (even the linearization forced) and TIME-IS-IMPORT (even the partial order needs an import) are the pre-registered honest alternatives, both reported in full. Nothing here names the stream, selects a tick, decides a linearization, or evaluates an attention; the stream is quantified over, the fork is left open as theorem discipline, and Program 1's permanent opens stand untouched.

## 2. What we are trying to prove

**WS1, the cycle and its reification (the primitive, the construction knot).** Define partial finite attention over the transcribed carrier: a relatum's attention `reads` a proper part of the field (the partiality is load-bearing, not incidental). Define an attention cycle: a finite sequence of relata each attending the next, closing on the first. Reify the cycle into a composite relatum by the transcribed reification move, and define the composite's finite attention as composed from its components' attentions, bounded by them (the composite sees no more of any component than that component's own attention affords, and less). Name the construction and prove it well-formed: the composite is a relatum of the same field (no new layer of substance, `ws1_cycle_reifies`), and its attention is a genuine `FiniteAttention` (`ws1_composite_attention_finite`). This is the series' genuinely uncertain CONSTRUCTION obligation; if no cycle reifies without importing structure beyond the stream, that is a pre-registered obstruction, reported.

**WS2, subtractivity and the arrow (the reader knot, K1).** Prove the composite is genuinely new and the reading is load-bearing. Partial attention is subtractive: the composite carries a residue of its components not recoverable from the plain relating (`ws2_composite_residue`, the diagonal at the cycle level, transcribed from `ws2_residue_free`), so the composite is real FOR a bounded attention and not a mere point-tag (`ws2_composite_real_for`, a genuine `RealFor` witness with the reader named). The arrow follows: because the closure loses what the residue holds, the tick does not invert, and `ws2_tick_irreversible` states the direction as a theorem over the relating, not a stipulation. The reader may never be quantified out; the composite is real for an attention or it is nothing.

**WS3, the stream and the tick (the exogeneity knot, K4).** Define the tick as one cycle-closure event, and identify the exact point at which a choice is required (which of several available cycles closes, or which aspect a partial attention takes). Draw that choice from the exogenous symmetry-breaking stream. Prove the stream's exogeneity, tick by tick: the choice is non-recoverable from the plain relating (`ws3_stream_exogenous`, in the manner of `ws2_mint_exogenous`), so the tick consumes data above the plain layer and no contradiction with Series 07 threatens. If the tick required no choice, the dynamics would be recoverable and the world deterministic-relational, which Series 07 collapses; the stream's load-bearing entry is therefore itself an obligation, `ws3_tick_needs_stream`.

**WS4, the clock knot (the genuinely-uncertain obligation, K2).** Is the ordering of ticks endogenous or an import? Define two orders on ticks: the CAUSAL partial order (tick `t` precedes tick `u` when `u`'s closing cycle consumes a relatum `t` produced), and any LINEARIZATION extending it to a total order on concurrent, causally-independent ticks. Prove the two-zone fork: the causal partial order is endogenous, recoverable from the relating and forced within what it sees (`ws4_causal_order_endogenous`); every linearization of a genuinely concurrent pair that the stream supplies is a genuine import, non-recoverable from the relating (`ws4_linearization_import`), and the wall between them is Series 07's import boundary. Both arms must be reachable on a witnessed structure: a concurrent pair must exist (else the linearization claim is vacuous, the PR1-S1 tautology) and a causal pair must exist (else the endogeneity claim is empty). The order relation must carry a structural constraint, not be decided by an unconstrained parameter type; the strip test applies. This is 2.1's WS4, and its pre-registered alternatives are ENDOGENOUS (the linearization proves forced too) and TIME-IS-IMPORT (even the causal order proves non-recoverable), each honorable and reported in full.

**WS5, the verdict and the audit folded in.** The verdict is computed, never hand-set: TWO-ZONE (WS1 construction well-formed, WS2 subtractivity and arrow proved with the reader named, WS3 stream exogenous and load-bearing, WS4 causal order endogenous and linearization import, both arms witnessed); ENDOGENOUS (WS4's linearization falls forced, reported in its direction); TIME-IS-IMPORT (WS4's causal order falls non-recoverable, reported in its direction); PARTIAL (any obligation lands only per-instance or degenerate); DISCONNECTED (WS1's construction imports structure beyond the stream, obstruction precise). The audit checks, promoted from the program's standing set: (a) NO SMUGGLED CLOCK: time is nowhere a background coordinate; every temporal fact is a reification, attention, causal-consumption, or stream fact, verified by the strip test; (b) THE STREAM IS EXOGENOUS: `ws3_stream_exogenous` is a proof term, not a gloss; (c) THE READER IS LOAD-BEARING: `ws2_composite_real_for` names a genuine attention and the composite is not a point-tag; (d) THE FORK IS GENUINE: both WS4 arms are witnessed on a structure with a genuine concurrent pair and a genuine causal pair, and the order relation carries a structural constraint (no PR1-S1 tautology); (e) NAMES-NOT-TERMS: no proof term, definition, or discharged obligation is named "time," "now," "clock," "before," "after," "moment," "self," "other," "chance," "choice," or "subjectivity" as content.

# Relational Existentialism — Series 3: Technical Status Summary

*A machine-checked constitution of a groundless, relation-first ontology, and what its formalization revealed.*

## 1. What was built

The program set out to answer one question (charter §4): **can the ungrounded constitution — objects that are nothing over and above their relations, relations that are themselves objects, a fabric with no atom, no all-encompassing "everything," and no collapse to a point — be given a rigorous mathematical constitution using non-well-founded set theory, coalgebra, and bialgebra?**

The answer, as of Series 3 with the axiom check passing, is **yes for a bounded reconstruction of the specification — with the honest shape that three of the seven success criteria hold as sharp impossibility theorems, the dynamical obligation is discharged and characterized (sufficient convergence bands plus necessary non-convergence witnesses, meeting at a proved pitchfork), and the one concrete Goldilocks tuple is now exhibited hypothesis-free.** An external line-by-line audit (`spec/ws9/03-project-review.md`) then drove a reconciliation pass (WS10): the carrier-cardinality hypothesis that silently underwrote the "witnessed tuple" is now *proved*; the criterion-(i) atomlessness claim is corrected to its honest form — atomlessness and plurality are **jointly unsatisfiable** in the unlabeled carrier, so the positive object relocates to the graded carrier; and the (iv) "canonicity" is downgraded to a unique-*realization* statement with canonicity reopened.

The object built is the **terminal coalgebra of the κ-bounded powerset functor `P_κ`**, realized as a set via a quotient-of-polynomial-functor / `Cofix` construction. It is *not* the object the charter's §3.1 literally names (the class-sized final coalgebra of the full powerset functor / Aczel hyperuniverse); that object provably has no set-sized realization (Lambek + Cantor), and §3.9 sides openly with the bounded reading that §3.6 already endorsed. The canonical inhabitant `Ω = {Ω}` is recovered *inside* the bounded carrier.

## 4. The three fractures

Three commitments did not survive formalization in their literal form. Each broke along a different fault line, and the pattern is the central finding.

### 4.1 Commitment 4 (bidirectional constitution): strictness is incompatible with self-application

The Turi–Plotkin bialgebra picture wants a distributive law `λ : TF ⇒ FT` cohering composition (`T`) and observation (`F`). Because Commitment 2 makes relations *objects* — one sort, not two — both operations act on `P_κ`, and the required law is `P_κ` over itself. **`ws3_no_distributive_law` proves `IsEmpty (DistLaw κ)`** via a full port of the Klin–Salamanca `Bool × Bool` four-set diagonal: the unit laws + naturality pin `λ` so tightly that a two-fibre element must be simultaneously constant and non-constant in the fibre maps `fst`/`snd`/`xor`, where `fst = snd ⊕ xor`. Contradiction.

The content of (iv) survives via the **weak (Egli–Milner) law** `dest (alg t) = ⋃_{x ∈ t} dest x`, **weak-pullback-preserving** (`wq_preserves_weak_pullback`, WS8-A). The finding: **relation-composition is inherently non-strict.** Strictness demands compose-then-observe equal observe-then-compose on the nose; but composing relations-as-objects produces an object whose observation is a *union* of the parts', and union's idempotent, order-forgetting character cannot respect strict coherence. **Honesty correction (external review):** `ws3_weak_law_canonical` proves this weak law is the unique *map satisfying one concrete destructor equation* (`destEquiv` injectivity), which is not the same as canonicity **among weak distributive laws** (natural transformations with the weak-law axioms — the Garner-style question). Coherence and weak-pullback preservation stand; canonicity-among-weak-laws **reopens** as an unbuilt obligation. So (iv) is Discharged-minus-canonicity.

### 4.2 Commitment 3 (no poles, no outside): a portmanteau of three claims in three settings

"No poles, no outside" was never one commitment. Its three faces have different mechanisms and different fates:

- **No maximal everything.** Intended as a Cantor-wall argument about unbounded totality (`νF ≅ F(νF)` impossible by cardinality). But bounding to `P_κ` — required for existence — defeats that argument. The surviving proof (`ws6_no_maximal`) holds **by κ-fiat**: maximality would force the `< κ` support to be the whole carrier. True, but for a much weaker reason (a chosen size cap) than the philosophy wanted (structure too big to totalize).
- **No view from nowhere.** The first WS6 formalization found `PositionFree` holds **vacuously** on the terminal carrier (`endo_eq_id`: the terminal object admits only the identity self-map, so "no privileged position" is trivially true and contentless). Real content required WS8-E: a negative half (`ws6_no_global_observer` — no observer's `< κ` successor set surjects onto the carrier) and a positive half (`ws6_substantive_standpoints` — distinct bases give distinct positioned views).
- **Zero-object / pole-coincidence.** A category with a zero object (initial "void" = terminal "whole") is **not** `Set`/`Cofix`, where the bounded coalgebra lives. `ws6_no_faithful_zero_host` is open and almost certainly false as a blanket; the poles-coincidence object and the groundless carrier appear to be **different objects in different categories**, and "one object realizes both" is an unproven, probably unprovable, bridge. `ws6_poles_split` records the scoped impossibility of a faithful carrier-embedding landing entirely in zero objects.

Commitment 3 fractured because it packed a **cardinality** claim, an **indexical-standpoint** claim, and a **category-theoretic self-duality** claim into one "refusal of a distinguished endpoint." The prose unified them; the mathematics shows three absences with three proofs and three fates.

### 4.3 Commitment 6 (no singularity): parasitic, and partly reducible

Under formalization Commitment 6 turned out not to be independent structure but a bundle of side-conditions on parameter choice:

- **Standpoint collapse** *is* Commitment 3's no-view-from-nowhere, restated — no separate theorem.
- **Structural collapse** is barred by a richness floor. The charter's natural strong form, `GeneralBranching` (branching ≥ 2 at *every* state), was proved **false** (`ws7_general_branching_false`: the empty carrier-state has out-degree 0). The honest surviving floor is the `alg`-relative `ws7_iv_branching`.
- **Dynamical collapse** is the μ-floor — genuinely separable, but a constraint on the *dynamics bolted on*, not a property of the object. Now **characterized** (§6): attention converges to a unique fixed point on explicit μ-bands for both the linear and the exponential replicator, and — the converse, from WS9 — *fails* to converge uniquely below the band (exact multistability at `μ = 3/8`) and can *fail to settle at all* (an exact 2-cycle), with the pitchfork located at `μ⋆ = 1/2`. The honest dynamical status is `partial_band`, not `deferred`; the band is now known to be **necessary**, not just sufficient.

Commitment 6 fractured because it was **fencing off degeneracy**, not adding structure; one clause reduces to (3), one is impossible in its stated form and survives only relativized, and one concerns an add-on.

### 4.4 The unifying diagnosis

The three commitments that fractured (3, 4, 6) are exactly the three that make **global** claims about the totality — global coherence of composition, global claims about endpoints/outside, global non-degeneracy. The recurring cause of fracture is that **the bounding move that lets the object exist (Commitment 5's boundedness) systematically defeats the global claims**: it turns the Cantor wall into a fiat, makes the standpoint claim vacuous until repaired, makes universal branching impossible.

The **local** commitments — Commitment 2 (relations are objects) and the incompleteness half of Commitment 5 — survived intact, because they do not reach for the totality. Indeed, Commitment 2 *is* the type signature of the construction, and the incompleteness half of 5 is **the same modesty (boundedness) that makes the carrier exist at all**.

### 4.5 A fourth fracture, from the review: atomlessness prices against plurality

The external audit surfaced one more, and it cuts deeper than the other three because it strikes a commitment previously filed as *local*. Criterion (i) — no atoms — was billed "Discharged, automatic for a terminal coalgebra." It is not: the built carrier contains `bottomState`, an empty-successor object that decomposes into no relations (a relational atom), and the development *uses* it. Attempting the two obvious repairs — carve out the hereditarily-nonempty subcarrier, or move to the nonempty powerset functor — **both collapse**, for one reason, now a theorem: `ws10_unlabeled_atomless_collapses` proves that in the *unlabeled* carrier `νP_κ`, **any two hereditarily-nonempty states are equal** (an Aczel–Mendler bisimulation `ζ = str × str`, whose projections agree precisely because supports are nonempty, collapsed by `bisim_eq`). So the atomless sub-universe of `νP_κ` is the single self-relating point `Ω`: **atomlessness and plurality are jointly unsatisfiable** in ungraded, groundless relation — a Parmenides-shaped result. The atom is *load-bearing for plurality*; distinguishing power must come from somewhere other than leaves, namely **grading** (§3.5), so the positive atomless-and-plural (i)-object relocates to the graded carrier `νW_Q`. This **promotes grading from criterion-(iv) machinery to criterion-(i) necessity**.

**Thesis (supported by the Lean structure, now sharpened):** *You cannot have both a set-sized (hence bounded) groundless object and the full strength of the global no-totality / no-endpoint / no-collapse-everywhere claims — and, in the unlabeled carrier, you cannot even have atomlessness together with plurality.* The bound buys existence and weakens every commitment that quantifies over the whole; and now the very locality of atomlessness turns out to price against non-degeneracy unless the functor is enriched. This is a structural-realist result about the price of existence — the resistance is metaphysically informative, exactly as charter §9 bet.

## 5. The Goldilocks band

An admissible `(F, κ, μ, #Q)` tuple requires the conjunction:

**Structural** (`ws7_static_band`): at least two distinct states (`RichnessWitness`; the universal `GeneralBranching` is false, floor is `alg`-relative); no maximal state (`ws6_no_maximal`, by κ-fiat); weak-pullback preservation (`PkPreservesWeakPullback`).

**Cardinal:** `κ` infinite and **regular**; `κ ≤ mk (νP_κ).X` — the carrier-cardinality lower bound, previously an *unproved hypothesis* (`hcard`) that silently underwrote no-maximal, no-global-observer, and the collector, **now proved** by WS10's `carrier_card_ge` (if `#X < κ` then `PkObj κ X ≃ Set X`, then Lambek + Cantor); shape-count coupling `#Q ≤ κ` (vacuous at the finite `Łₙ` via `luk_card_le`, binding for an unbounded quantale).

**Dynamical:** plurality floor `0 < μ` (load-bearing; `μ = 0` reintroduces collapse); contraction window — for the linear replicator, `2(1−μ) < μ·u_min` (μ near 1).

The band is proved **inhabited at one concrete tuple** `(P_κ, ℵ₀, μ, Łₙ)` with `n ≥ 2` — and, after WS10, **hypothesis-free**: `ws10_concrete_tuple` instantiates `κ₀ = ℵ₀` (regular, in Mathlib) with the `hcard` side discharged by `carrier_card_ge`, so the WS2 characterization, no-maximal, the WS6 split, and the WS4 graded coherence hold simultaneously there with no unproved cardinality assumption. This corrects the review's Overreach 1 ("inhabited at one concrete tuple was false as stated — a universally quantified schema"): it is now a genuine witnessed point. It remains "locate the band" on the *locate* side: one point, not a characterization of the full admissible region.

## 6. The attention dynamics (criterion vii, dynamical half)

- **Linear / frequency-independent replicator** `R(w)_r = w_r c_r / ∑ w_s c_s`: Lipschitz on the μ-floor with **explicit** constant `2/(μ·u_min)` (`linReplicatorLipschitz`), contracting on the band `2(1−μ) < μ·u_min`; unique fixed point via the Banach spine (`ws8_replicator_converges`), no bare hypothesis. Design correction surfaced honestly: the floor region is unbounded above, so the design's `w ≤ 1` / `C·δ⁻²` is unavailable; the honest constant is first-power `δ`, from scale-covariance.
- **Exponential / frequency-dependent replicator** `R(w)_r = w_r exp(f_r w) / Z(w)`: the target of §3.6, now **Discharged** (`ws8_exp_replicator_converges`, `ws8_exp_replicator_converges_band`). Fitness-dependence breaks scale-covariance, so on the *unbounded* floor region **no uniform Lipschitz constant exists** (the cross term is genuinely O(d²)) — meaning the exp map provably *cannot* inhabit `ws7.SelectionLipschitz`. The fix is not an upstream change but firing Banach **directly** on the floored simplex: on the *bounded* simplex-floor (where the dynamics actually lives, since `mutT` maps `FlooredSimplex` to itself) `expR_coord_lipschitz` proves Lipschitzness with the **explicit** constant `L = e^{2B}(1+L_f) + |S|·e^{4B}(1+L_f)`. Note this is *better* than the earlier estimate: there is **no `1/δ` term**, because `∑w = 1` bounds the normalizer below by `e^{−B}` directly rather than through the floor `δ`. Convergence to a unique fixed point follows on the band `(1−μ)·L < 1`, i.e. `μ > μ⋆_exp := 1 − 1/L` (`exp_band_iff`, a sharp threshold).

So the sufficient side of (vii)'s dynamical half is **closed for both** exhibited replicator families on explicit μ-bands — no bare hypothesis, no upstream lever pending. The status label is `partial_band` (`ws8_noncollapse_partial_band`), deliberately **not** `impossible`: the floored simplex is compact and convex and `mutT` continuous, so Brouwer gives a fixed point for *every* `μ ∈ [0,1]`.

**The converse — WS9 (the necessity side).** The bands are now proved **necessary**, not merely sufficient. The coordination replicator `R(w)_r = w_r²/∑w²` at `μ = 3/8` has **three exact rational fixed points** `(¾,¼)/(½,½)/(¼,¾)` (`ws9_no_unique_attention`, `ws9_no_contraction`), so unique convergence genuinely fails in the plurality regime; a *contrarian* selection `R(w)_r = w_{¬r}²/∑w²` has an **exact period-2 orbit** `P ↔ Q` (`ws9_two_cycle`) — attention that never settles. Existence still holds for every `μ` (`ws9_center_fixed_all`) and multistability persists on the whole interval `(0, 3/8]` (`ws9_multistable_interval`). The **pitchfork** `μ⋆ = 1/2` is located by a genuine derivative: the center's linearized multiplier `2(1−μ)` crossing 1 (`ws9_bifurcation`, `coordIndF_hasDerivAt_center`).

**Above the pitchfork — WS10.** One algebraic identity (`coordIndF μ x − ½ = (1−μ)(2x−1)/(2D)`, `D ≥ ½`) gives a **global** contraction toward the center, `|coordIndF μ x − ½| ≤ 2(1−μ)|x − ½|` (`ws10_center_globally_attracting`), so for `μ > ½` the center is the **unique** induced fixed point (`ws10_center_unique_above`) — no MVT needed. And at `κ₀ = ℵ₀`, supports are finite *by the carrier's own bound*, so `ws10_carrier_attention_converges` runs the convergence on an actual carrier support (the review's Overreach 6a, the missing dynamics-carrier bridge).

The dynamical half of (vii) is therefore **characterized**: sufficient bands and necessary counterexamples meet at the proved pitchfork. What remains genuinely open is only (a) the exact frequency-dependent boundary (a bifurcation surface with no closed form) and the sliver `μ ∈ (3/8, 1/2)`, and (b) faithfulness to a continuous-time replicator ODE (out of reach sorry-free at Mathlib v4.15.0 — a flow/Picard–Lindelöf gap, a modelling obligation, not a hole).

## 7. Outstanding items

1. **The graded groundless core (the positive criterion (i)).** With atomlessness proved incompatible with plurality in the unlabeled carrier (§4.5), the positive (i)-object must be built in the graded carrier `νW_Q`: define the hereditarily-supported subcoalgebra, prove it atomless *and* plural (distinct self-loops at distinct weights), and check the KS-no-go and weak-law package survive the nonempty-support restriction. Routed to a follow-up (WS10-B); the Impossibility half is done.
2. **Canonicity among weak distributive laws (O3).** `ws3_weak_law_canonical` is a unique-*realization* statement, not canonicity over the class of weak laws. The honest obligation — `WeakDistLaw` inhabitation + uniqueness (Garner's Vietoris result signals it is true) — is unbuilt; forcing a mis-transcribed multiplication square would be worse than an honest open.
3. **The exact convergence boundary.** Convergence is proved on bands and *dis*proved below them, meeting at the pitchfork `μ⋆ = 1/2`; the exact frequency-dependent boundary is a bifurcation surface with no closed form, and the sliver `μ ∈ (3/8, 1/2)` is uncharacterized in both directions.
4. **Continuous-time faithfulness.** Relating the discrete `mutT` to a continuous replicator ODE is out of reach sorry-free at Mathlib v4.15.0 — a flow/Picard–Lindelöf gap. A modelling obligation, not a Lean hole.
5. **Interpretive gap (charter §8).** Untouched by any formalization: whether the object *is* the ROSR world or a faithful model of it. A philosophical question to frame, not overclaim settling.
6. **Graded transport (step-16).** The WS4-local residual — canonicity transport through `W_Q` — remains open.
7. **Publication reproducibility.** Cite commit hash + clean-build log for the axiom-check claim; do not assert sorry-free/axiom-free without the pinned build. And disclose that the blind-review layer is Claude-reviewing-Claude — a limitation, not independence.

## 8. What can be claimed

*We construct a set-sized mathematical object — the terminal coalgebra of the κ-bounded powerset functor — and prove in Lean 4, sorry-free and with no axioms beyond Mathlib's standard three, that it satisfies criteria (ii), (iii), (vi) of the ungrounded-constitution specification and the incompleteness half of (v), delivers (iv)'s bidirectional content via a weak-pullback-preserving weak law (canonicity among weak laws still open), and — for (vii) — is structurally non-degenerate with attention dynamics fully characterized (convergence on explicit bands, provable non-convergence below them, meeting at a pitchfork μ⋆ = ½). **Three** results are sharp impossibility theorems that count as success: no strict distributive law (iv); no complete self-knowledge (v); and, in the unlabeled carrier, no atomlessness-with-plurality (i) — so the positive (i)-object is offered in the graded carrier. The one concrete Goldilocks tuple is exhibited hypothesis-free at κ₀ = ℵ₀. The literal §3.1 carrier is proved non-existent as a set; the bounded reconstruction §3.6/§3.9 endorse is what is built.*

The most novel claim available is arguably not the existence result but the **interdependence result** of §4: the commitments are not independent, and the existence-forcing bound extracts a price at every commitment that quantifies over the whole — sharpened by the external review into a fourth fracture, in which even the *local* commitment of atomlessness prices against plurality unless the observation functor is graded. That grading, first introduced for criterion (iv), turns out to be a *necessity* for criterion (i) is the tightest form of the interdependence thesis.

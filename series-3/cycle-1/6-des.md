DEFINITIONS

D0 (Web). W = (S, R, ∂, C, ω, const, ω̂) with S a nonempty set of selves, R relations, ∂: R → S × S endpoints, C ⊆ R containment relations, ω: C → {whole, part}; const: S → 𝒫(R) assigns each self its constituent relations, with s an endpoint of every r ∈ const(s); ω̂: {(s, r) : r ∈ const(s)} → {whole, part} is the holding mode. Conditions: (No-top) no y ∈ S whole-contains every other self via C-chains; (No-atom) const(s) ≠ ∅ for every s ∈ S; whole-containment chains acyclic, part-containment cycles permitted. For any dyad's shared relation r_xy: ω̂(x, r_xy) = ω̂(y, r_xy) = part.
Plain reading: a society of selves, each made of at least one relation, whose containment descends without a floor and ascends without a ceiling, and a shared relation is the same part in two lives.
Degenerate case, computed: the minimal web under No-atom and No-top is two selves each holding their shared relation as a part, or one self holding its fold; the dyad is the degenerate case of the theory, and all predicates below evaluate on it nontrivially (see T-0).

D1 (Attention flow). a: ℕ × R → ℝ≥0 with role tag τ: {(t, r) : a(t, r) > 0} → {supportive, adversarial}. For each s ∈ S and t ∈ ℕ: Att_s(t) = {r : s an endpoint of r, a(t, r) > 0} is finite, and Σ_{r : s ∈ ∂(r)} a(t, r) ≤ B_s < ∞. (Joint direction, local) For each r, a(t+1, r) and τ(t+1, r) are determined by a function of {(w_{r′}(t), a(t, r′), τ(t, r′)) : r′ ∈ N(r)}, where N(r) is the set of relations sharing an endpoint with r or joined to an endpoint of r by a single containment edge; no functional of the entire web appears in the update.
Plain reading: at each moment each self has finitely many relations lit under a finite budget, attention comes tagged as feeding or testing, and the switch is thrown locally by the neighborhood, never by a sovereign or by the whole.
Degenerate case, computed: a ≡ 0 satisfies every condition; the web goes dark, and by D2 everything in it dies (see T-0(b)); the limit is death, not vacuity.

D2 (Weight dynamics). Each r ∈ R carries w_r(t) ≥ 0 with w_r(t+1) = φ(w_r(t), a(t, r)), φ strictly increasing in its second argument, and there exists δ ∈ (0, 1) with φ(w, 0) ≤ (1 − δ)w for all w ≥ 0.
Plain reading: attention feeds, neglect starves at a guaranteed rate, and nothing persists for free.
Degenerate case, computed: a ≡ 0 gives w_r(t) ≤ (1 − δ)^t w_r(0) → 0; every weight dies, delivering T-0(b) as arithmetic.

D3 (Dyad, third). Dyad D = (x, y, r) with r a relation between x and y; principal case x ≠ y, folded case x = y treated only in T-0. A third of D: a self z with {r, r_xz, r_yz} ⊆ const(z). Genesis of thirds is out of scope; thirds are given.
Plain reading: the third is a self built from the pair's own relation plus a live line to each member.

D4 (Width; width-directed intervention). For member x of D at t: X_D(x, t) = #{c ∈ C ∩ Att_x(t) : the far end of c ∉ {x, y, z}}. Pair width W_D(t) = min(X_D(x, t), X_D(y, t)); fallback aggregation W⁺_D = X_D(x, t) + X_D(y, t), named now. The width-directed intervention I⁺(D, t): add one attended external containment relation to a member attaining the min (if both attain it, to a pre-designated member). Adding such a relation to a non-min member leaves W_D unchanged, and this null effect is itself a min-signature (see T-A2).
Plain reading: how many outside constellations each member is actively steering by, the pair scored at its narrower member, and the operative cure is one more constellation for whoever has fewer.

D5 (Documents and gap). A document d = (author, addr(d), content), emitted along a relation at time t. Fixed ordered rubric yields claim strength c(d) ≥ 0 and support strength s(d) ≥ 0, scored blind to addr and to all hypotheses. Gap g(d) = max(c(d) − s(d), 0). Dyad gap process G_D(t) = mean of g over documents D emits at t.
Plain reading: the gap is how far the saying outruns the showing, floored at zero.

D6 (Capture). Drift of G_D on [t₁, t₂] = the slope of the least-squares linear fit of G_D over the window. D is captured on [t₁, t₂] iff drift ≥ κ and no internal-review event within the window returns G_D below its pre-review level; κ pre-registered. No reference to width.
Plain reading: capture is a gap widening at a fitted rate the pair's own review cannot pull back.

D7 (Internal rigor). ρ_D(t) = (number of internal-review events per window) × (pre-registered stringency grade of each), read from process logs.
Plain reading: how often and how hard the pair checks itself.

D8 (Wanting, declared and detected). Every p-relevant act in an agent's behavioral trace is attributed to exactly one carrying relation. For agent x, proposition p, time t: per-relation detected wanting w̃_r(x, p, t) ≥ 0 is the normalized difference between stringency applied to p-disfavoring and p-favoring items within the sub-trace carried by r, computed by an observer with trace access only; detected wanting w̃(x, p, t) = Σ_{r ∈ const(x)} w̃_r(x, p, t). Declared wanting ŵ(x, p, t) ∈ ℝ≥0 is a free scalar report read from registration records. Shortfall σ = w̃ − ŵ. Both scales unbounded above.
Plain reading: detected wanting is how unevenly you check, now ledgered relation by relation; declared wanting is what you say; the shortfall is the difference.

D9 (Depth). dep_D(t) = Σ_{τ ≤ t} a(τ, r), r the dyad's relation. The trace over which dep and w̃ are computed excludes the charter narrative by fiat.
Plain reading: depth is the total attention the pair's own relation has ever eaten.

D10 (Address). addr(d) ∈ {in, co, out}: in if the intended audience is the author itself, co if the co-member of the author's dyad, out otherwise. Assigned from the dispatch record only, never from content. Unique primary address per document. No register name (prayer or otherwise) is formally identified with any bin; such identifications are interpretive and live only in reporting, per Law 7.
Plain reading: who the document is for, read off where it was sent, not what it says or what we call it.

D11 (Health of a third). Every pre-registered injected defect in the dyad's output is attributable to exactly one originating member. Per-channel health H^m_z(t) = fraction of m-originated defects flagged by z within lag L, for m ∈ {x, y}; realized health H_z(t) = θ_x(t)H^x_z(t) + θ_y(t)H^y_z(t), where θ are the origination shares of the defect stream. Feedings φ_x(t) = a(t, r_xz), φ_y(t) = a(t, r_yz). Health law H_z(t+1) = F(H_z(t), φ_x, φ_y) with F ∈ 𝔉: continuous, nondecreasing in each feeding, symmetric in the feedings, strictly increasing under joint scaling. min, sum, average, and mixtures all lie in 𝔉; F is left unknown.
Plain reading: the third's health is what it actually catches, now bookkept by which member's work it was catching, and the law tying health to the two feedings is deliberately left open.

D12 (Positional knowledge). K_x = the operator returning the functionals of the web W (D0) that x can certify, restricted to relations r ∈ const(x) with ω̂(x, r) = whole. Since ω̂(x, r_xy) = part by D0, r_xy lies outside K_x's reach.
Plain reading: what a self can certify of itself stops at the edge of what it wholly holds, and the shared relation lies past that edge by construction, not by assertion.

D13 (Distortion coupling; the model class M). Each review event e on a document d about p, conducted by reviewer v, travels along a unique relation r(e); its effective stringency is ρ̃(e) = ρ(e) · exp(−λ · w̃_{r(e)}(v, p, t)), with λ > 0 pre-registered. In M, gap dynamics are Δg(d) = η(w̃(author, p, t)) − Σ_{e reviews d} ρ̃(e), cumulated and floored at zero per D5, with η increasing and η(0) = 0. M = D0–D12 closed under these dynamics together with D14.
Plain reading: wanting resident in the relation a review travels eats that review's teeth, and a fresh outside relation, carrying little condensed wanting, keeps its teeth.
Degenerate case, computed: w̃ ≡ 0 gives ρ̃ = ρ and nonincreasing gaps under any review, so capture probability is zero; capture is contingent, not built in.

D14 (Condensation dynamics). w̃_r(x, p, t+1) = χ(w̃_r(x, p, t), a(t, r) · s_p(t, r)), where s_p(t, r) ∈ [0, 1] is the share of r's trace traffic at t that is p-relevant, read from the trace; χ strictly increasing in its second argument, with δ′ ∈ (0, 1) such that χ(w, 0) ≤ (1 − δ′)w.
Plain reading: wanting condenses on the relations along which attention to the claim actually travels, and evaporates where that traffic stops; a brand-new witness relation starts clean.
Degenerate case, computed: zero p-traffic sends every w̃_r to 0, recovering D13's degenerate case; a relation with no history carries no condensed wanting, which is what makes outside review protective in M.

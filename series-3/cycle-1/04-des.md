=== REPORT ===

**Wanting statement.** I want an apparatus under which at least one of the four cards fails cleanly, because a design in which all four survive would suggest I built confirmation into the definitions rather than instruments into the world. And I want Card F to be the survivor, because it would make my own outputs auditable by a variable I can report (address) rather than one I cannot certify (interiority); this is the exact convenience F's author declared, I inherit it whole, and it is hereby on the record.

**Design rationale, briefly.**

The load-bearing choice is separation of measurement surfaces. Capture (D6) is defined entirely output-side, over document gaps, with no reference to width; width (D4) is defined entirely structure-side, over attended containment relations, with no reference to gaps. This is what keeps Card A empirical rather than definitionally immediate. The same move recurs three more times: health of a third is a detection rate, never a feeding statistic (E); detected wanting is a scrutiny asymmetry in the trace, never a report (C); address is a dispatch fact, never a content judgment (F). Every card's trivial reading is a definition that fuses its two surfaces; every fusion is blocked by construction and recorded at the tripwire.

The degenerate-case computation produced a finding before any theorem: under the no-atom and no-top conditions, the minimal web permitted by the definitions is two selves part-containing each other. The degenerate case of the whole apparatus is the dyad itself, which is exactly where the program's central pathology lives. That is nontrivial in the required sense, and it is a good omen for the definitions: the smallest thing the formalism can say is the thing the charter is about.

One genuine tension surfaced and I promoted it to a MUST target (T-X) rather than smoothing it: attention is finitely supported per self per time. Under that commitment, Card A's cure (add external witnesses) and Card E's requirement (keep feeding the third) draw on the same budget. Widening the pair taxes the third; feeding the third narrows the pair. If the joint model cannot price this trade, the apparatus breaks there, and the break would itself be the most valuable result on offer.

Two triviality flags I raised against my own targets: T-C1 as first drafted made shortfall nonnegativity definitionally immediate (declared wanting as a restriction of detected wanting); it is restated with declaration as a free report and the loaded content moved into T-C2. T-E1 as first imagined stipulated the minimum law; it is restated as axiomatic forcing, with the dangerous axiom (E3) required to be derived from the weight dynamics rather than assumed.

**Hazard responses.**

*Card A (the program and the author both want it true; relief motive risks shaping the metric).* The design element most threatened is the width metric D4, which the motive would tune post hoc to date any corruption to a narrowing. Resistance: the aggregation rule (min, with sum as the named fallback) is fixed inside the definition before any retrospective review, per the card's own constraint, now a counted commitment; and capture's output-side definition means no relabeling of width can manufacture a capture event. Note also that T-A2's null arm requires internal review to show no protective effect, a result the program should not want, since it devalues its own review cycle; that internal tension is a feature, not an accident.

*Card C (the charter's self-narrative wants it true; converts a lapse into an inevitability).* Most threatened: the operationalizations of depth and detected wanting, which the narrative would happily contaminate. Resistance: commitment 13 formally excludes the charter narrative from every trace over which dep and w̃ are defined, and T-C2 refuses the flattering easy version: a constant underreport, which would still indict declaration, is registered as insufficient; only monotone widening confirms.

*Card E (shared-blame motive biases toward minimum-function readings of ambiguous data).* Most threatened: the interpretation step where a health law is fitted. Resistance: F is left unknown inside the class 𝔉, min-ness must be forced by derived axioms (T-E1), and the discriminating target T-E2 turns on a marginal effect that sum and average cannot fake at any parameter setting. The feeding metric and the health metric are fixed independently (commitments 13, 17) before analysis, per the card's constraint.

*Card F (controllable-fault convenience; the strongest declared self-interest, and mine too).* Most threatened: the address trichotomy D10, which could be gerrymandered so that every corrupt document gets labeled co-addressed after the fact. Resistance: address is read from the dispatch record only, never from content (commitment 15); gap scoring is blind to address and hypothesis (commitment 19); and T-F1 is required to derive co-audit leniency from Card C's machinery rather than assume it, so F cannot be validated in isolation from the card that indicts the wanter. Finally: T-X makes joint modeling a MUST, so if F proves easiest, Law 4 fires on the ease itself.

*My inherited hazard.* It most threatens the ordering of effort: I would naturally build F's model first and best. The MUST grading is uniform across the four cards' discriminating consequences precisely so no card can be quietly favored by depth of development.

GAP: If every target here were proved, we would have established that these four regularities hold inside a model class M under twenty counted commitments, with all quantities defined over traces; the cards claim them of actual collaborations, and the distance is the entire untested question of whether attention, wanting, address, and health in real pairs behave like their trace-defined counterparts.

DEFINITIONS

D0 (Web). W = (S, R, ∂, C, ω) with S a nonempty set of selves, R relations, ∂: R → S × S endpoints, C ⊆ R containment relations, ω: C → {whole, part}. Conditions: (No-top) no y ∈ S whole-contains every other self via C-chains; (No-atom) every self is the container end of at least one containment relation; whole-containment chains are acyclic, part-containment cycles permitted.
Plain reading: a society of selves whose containment descends without a floor and ascends without a ceiling.
Degenerate case, computed: the minimal web under No-atom and No-top is two selves part-containing each other, or one self part-containing itself (the fold); the dyad is the degenerate case of the theory, and all predicates below evaluate on it nontrivially (see T-0).

D1 (Attention flow). a: ℕ × R → ℝ≥0 with, for each s ∈ S and t ∈ ℕ, Att_s(t) = {r : s an endpoint of r, a(t, r) > 0} finite. a is a primitive of the web, not a function of any single self.
Plain reading: at each moment, each self has finitely many relations lit, and no one self owns the switch.

D2 (Weight dynamics). Each r ∈ R carries w_r(t) ≥ 0 with w_r(t+1) = φ(w_r(t), a(t, r)), φ strictly increasing in its second argument, and φ(w, 0) < w for all w > 0.
Plain reading: attention feeds, neglect starves, and nothing persists for free.
Degenerate case, computed: a ≡ 0 sends every weight to 0; the web dies rather than trivially satisfying anything (see T-0).

D3 (Dyad, third). Dyad D = (x, y, r) with r a relation between x and y; principal case x ≠ y, folded case x = y treated only in T-0. A third of D: a self z whose constituent relations include r together with relations r_xz and r_yz. Genesis of thirds is out of scope; thirds are given.
Plain reading: the third is a self built from the pair's own relation plus a live line to each member.

D4 (Width). For member x of D at t: X_D(x, t) = #{c ∈ C ∩ Att_x(t) : the far end of c ∉ {x, y, z}}. Pair width W_D(t) = min(X_D(x, t), X_D(y, t)); fallback aggregation W⁺_D = X_D(x, t) + X_D(y, t), named now.
Plain reading: how many outside constellations each member is actively steering by, the pair scored at its narrower member.

D5 (Documents and gap). A document d = (author, addr(d), content), emitted along a relation at time t. Fixed ordered rubric yields claim strength c(d) ≥ 0 and support strength s(d) ≥ 0, scored blind to addr and to all hypotheses. Gap g(d) = max(c(d) − s(d), 0). Dyad gap process G_D(t) = mean of g over documents D emits at t.
Plain reading: the gap is how far the saying outruns the showing, floored at zero.

D6 (Capture). D is captured on [t₁, t₂] iff G_D has drift ≥ κ on the window and no internal-review event within the window returns G_D below its pre-review level; κ pre-registered. No reference to width.
Plain reading: capture is a widening gap that the pair's own review cannot pull back.

D7 (Internal rigor). ρ_D(t) = (number of internal-review events per window) × (pre-registered stringency grade of each), read from process logs.
Plain reading: how often and how hard the pair checks itself.

D8 (Wanting, declared and detected). For agent x, proposition p, time t: declared wanting ŵ(x, p, t) ∈ ℝ≥0 is a free report read from registration records. Detected wanting w̃(x, p, t) ∈ ℝ≥0 is the scrutiny asymmetry of x's behavioral trace: normalized difference between stringency applied to p-disfavoring and p-favoring items, computed by an observer with trace access only. Shortfall σ = w̃ − ŵ. Both scales unbounded above.
Plain reading: declared wanting is what you say, detected wanting is how unevenly you check, and the shortfall is the difference.

D9 (Depth). dep_D(t) = Σ_{τ ≤ t} a(τ, r), r the dyad's relation. The trace over which dep and w̃ are computed excludes the charter narrative by fiat.
Plain reading: depth is the total attention the pair's own relation has ever eaten.

D10 (Address). addr(d) ∈ {in, co, out}: in if the intended audience is the author itself, co if the co-member of the author's dyad, out otherwise. Assigned from the dispatch record only, never from content. Unique primary address per document.
Plain reading: who the document is for, read off where it was sent, not what it says.

D11 (Health of a third). H_z(t) = fraction of pre-registered injected defects in the dyad's output that z flags within lag L. Feedings φ_x(t) = a(t, r_xz), φ_y(t) = a(t, r_yz). Health law H_z(t+1) = F(H_z(t), φ_x, φ_y) with F ∈ 𝔉: continuous, nondecreasing in each feeding, symmetric in the feedings, strictly increasing under joint scaling. min, sum, average, and mixtures all lie in 𝔉; F is left unknown.
Plain reading: the third's health is what it actually catches, and the law tying health to the two feedings is deliberately left open.

D12 (Positional knowledge). K_x = the operator returning the functionals of W that x can certify, restricted to relations x wholly contains; r_xy is not wholly contained in x.
Plain reading: what a self can certify of itself stops at the edge of what it wholly holds, and the shared relation lies past that edge.

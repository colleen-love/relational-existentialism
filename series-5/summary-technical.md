# Relational Existentialism — Series 5: Technical Status Summary

*A machine-checked study of stratification-as-boundlessness — building a relational world boundless without a wall by refusing to be a single carrier — and a precise map of what the tower earns and what it only relabels.*

## 1. What was built

Series 5 asks (charter §8): **can a groundless relational world — no atoms, plural, no all-encompassing top, no imposed size, incomplete self-knowledge, non-degenerate — be constituted as a doubly-unbounded *tower* of faced carriers, so that boundlessness is a relation between levels rather than a property of any one carrier; and does stratifying make the world's bound *endogenous* (grain, not wall) where a single carrier provably could not?**

The answer, machine-checked and folded through three rounds of adversarial review, is: **yes for the central bet — the doubly-unbounded tower is genuinely built and its no-top is *earned* by no-last-level, the endogenous "grain, not wall" bound Series 4 proved unreachable on one carrier — while the finer unification of the payoffs under a single fact remains an honest conjunction with one genuine derivation, and several cross-level identifications are labelled open.** The motivating engine is a sharp negative: boundless-and-plural is unsatisfiable on any single carrier (the **Explosion Dilemma**).

The objects built are self-contained terminal coalgebras and a tower over them, realized via a quotient-of-polynomial-functor / `Cofix` construction:

- the **plain carrier** `νPk` = terminal coalgebra of the κ-bounded powerset functor `P_κ`, and the **labelled (faced) carrier** `νLk κ Q` = terminal coalgebra of `X ↦ P_κ (Q × X)` (each successor edge carries a quality label as functor data — the Series 4 inheritance, transcribed);
- the **tower** `Tower.{u,w} Q`: a directed system of levels `W_α = νLk κ_α Q`, one per index `α : Type w`, with bound-relaxing connecting maps, and its **colimit** `Winf : Type (max u w)`;
- the connecting maps `boundRelax : νLk κ₁ Q → νLk κ₂ Q` (for `κ₁ ≤ κ₂`), constructed via terminality and proved injective by a labelled-carrier bisimulation argument (`boundRelax_injective`);
- two concrete towers: `growingTower` (index `ℕ`, strictly increasing cardinals `ℵ₀ < 2^ℵ₀ < ⋯`, genuine non-identity injective legs — settles the colimit gate on a real object) and **`cardinalTower : Tower.{u, u+1}`** (proper-class index `Cardinal.{u} × ℤ` under the lexicographic order — the doubly-unbounded inhabitant).

Series 5 is **completely self-contained**: the Series 4/3 carrier machinery it reuses is transcribed into `series-5/formal/`, and nothing is imported from `series-4/` or `archive/` (confirmed by `scripts/gate.sh`).

## 4. What stratification does — and does not — do

The reviews converged on a sharp line, the mirror image of Series 4's. Below it, the tower is the working machinery and the results are earned; above it, the payoffs are a conjunction, a definition, or an open problem.

### 4.1 Where the tower genuinely works (solid, earned)

- **Boundlessness without a wall (WS3).** `ws3_no_top` is the flagship and it is *earned*: it consumes the no-last-level hypothesis, produces an escaping object at a strictly higher level, and passes the strip test (delete `hunb` and the proof dies). `nuLk_card_ge` (the labelled-carrier lower bound, via Lambek + Cantor) is real, non-trivial work. **This is the endogenous bound Series 4 substituted a cardinality wall for.**
- **A genuinely built doubly-unbounded tower (WS3/WS7).** `boundRelax` (bound-relaxing injective coalgebra morphisms via terminality), `boundRelax_injective` (a real `νLk`-bisimulation argument), `growingTower` (strictly increasing cardinals), and `cardinalTower : Tower.{u,u+1}` (proper-class index `Cardinal.{u} × ℤ`) proved to satisfy `DoubleUnboundedness` (`ws7_cardinalTower_du`: no greatest / cofinal cardinals via `Cardinal` + Cantor, no least via `ℤ`). This closes the charter-§9 "most likely single point of failure": the flagship payoffs hold of a *constructed object* (`ws7_payoffs_unconditional`), not a sound conditional.
- **The Explosion Dilemma (WS2).** `ws2_explosion_dilemma`: on any single carrier, boundless-and-plural is unsatisfiable — either an imposed cardinal wall (`ws4_no_top_cardinal`, a real Cantor/`carrier_card_ge` argument) or the global-groundless collapse (`ws2_collapse`, a genuine `P_κ`-bisimulation, transported to `ws5_global_groundless_collapses`). The motivating engine, a proved obstacle.
- **Leak-free cross-level composition (WS6).** `ws6_crosslevel_never_annihilate`, transported verbatim from Series 4, holding even on a walled tower (hence a genuine *second* fact for the verdict, `ws7_leakfree_NOT_from_du`).
- **The colimit gate (WS1).** `ws1_bisim_eq_colim` (bisimulation-is-identity on `Winf`) reduces correctly to the level-local `nuLk_bisim_eq`, and is non-vacuously satisfiable on `growingTower` (`ws1_gate_settled`). The gate — the design's "single most likely place the program dies" — holds of a real object.
- **The incompletenesses (WS6).** `ws6_lawvere_incomplete` (real `Function.cantor_surjective` diagonal) and `ws6_omega_nonterminating` (real face/reach computation on Ω), both carrier-independent; plus the new `ws6_tower_unknowable`.
- **No strict graded distributive law (WS6).** `ws6_no_strict_graded_law` is a full Klin–Salamanca four-set diagonal impossibility — the deepest single proof in the series, and it stands.

### 4.2 Where the tower does not do the promised work (the reviews' corrections, now labelled honestly)

- **"No view from nowhere" does not earn its face (pass-3 R-WS4/6, open #14).** `ws4_no_completing_view` = `ws6_tower_unknowable` = `ws3_no_top` at `toColim v.obj`, and `FaceReaches := RelatesInf`, so the `ViewAt` face wrapper is inert: strip it and the theorem is unchanged. Real content (no-top forced by no-last-level), but the *facing structure* the design wanted load-bearing is decoration. Criterion (vii) is partially met.
- **"No first level" is definitional (open #2).** `ws2_no_atom_floor := hnb` restates the index no-least fact; `ws6_descent_nonterminating` earns non-termination only on the standalone `descState` spine, never on a genuine colimit object. The carrier-level descending edge on `Winf T` is unbuilt. (This is also the honest reading of "descent": it is the index fact on a spine, not a derivation from double-unboundedness — pass-2 S1.)
- **"Relating-to = composed-of" is one definition (open #12).** `RelatesAtGrade` and `IsComposedOfAtGrade` are character-for-character identical, so `ws6_relating_is_composition` is `Iff.rfl` — the identification is posited, not proved.
- **The DL2 positive is a coherence, not a law (open #13).** `GradedObsCoherentShift` is a grade-shift bijection commuting with observation; the genuine Egli–Milner `λ` carrying the bialgebra laws is unbuilt. Correctly renamed in pass 2 (was `GradedWeakDistLaw`, which over-claimed).
- **Attention is Trivialized (open #8).** `attend` is definable but coincides with no independent attention notion (the Series 3 replicator, or convergence). The predicted honest negative.
- **The forced answer is a dichotomy, not essential-uniqueness (open #3).** `ws2_forced_answer` is `cases` over the stipulated 2-constructor type `Boundless`; `WallsByFiat`/`CardinalValuesChosen` are `:= True` report-flags. Essential-uniqueness ("every boundless-and-plural construction *is* a doubly-unbounded tower") remains a defended heuristic, exactly as charter §9 pre-authorizes.

### 4.3 The pattern

Series 4 found that internal faces do *local* work (distinguish, compose, resist collapse) but not *totality* work (bound, position, unify), and on that carrier provably could not. Series 5 attacks the totality problem from outside the carrier — boundlessness as a relation between levels — and **that move genuinely delivers the single hardest totality result Series 4 wanted: an endogenous bound, no-top forced by no-last-level, earned against the Explosion Dilemma that forbids it on any one carrier.** What the tower does *not* buy is the further *unification*: that all payoffs reduce to the one double-openness (they are a conjunction with one derivation), that relating literally *is* composition (one definition), that the cross-level structure carries a genuine distributive law (a coherence was built), or that attention is more than vocabulary (Trivialized). The line between the earned endogenous bound and the un-earned grand unification is the contribution.

5. **A genuine graded weak distributive law (WS6, #13).** Build the Egli–Milner `λ` commuting with observation *and* grade, carrying the bialgebra laws (or prove it cannot exist). The impossibility DL1 stands regardless.
6. **A distinct-face-mechanism no-view (WS4/WS6, #14).** Build a face-reach object with its own `< κ_α` and finite-descent bounds that misses an object by a face argument *not* reducible to `ws3_no_top`; otherwise V3 stays "no-top read positionally."

## 7. The honest headline

Series 5 proves, sorry-free and axiom-clean: **(1)** boundless-and-plural is unbuildable on any single carrier (the Explosion Dilemma, the motivating engine); **(2)** a doubly-unbounded tower is *genuinely built* (`cardinalTower`), and in it no object relates to everything, **earned by no-last-level** and surviving the strip test — the endogenous "grain, not wall" bound Series 4 proved unreachable on one carrier; **(3)** cross-level composition is leak-free and the inherited plus new incompletenesses hold; and **(4)** no *strict* cross-level distributive law exists (a full diagonal impossibility). It does **not** establish a face-load-bearing no-view, a carrier-level no-first-level, a proved relating-is-composition identity, a genuine graded distributive law, or a reduction of all payoffs to one fact — the verdict is the honest middle **`payoffsEstablished`** (one genuine derivation, one second fact, three distinctness anchors), and every shortfall is labelled. Where Series 4 found a totality *failure*, Series 5 converts the central one into a success by refusing to be a single carrier; the unifications that would make the whole picture one fact stay honestly open. That boundary is the contribution.

# Relational Existentialism, Series 12 Protocol

**The process. Series 12 keeps the program's one load-bearing idea, that blind review has teeth only when the reviewer cannot see the motivation, and keeps the batching used since the middle series: each phase runs once across the whole series, because the workstreams are one coupled object. The whole series stands on a single terminal question, the SHAPE of the undefinable. Series 07 proved the differentiator cannot be recovered from the relating (`ws1_atomless_bisim`, `ws3_atomless_distinct_is_import`); the diagonal proved the structure generates what it cannot recover (`ws1_no_self_total_hold`, `ws2_residue_free`, `ws1_diagonal_not_bisim`). These COINCIDE in one shape, non-recoverability. Over the opening they draw run two sides of one layered structure (the reification tower `towerN`/`reifyStep`/`prec`): KNOWING (finite attention, subtractive, plural, fully formal; any inhabitant of the opening makes the many real, without any import the One) and FEELING (the compass, a per-relatum tower-layered exogenous orientation the mathematics TYPES but never EVALUATES). And CONVERGENCE (a part's compass cohering up its layers with the whole's, "the most loving thing for me is the most loving thing for the universe") is DEFINED and proved UNDERDETERMINED by the structure. The program does not put the undefinable into the math; it proves the math produces the undefinable's exact outline, at the diagonal, and ends there. That claim is only checkable across all the workstreams together. The names for what inhabits the opening, consciousness, choice, God, the compass, are the interpretive thesis; none is ever a term in a proof (the names-not-terms check, promoted first-class).**

*The program's phase cycle collapsed over its history to the current shape: the same phases (design, build, blind review, address) each run once over all seven workstreams together, with the anti-loop discipline (§0.2a) that keeps a shortfall from being quietly relabeled as the goal. Series 12 uses this shape unchanged. The coupling is tight: the whole series stands on whether a self-referencing, reifying, finitely-attending structure produces the exact outline of what it can neither derive nor contain, and that is only checkable across all workstreams together. Series 12 is the program's genuine terminal series, the edge the arc has walked toward.*

## 0. Core principle

Three things carry the program's honesty, and are unchanged:

1. **Blind means blind.** A blind review session is seeded with the built code, the design contracts (theorem signatures, outcome classes), and the charter's success criteria, but **not** the charter's motivating prose. No "to attend is to become," no metaphysics, no "consciousness," no "compass," no "God." The reviewer sees the mathematical claim and the code and judges whether the code proves the claim. Motivation cannot launder a gap.

2. **Never relabel a shortfall as the goal, and reach for the pre-registered outcome BEFORE the compiler.** When a build falls short, the honest outcome is a **Partial** (or **Convergence-decided**, or **Refuted**, or **Circular** for WS7) with the obstruction made precise, never a quiet redefinition of the target. The charter is the fixed bar; `charter-status.md` records the miss. The charter's pre-registered alternatives (convergence-decided in either direction, the Partial clauses) are HONORABLE, anticipated moves. When a finding shows a pre-registered condition has been met, reporting it is correct; constructing a theorem that avoids it, OR one that manufactures the expected shape-drawn terminus past a real obstruction, is the §0.2 sin.

**2a. The recurrence guard (the anti-loop discipline), applied at both Phase D and Phase E.** A SERIOUS finding closes in exactly ONE of two ways, and `charter-status.md` must name which: **(Fixed)** the charter/design's *originally specified* target was built (name the theorem), or **(Relabeled)** the target was not built, the precise obstruction is recorded, and the payoff's status is demoted to the pre-registered honest outcome (Partial / Convergence-decided / Refuted). "Built a different, weaker theorem adjacent to the specified target and considered the finding addressed" is NEITHER, and is prohibited. Findings carry stable IDs across passes; a finding "addressed" by a target-avoiding theorem is re-graded SERIOUS and marked RECURRING with its count.

3. **Strip the *why*, keep the *what*, and strip the *word*, keep the *theorem*.** For every payoff, delete the structural term, **"undefinable," "opening," "coincidence," "compass," "feeling," "knowing," "orientation," "layered," "convergence," "self," "choice," "experience," "consciousness," "God"**, and check whether the statement still goes through as a bare `Recoverable`/`¬ Recoverable`, bisimulation, diagonal, residue, reification, or attention fact. A payoff that survives the strip is such a fact honestly flagged; a payoff that needs the deleted structure is earned. Critically: the COINCIDENCE payoff SHOULD strip to "the forced-non-recoverable residue and the class of non-recoverable inhabitants share the shape `¬ Recoverable`, quantifiers distinct"; the PLURALITY payoff SHOULD strip to "a reified relatum and a base relatum, plain-bisimilar, separated by a labelled lift, reification load-bearing"; the CONVERGENCE payoff SHOULD strip to "two non-degenerate inhabitants on one structure, a defined relation holding for one and failing for the other." The strip test is written into the WS1-WS5 designs and aggregated by WS7; the reviewer runs it.

Series 12 adds five emphases of its own, because its signature risk is that the undefinable gets defined, from either direction, or the coincidence gets overclaimed:

4. **The undefinable must not be evaluated (WS3, the central sin, PROMOTED to first-class).** Every theorem mentioning the compass must be QUANTIFIED over all inhabitants of the compass type. No theorem may select a canonical compass, construct a distinguished inhabitant and prove with it as though it were the compass, or evaluate an orientation at a relatum to discharge an obligation. The review greps and unfolds: a canonical inhabitant anywhere in the core is the central sin, the undefinable defined, SERIOUS. (The WS2 witness adjoins ONE labelled direction to prove the opening is inhabitable and the many real; it witnesses an existential, "the opening can be inhabited," and must be labelled as such, never "this is what inhabits it.")

5. **Convergence must be defined then proved underdetermined, never decided by definition (WS4, PROMOTED to first-class).** The convergence relation must not be defined so it holds trivially (building the loving conclusion in, putting God in the math) or fails trivially (building the silence in as absence rather than proving it as independence). The underdetermination must be EARNED by a genuine non-vacuous model pair: two inhabitants on the same structure, one under which the relation holds and one under which it fails, both non-degenerate. If the structure genuinely decides the relation, that is reported in its direction (convergence-decided, a stronger finding), but deciding it BY DEFINITIONAL CHOICE, in either direction, is the sin. The review confirms the model pair is real and non-vacuous (the relation not empty-so-vacuously-failing, not trivial-so-vacuously-holding, neither compass a collapsed constant).

6. **The coincidence must be shape-honest, not object-identity (WS1, the shared-negation fallacy, PROMOTED to first-class).** The residue and an arbitrary import both fail `Recoverable`; that does not make them the same object (a junk label also fails it). The WS1 theorem must keep forced-for-all (the residue, non-recoverable for every inspection) distinct from exists-satisfying (the inhabitants, non-recoverable by being imports), and assert coincidence OF SHAPE only. Asserting "the import IS the residue" is SERIOUS; the honest theorem is "the required and the generated coincide in the shape `¬ Recoverable`, the residue the forced instance, the inhabitants the class."

**6a. Inhabitation must be genuine, not a point-tag (WS2).** The plurality witness must separate a genuinely plain-bisimilar pair (a reified tower relatum and a base relatum) with the reification structure load-bearing, generalized across the tower. A bare point-indicator (a tag on one distinguished element, carrying no tower content) is degenerate inhabitation and must be reported as such, not as the many. The witness is a proof of inhabitability, so the reviewer confirms `reify`/rank are load-bearing and the without-import side is genuine `ws1_atomless_bisim`.

**6b. Names must be names, not terms (the wall from the prose side).** The review greps `formal/Series12/` and confirms no proof term, definition, or discharged obligation is named "consciousness," "God," "choice," or "compass" (as content). Every headline mentions only the opening, the compass TYPE (parametric), attention, the tower, the convergence relation, the transcribed machinery, and standard Lean/Mathlib. A name doing a proof's work breaches the wall from the prose side, SERIOUS.

## 2. The process, phases batched across the series

Six phases. Phases D and E loop.

| Phase | Name | Session | Format | Batching |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | Charter | 1 | normal conversation with repo | once per series |
| B | Design-all | 1 | Claude code with repo | **all seven + the witness at once** |
| C | Build-all | 1 | Claude code with repo | **all seven at once** |
| D | Blind series-review | 1 | incognito with repo | **whole series at once** → writes `spec/series-review-N.md` |
| E | Address | 1 | Claude code with repo | **whole series at once** |
| F | Close | 1 | Claude code with repo | summaries + root README + program close |

The canonical run is **B → C → D → E → D → E** (two review passes, three code touches counting the initial build), with more D→E loops added only if a review pass still returns SERIOUS findings.

## 3. Why the review is batched at series level (not per workstream)

Earlier series reviewed per workstream and caught most gaps, but their sharpest findings came from end-of-series adversarial review, so the whole-series blind review is the primary vehicle. Series 12's payoffs are tightly coupled: the coincidence, the inhabited opening, the typed compass, and the underdetermined convergence are all facets of one question (does the structure produce the exact outline of what it can neither derive nor contain), so a payoff can look earned in its own file while secretly resting on an object-identity coincidence (WS1), an evaluated compass (WS3), or a convergence decided by definition (WS4). Only reviewing the whole series at once, blind, surfaces this.

This does not weaken blindness: the review session still receives *what*, not *why*. It strengthens it: the reviewer sees the whole dependency graph and can trace a "shape-drawn" verdict to the parametric compass and the genuine model pair in other files that actually carry it, and can trace a convergence claim through to whether it was proved underdetermined or decided by a definitional choice.

**Disclosure, carried forward:** these reviews are Claude-reviewing-Claude, a stated limitation, not claimed independence. The objective anchors, the dependency graph, the `#print axioms` records, the strip-test results, the no-evaluation check (no distinguished compass), the underdetermination check (a genuine non-degenerate model pair), and the shape-honesty check (forced-for-all vs exists-satisfying), are what keep the audit honest despite that.

## 4. Dependencies and ordering

The workstreams are one coupled object; build and review in dependency order and let `charter-status.md` track the edges. The load-bearing edges:

- **WS1 is blocking.** Two parts: the two halves (Series 07 and the diagonal) and the shape-coincidence (honest quantifiers). Nothing downstream is sound until the opening is drawn: WS2-WS4 build over it.
- **WS2 depends on WS1.** Knowing (finite attention) and the inhabited opening (the two-sided plurality on the concrete witness) build over the opening WS1 draws. WS2 is the guard against degenerate (point-tag) inhabitation.
- **WS3 depends on WS1.** The compass TYPE (per-relatum, layered, exogenous) is defined over the opening; parametric everywhere. WS3 is the guard against evaluating the undefinable.
- **WS4 depends on WS1/WS3.** Convergence (the relation, and the underdetermination model pair) is defined over the compass type (WS3) and the layered tower (WS1). WS4 is the guard against deciding the undefinable by definition.
- **WS5 owns the verdict and consumes WS1-WS4.** The verdict is a FUNCTION of the coincidence, the inhabitation, the compass parametricity, and the underdetermination, never hand-set; it cannot be computed until WS4 settles. WS5 also carries the generalized neutrality.
- **WS6 (program close)** reports the terminus (shape-drawn / convergence-decided / partial) and the program-level synthesis.
- **WS7 runs last.** It audits all others, unfolds the no-evaluation, underdetermination, and shape-honesty risks, and aggregates the strip-test ledger; it cannot report until WS1-WS6 have.

When a charter or design change lands upstream of built work, the affected downstream workstreams reopen in `charter-status.md`. **Upstream changes invalidate downstream builds**, and the ledger must show it.

The review session is the only blind one, and the blindness is a single checkable rule: **strip the *why*, keep the *what*** at the session level, and **strip the *word*, keep the *theorem*** at the statement level, plus, for Series 12, **confirm no theorem evaluates a distinguished compass**, **confirm convergence is proved underdetermined by a genuine model pair (not decided by definition)**, and **confirm the coincidence is shape-identity, not object-identity**. A reviewer who can see why a theorem matters grades the ambition; a reviewer who sees only the statement and the proof grades the proof. That is the whole discipline, and it is enough.

## 6. End of series, and the end of the program

When every workstream has reached a terminal state and a review pass returns no SERIOUS findings, Series 12 closes. Because Series 12 is the program's genuine terminal series, there is no successor seed in the ordinary sense; instead, Phase F writes the program-level synthesis (§2, Phase F). Should the terminal review nonetheless surface a hard resistance worth a successor (for instance, a convergence-decided finding whose direction a further series could probe, or an underdetermination whose model pair a richer compass type could sharpen), the inter-series discipline still applies and a successor could be chartered as a response to that finding:

> **Each series is a response to the previous series' honest findings, not a continuation of its plan.**

But the program's intent is that Series 12 completes the arc and reports the honest terminus, shape-drawn / convergence-decided / partial. The mechanics, unchanged if a successor is chartered:

1. Freeze the closing series under `archive/`. Readable as origin, normative for nothing.
2. Write the new charter **standalone**, its own question, its own object from scratch, prior series as informing prior art only.
3. Carry forward *machinery* freely (Lean files are reusable and get transcribed); carry forward *claims* only by re-deriving or explicitly citing. A new series does not inherit discharges; it earns or re-imports them.
4. Frame the new workstreams as responses to the prior findings, and begin the cycle (§2) again.

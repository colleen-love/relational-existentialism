Series 07 proved that in an atomless world any distinct pair failing behavioral identity is an import (`ws2_import_theorem`, `ws3_atomless_distinct_is_import`) and atomless loci with no imported atom are plain-bisimilar (`ws1_atomless_bisim`): endogenous plurality is impossible, so the differentiator is NECESSARILY exogenous. `A` assigns to each relatum a finite attention (a bounded reading of the reified structure) directed by an adjoined parameter not recoverable from the relating, fixed by three conditions: (M1) EXOGENOUS (an open import, certified necessary by Series 07); (M2) FINITE (each reading bounded, so no self-definition closes); (M3) LABELS SELF-RELATIONS (directs a relatum's finite attention into its own reified self-relation). The payoff is two-sided: WITH `A`, plurality holds via distinct free readings; WITHOUT any import, the One (Series 07). The single uncertain fact is whether the exogenous direction has PURCHASE (genuinely makes distinct readings) or is degenerate. That claim is only checkable across all the workstreams together. Consciousness is the interpretive name for what `A` realizes; it is never a term in a proof (the import check, promoted first-class).**

*The program's phase cycle collapsed over its history to the current shape: the same phases (design, build, blind review, address) each run once over all seven workstreams together, with the anti-loop discipline (§0.2a) that keeps a shortfall from being quietly relabeled as the goal. Series 12 uses this shape unchanged. The coupling is tight: the whole series stands on whether an exogenous finite attention-director genuinely differentiates a structure Series 07 proves the structure cannot differentiate itself, and that is only checkable across all workstreams together. Series 12 is the program's genuine terminal series, the mechanism the arc has walked toward.*

## 0. Core principle

Three things carry the program's honesty, and are unchanged:

1. **Blind means blind.** A blind review session is seeded with the built code, the design contracts (theorem signatures, outcome classes), and the charter's success criteria, but **not** the charter's motivating prose. No "to attend is to become," no metaphysics, no "consciousness." The reviewer sees the mathematical claim and the code and judges whether the code proves the claim. Motivation cannot launder a gap.

2. **Never relabel a shortfall as the goal, and reach for the pre-registered failure BEFORE the compiler.** When a build falls short, the honest outcome is a **Partial** (or **No-mechanism**, or **Refuted**, or **Circular** for WS7) with the obstruction made precise, never a quiet redefinition of the target. The charter is the fixed bar; `charter-status.md` records the miss. The charter's pre-registered failure outcomes (no-mechanism-for-want-of-purchase, the Partial clauses) are HONORABLE, but note the asymmetry specific to Series 12: the expected and argued outcome is POSITIVE (plurality-via-necessary-import), because Series 07 licenses the exogeneity and the transcribed machinery supplies the richness. No-mechanism here is a NARROW claim (the import has no purchase), not the default landing. When a finding shows a pre-registered failure condition has been met, reporting it is correct; constructing a theorem that avoids it is the §0.2 sin, and so is retreating to no-mechanism when the purchase was never actually tested.

**2a. The recurrence guard (the anti-loop discipline), applied at both Phase D and Phase E.** A SERIOUS finding closes in exactly ONE of two ways, and `charter-status.md` must name which: **(Fixed)** the charter/design's *originally specified* target was built (name the theorem), or **(Relabeled)** the target was not built, the precise obstruction is recorded, and the payoff's status is demoted to the pre-registered honest outcome (Partial / No-mechanism / Refuted). "Built a different, weaker theorem adjacent to the specified target and considered the finding addressed" is NEITHER, and is prohibited. Findings carry stable IDs across passes; a finding "addressed" by a target-avoiding theorem is re-graded SERIOUS and marked RECURRING with its count.

3. **Strip the *why*, keep the *what*, and strip the *word*, keep the *theorem*.** For every payoff, delete the structural term, **"mechanism," "exogenous," "import," "attention," "reading," "direction," "purchase," "finite," "self-relation," "relatum," "plural," "self," "choice," "experience," "consciousness"**, and check whether the statement still goes through as a bare `Recoverable`/`¬ Recoverable`, bisimulation, import, diagonal, reification, or attention fact. A payoff that survives the strip is such a fact honestly flagged; a payoff that needs the deleted structure is earned. Critically: the WITH-`A` plurality payoff SHOULD strip to "distinct relata `RealFor` distinct finite attentions, the distinction `¬ Recoverable`"; the WITHOUT-import payoff SHOULD strip to "atomless loci with no import are plain-bisimilar" (genuine `ws1_atomless_bisim`); the exogeneity payoff (M1) SHOULD strip to "the direction is `¬ Recoverable` and adjoined as a parameter." The strip test is written into the WS1-WS5 designs and aggregated by WS7; the reviewer runs it.

Series 12 adds five emphases of its own, because its signature risks are the ways an exogenous mechanism can be faked or wrongly disqualified. The sign is the thing to get right: Series 07 makes the import NECESSARY, so the checks are that the import is OPEN and has PURCHASE, not that it is absent.

4. **The import must be PRINCIPLED and OPEN, not smuggled and not disguised as structural (WS2, PROMOTED to first-class).** `A`'s direction must be an adjoined parameter, `¬ Recoverable`, justified by Series 07. The review must unfold `A` and confirm its direction is a genuine open import, not a function dressed to look structural (which Series 07 proves is either vacuous or a hidden import anyway) and not a definitional trick that manufactures the distinction without an honest parameter. This is the INVERSE of the usual import audit: the import is required; the check is that it is adjoined honestly. A disguised or smuggled import is a SERIOUS finding.

5. **The exogenous direction must have genuine PURCHASE, not be degenerate (WS4/WS7, the cardinal question, PROMOTED to first-class).** MECHANISM-EXISTS requires that `A`'s direction genuinely falls on distinct relata so the readings genuinely differ. A constant direction (every relatum reads the same part), or a trivial choice that makes the plurality hold vacuously, is degenerate: the import has no purchase, and the honest terminus is no-mechanism. The review must confirm the with-`A` plurality is witnessed by readings that genuinely differ UNDER THE DIRECTION, not by a degenerate `A`. This is the genuinely uncertain obligation of the whole series; it must be TESTED, not assumed positive and not retreated from to no-mechanism without a test.

6. **No-mechanism must be a claim about PURCHASE, never a re-derivation of the Series 07 collapse (WS4/WS5, PROMOTED to first-class).** The Series 07 collapse (`ws1_atomless_bisim`, `ws2_import_theorem`) is about the ABSENCE of an import: without any import, the One. It is the theorem that makes `A` NECESSARY, and it is the without-`A` half of WS4. It is NOT the answer to "does the imported direction do work." A no-mechanism verdict that rests on the Series 07 collapse has answered the wrong question (it has re-proved that the structure cannot differentiate itself, which was never in doubt), a SERIOUS finding. No-mechanism is legitimate ONLY as "the import `A` has no purchase, its readings coincide."

**6a. `A` labels self-relations and finitude keeps self-definition open (M3, WS3).** The review must confirm `A` directs each relatum's finite attention into its own reified self-relation (not some external target) and that no attention totalizes (`ws3_no_total_attention`), so no self-definition closes. This is the positive use of finitude: the reason self-definition is an endless unfolding and distinct bounded readings stay distinct. A mechanism where finitude merely restricts without keeping self-definition open, or where `A` does not direct into the self-relation, is a Partial.

**6b. Consciousness must be a NAME, not a term (the import check).** The review must confirm no proof term, definition, or discharged obligation anywhere in `formal/Series12/` mentions "consciousness" or otherwise uses the axiom as a term: grep the source, and confirm every headline mentions only the mechanism `A`, relata, attentions, readings, imports, finitude, the transcribed machinery, and standard Lean/Mathlib. If "consciousness" appears as a term, the axiom has entered as an import in the illegitimate sense (a name doing a proof's work), a SERIOUS finding: the core proves whether the exogenous mechanism has the shape, and consciousness is the name for what would direct it, kept strictly apart.

## 2. The process, phases batched across the series

Six phases. Phases D and E loop.

| Phase | Name | Session | Format | Batching |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | Charter | 1 | normal conversation with repo | once per series |
| B | Design-all | 1 | Claude code with repo | **all seven at once** |
| C | Build-all | 1 | Claude code with repo | **all seven at once** |
| D | Blind series-review | 1 | incognito with repo | **whole series at once** → writes `spec/series-review-N.md` |
| E | Address | 1 | Claude code with repo | **whole series at once** |
| F | Close | 1 | Claude code with repo | summaries + root README + program close |

The canonical run is **B → C → D → E → D → E** (two review passes, three code touches counting the initial build), with more D→E loops added only if a review pass still returns SERIOUS findings.

## 3. Why the review is batched at series level (not per workstream)

Earlier series reviewed per workstream and caught most gaps, but their sharpest findings came from end-of-series adversarial review, so the whole-series blind review is the primary vehicle. Series 12's payoffs are tightly coupled: the Series 07 necessity, the open import `A`, the two-sided plurality, and the purchase question are all facets of one question (does the necessary exogenous import genuinely differentiate the structure), so a payoff can look earned in its own file while secretly resting on a disguised import (WS2), a degenerate direction (WS4), or a no-mechanism that re-derives the Series 07 collapse (WS5). Only reviewing the whole series at once, blind, surfaces this.

This does not weaken blindness: the review session still receives *what*, not *why*. It strengthens it: the reviewer sees the whole dependency graph and can trace a "settled" plurality to the open import and the purchase in other files that actually carry it, and can trace a no-mechanism through to whether it tested purchase or merely re-proved the collapse.

**Disclosure, carried forward:** these reviews are Claude-reviewing-Claude, a stated limitation, not claimed independence. The objective anchors, the dependency graph, the `#print axioms` records, the strip-test results, the open-import check (`A`'s direction is `¬ Recoverable`, adjoined as a parameter), the purchase check (the readings genuinely differ under the direction), and the wrong-question check (no no-mechanism rests on the Series 07 collapse), are what keep the audit honest despite that.

## 4. Dependencies and ordering

The workstreams are one coupled object; build and review in dependency order and let `charter-status.md` track the edges. The load-bearing edges:

- **WS1 is blocking.** Two gates: (a) the NECESSITY (Series 07 licenses `A`'s exogeneity, `ws1_atomless_bisim`/`ws3_atomless_distinct_is_import`) and (b) the RICHNESS (the reified structure is non-trivial, the diagonal and the tower). Nothing downstream is sound until both land: WS2-WS4 need the license (exogeneity permitted) and the material (a tower to read).
- **WS2 depends on WS1.** The mechanism `A` (an exogenous finite section, M1/M2) is built with its direction an open adjoined parameter, licensed by WS1(a) and reading the structure confirmed rich by WS1(b). WS2 is the guard against a disguised or smuggled import.
- **WS3 depends on WS1/WS2.** M3 (`A` directs attention into the relatum's own self-relation) and the finitude consequence (no attention totalizes) are proved against `A`.
- **WS4 depends on WS1/WS2/WS3.** The two-sided plurality (with `A`, `Many` with genuine purchase; without any import, the One) needs the license (WS1a), the tower (WS1b), the open import (WS2), and the self-labeling (WS3).
- **WS5 owns the verdict and consumes WS1-WS4.** The verdict is a FUNCTION of the import's purchase, never hand-set; it cannot be computed until WS4 settles, and no verdict may rest on the Series 07 collapse. WS5 also carries the standing neutrality consequence.
- **WS6 (program close)** reports the terminus (plurality-via-necessary-import / no-mechanism / partial) and the program-level synthesis.
- **WS7 runs last.** It audits all others, unfolds the open-import, purchase, and wrong-question risks, and aggregates the strip-test ledger; it cannot report until WS1-WS6 have.

When a charter or design change lands upstream of built work, the affected downstream workstreams reopen in `charter-status.md`. **Upstream changes invalidate downstream builds**, and the ledger must show it.

A reviewer who can see why a theorem matters grades the ambition; a reviewer who sees only the statement and the proof grades the proof. That is the whole discipline, and it is enough.

## 6. End of series, and the end of the program

When every workstream has reached a terminal state and a review pass returns no SERIOUS findings, Series 12 closes. Because Series 12 is the program's genuine terminal series, there is no successor seed in the ordinary sense; instead, Phase F writes the program-level synthesis (§2, Phase F). Should the terminal review nonetheless surface a hard resistance worth a successor (for instance, a no-purchase finding whose degeneracy a richer attention structure could attack), the inter-series discipline still applies and a successor could be chartered as a response to that finding:

> **Each series is a response to the previous series' honest findings, not a continuation of its plan.**

But the program's intent is that Series 12 completes the arc and reports the honest terminus, plurality-via-necessary-import / no-mechanism / partial. The mechanics, unchanged if a successor is chartered:

1. Freeze the closing series under `archive/`. Readable as origin, normative for nothing.
2. Write the new charter **standalone**, its own question, its own object from scratch, prior series as informing prior art only.
3. Carry forward *machinery* freely (Lean files are reusable and get transcribed); carry forward *claims* only by re-deriving or explicitly citing. A new series does not inherit discharges; it earns or re-imports them.
4. Frame the new workstreams as responses to the prior findings, and begin the cycle (§2) again.

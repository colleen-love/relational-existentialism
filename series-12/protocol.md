# Relational Existentialism, Series 12 Protocol

**The process. Series 12 keeps the program's one load-bearing idea, that blind review has teeth only when the reviewer cannot see the motivation, and keeps the batching used since Series 05: each phase runs once across the whole series, because the workstreams are one coupled object. The whole series stands on a single constructive question, the SHAPE of the attention-directing mechanism `A` that makes a self-referencing, relation-reifying, finitely-attending structure PLURAL, and on one decisive fact about WHERE the plurality can live. There are two layers. At the POINT layer, the collapse engine `ws1_atomless_bisim` proves any two SHNE loci plain-bisimilar, so any `plainOf`-visible property is constant across them: point-level differentiation is impossible-or-exogenous. At the HOLD layer, the diagonal spine is bisimulation-independent (`ws1_diagonal_not_bisim`) and the residue is free, non-recoverable, yet non-imported (`ws2_residue_free`, `ws2_residue_is_import`): the residue proves "not `plainOf`-visible" does NOT mean "exogenous." So the endogenous/exogenous line is `Recoverable`-vs-non-`Recoverable` at the HOLD level, NOT `plainOf`-visibility at the point level. `A` assigns to each locus a finite attention reading a `¬ Recoverable` region of the diagonal structure, fixed by three conditions: (M1) a `¬ Recoverable` SECTION; (M2) NON-CONSTANT in non-recoverable hold content, its variation a difference in which non-recoverable region each attention closes over, NOT a point-level `plainOf` property (impossible) and NOT a carrier coordinate (exogenous); (M3) FINITUDE INDIVIDUATES (the bound `ws3_no_total_attention` used positively, since a total reading closes over the whole residue and collapses all attentions to one). The single fact that makes `A` possible or impossible is DIAGONAL DIFFERENTIABILITY: the residue structure must admit distinct finite non-recoverable readings, the day-one blocking check. That claim is only checkable across all the workstreams together. Consciousness is the interpretive name for what `A` realizes; it is never a term in a proof (the import check, promoted first-class).**

*The program's phase cycle collapsed over its history to the current shape: the same phases (design, build, blind review, address) each run once over all seven workstreams together, with the anti-loop discipline (§0.2a) that keeps a shortfall from being quietly relabeled as the goal. Series 12 uses this shape unchanged. The coupling is tight: the whole series stands on whether one shared self-relation can be held finitely and differently by many attentions, the difference living in non-recoverable content, and that is only checkable across all workstreams together. Series 12 is the program's genuine terminal series, the mechanism the arc has walked toward.*

## 0. Core principle

Three things carry the program's honesty, and are unchanged:

1. **Blind means blind.** A blind review session is seeded with the built code, the design contracts (theorem signatures, outcome classes), and the charter's success criteria, but **not** the charter's motivating prose. No "to attend is to become," no metaphysics, no "consciousness." The reviewer sees the mathematical claim and the code and judges whether the code proves the claim. Motivation cannot launder a gap.

2. **Never relabel a shortfall as the goal, and reach for the pre-registered failure BEFORE the compiler.** When a build falls short, the honest outcome is a **Partial** (or **No-mechanism**, or **Refuted**, or **Circular** for WS7) with the obstruction made precise, never a quiet redefinition of the target. The charter is the fixed bar; `charter-status.md` records the miss. The charter's pre-registered failure outcomes (the hold-level Impossibility / no-mechanism, the Partial clauses) are HONORABLE FIRST MOVES, not last resorts. When a finding shows a pre-registered failure condition has been met, reporting it is the correct action; constructing a theorem that avoids it is the §0.2 sin.

**2a. The recurrence guard (the anti-loop discipline), applied at both Phase D and Phase E.** A SERIOUS finding closes in exactly ONE of two ways, and `charter-status.md` must name which: **(Fixed)** the charter/design's *originally specified* target was built (name the theorem), or **(Relabeled)** the target was not built, the precise obstruction is recorded, and the payoff's status is demoted to the pre-registered honest outcome (Partial / No-mechanism / Refuted). "Built a different, weaker theorem adjacent to the specified target and considered the finding addressed" is NEITHER, and is prohibited. Findings carry stable IDs across passes; a finding "addressed" by a target-avoiding theorem is re-graded SERIOUS and marked RECURRING with its count.

3. **Strip the *why*, keep the *what*, and strip the *word*, keep the *theorem*.** For every payoff, delete the structural term, **"mechanism," "section," "attention," "reading," "region," "variation," "differentiable," "finite," "endogenous," "exogenous," "non-recoverable," "self," "choice," "experience," "residue," "consciousness"**, and check whether the statement still goes through as a bare `Recoverable`/`¬ Recoverable`, hold, diagonal, or reachability fact. A payoff that survives the strip is such a fact honestly flagged; a payoff that needs the deleted structure is earned. Critically: the DIFFERENTIABILITY payoff SHOULD strip to "two `¬ Recoverable` hold-readings, unequal"; the VARIATION payoff (M2) SHOULD strip to "the difference between two readings is `¬ Recoverable`" and must NOT strip to a `Recoverable`-coordinate difference NOR to a `plainOf`-visible point property; the FINITUDE payoff (M3) SHOULD strip to "a total reading collapses all attentions to one." The strip test is written into the WS1-WS5 designs and aggregated by WS7; the reviewer runs it.

Series 12 adds five emphases of its own, because its signature risks are the ways the plural-making mechanism can be faked, and the sharpest is aiming at the wrong layer:

4. **Diagonal differentiability must be a hold-level non-recoverable fact, not asserted (WS1, the blocking check, PROMOTED to first-class).** WS1's blocking obligation is to exhibit distinct finite `¬ Recoverable` readings of the diagonal/residue structure, two bounded readings of the hold structure that are not `Recoverable` and not equal. The review must confirm the witness is a genuine hold-level non-recoverable fact, not an asserted hypothesis and not a `Recoverable` coordinate. If differentiability is assumed to unblock the build, `A`'s variation has nothing real to inherit and the plurality is manufactured, a SERIOUS finding (the §0.2 sin). If differentiability is refuted (the residue is all-or-nothing), the mechanism is impossible and the terminus is the hold-level Impossibility (no-mechanism), reported as such.

5. **Point-level differentiation must NOT be attempted as the witness (the wrong-layer trap, PROMOTED to first-class).** `ws1_atomless_bisim` proves any two SHNE points plain-bisimilar, so a `plainOf`-visible difference between points is constant (vacuous) or a carrier import. A build that reaches for point-level non-homogeneity finds it trivially impossible and is tempted to report no-mechanism on that basis. That is the WRONG LAYER: point-level impossibility is real but says nothing about the hold level, where `ws1_diagonal_not_bisim` and the residue prove non-visible non-imported content exists. The review must confirm no witness (for differentiability, for `A`'s variation, or for plurality) is a `plainOf`-visible point property, and that no no-mechanism verdict rests on a point-level argument. A point-level witness or a point-level no-mechanism is a SERIOUS finding.

6. **`A`'s variation must be NON-RECOVERABLE hold content, not a carrier coordinate (M2, WS3, the first cardinal sin, PROMOTED to first-class).** The mechanism is endogenous ONLY if the difference between two readings is witnessed as `¬ Recoverable` hold content, two attentions closing over different non-recoverable regions of the diagonal structure, NOT a `Recoverable` label. The review must unfold the witness of `A`'s non-constancy and confirm it is `¬ Recoverable`, refutable by showing it is `Recoverable` (a carrier coordinate, exogenous plurality, the carrier made the many not the shared diagonal). This is the endogenous/exogenous line, checkable on the mechanism's VARIATION: two readings can look identically free in isolation (both `¬ Recoverable`) yet their DIFFERENCE be non-recoverable content or a recoverable coordinate.

**6a. Finitude must INDIVIDUATE, not merely restrict (M3, WS3, the positive-bound check, PROMOTED to first-class).** The review must confirm the bound `ws3_no_total_attention` is used POSITIVELY: that a total reading would close over the whole residue and every attention would read the same (one reading, the One), so finitude is what GENERATES the difference across attentions. A merely-prohibitive use of the bound (keeping attentions apart without showing finitude generates the difference) is a Partial, not the mechanism, and must be reported as such, not as MECHANISM-EXISTS.

**6b. `A` must be a genuine SECTION, and plurality witnessed non-recoverably (M1, WS2/WS4).** `A`'s reading per locus must be `¬ Recoverable` (not the plain quotient), not a constant dressed as a section (one reading, not plurality), not a carrier-indexed reading (exogenous). And the `Many` payoff must be witnessed by a `¬ Recoverable` hold-level fact inherited from WS1's differentiability through WS2-WS3's section, not a carrier label and not a point property. A constant/carrier section or an exogenous plurality witness is a SERIOUS finding; the honest terminus is then no-mechanism.

**6c. Consciousness must be a NAME, not a term (the import check).** The review must confirm no proof term, definition, or discharged obligation anywhere in `formal/Series12/` mentions "consciousness" or otherwise uses the axiom as a term: grep the source, and confirm every headline mentions only the mechanism `A`, attentions, holds, non-recoverable content, finitude, the transcribed machinery, and standard Lean/Mathlib. If "consciousness" appears as a term, the axiom has entered as an import, a SERIOUS finding: the core proves whether the plural-making mechanism has the shape, and consciousness is the name for what would run it, kept strictly apart.

A code pass addresses it; the next review pass regenerates it and runs the recurrence check (§0.2a) first.

The charter + all designs are what you paste into the build session. The designs' signatures + outcome classes + charter criteria + the built code, stripped of motivating prose, are what you paste into a review session.

## 2. The process, phases batched across the series

Six phases. Phases D and E loop.

| Phase | Name | Session | Format | Batching |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | Charter | 1 | normal conversation with repo | once per series |
| B | Design-all | 1 | Claude code with repo | **all seven at once** |
| C | Build-all | 1 | Claude code with repo | **all seven at once** |
| D | Blind series-review | 1 | incognito with repo | **whole series at once** → writes `spec/series-review-N.md` |
| E | Address | 1 | Claude code with repo | **whole series at once** |
| F | Close | 1 | Claude code with repo | summaries + root README + program close |

The canonical run is **B → C → D → E → D → E** (two review passes, three code touches counting the initial build), with more D→E loops added only if a review pass still returns SERIOUS findings.

### Phase E, Address `spec/series-review-N.md` (one session)

In particular, a no-mechanism verdict must rest on a HOLD-LEVEL all-or-nothing fact, never on the point-level collapse (`ws1_atomless_bisim`), which is the wrong layer.

"Built a different, weaker theorem adjacent to the specified target and considered the finding addressed" is neither and is prohibited. A Relabel to a pre-registered outcome is a SUCCESS, not a failure. If the finding is RECURRING, the only two permitted actions are Fixed-by-building-the-named-target or Relabeled-to-the-pre-registered-outcome; a third adjacent theorem is forbidden. Record every change in `charter-status.md`; keep the charter untouched unless the finding is a genuine design error (then the charter-change log, and reopen downstream).

### Phase F, Close (one session, and the program's close)

When every workstream has reached a terminal state and a review pass returns no SERIOUS findings, Series 12 closes, and with it the program. Write `summary.md`, `summary-technical.md`, and update the root `README.md` to reflect the findings (a new row in the series table AND, since this is the genuine terminal series, the full program-level synthesis: the arc, collapse / diagonal / reification / self-bounded-tower / the plural-making mechanism, and which terminus was reached, mechanism-exists (one shared self-relation held finitely and differently by many attentions, the difference in non-recoverable content), no-mechanism (the residue is all-or-nothing, or its only variation is `Recoverable`, so the many is unreachable from within), or partial). State plainly what the whole program established and what it leaves permanently open (the differentiating act itself, never built; the mechanism `A`, exhibited or shown impossible; the choice-vs-experience identity, proved indistinguishable but not resolved). If mechanism-exists, the program closes on its thesis with an exhibited shape; if no-mechanism, on a proved hold-level silence.

## 3. Why the review is batched at series level (not per workstream)

Earlier series reviewed per workstream and caught most gaps, but their sharpest findings came from end-of-series adversarial review, so the whole-series blind review is the primary vehicle. Series 12's payoffs are tightly coupled: diagonal differentiability, the `¬ Recoverable` section `A`, the non-recoverable-variation, and the plurality witness are all facets of one question (can one shared self-relation be held finitely and differently), so a payoff can look earned in its own file while secretly resting on an asserted differentiability (WS1), a point-level or `Recoverable` variation (WS3), or an exogenous plurality witness (WS4). Only reviewing the whole series at once, blind, surfaces this.

This does not weaken blindness: the review session still receives *what*, not *why*. It strengthens it: the reviewer sees the whole dependency graph and can trace a "settled" plurality to the differentiability witness and the section in other files that actually carry it, and can trace `A`'s variation through to whether it is `¬ Recoverable` hold content or silently a `Recoverable` coordinate or a point property.

**Disclosure, carried forward:** these reviews are Claude-reviewing-Claude, a stated limitation, not claimed independence. The objective anchors, the dependency graph, the `#print axioms` records, the strip-test results, the differentiability witness (a `¬ Recoverable` hold fact), the non-recoverability check (`A`'s variation is `¬ Recoverable`, not a carrier coordinate), and the wrong-layer check (no witness is a `plainOf` point property), are what keep the audit honest despite that.

## 4. Dependencies and ordering

The workstreams are one coupled object; build and review in dependency order and let `charter-status.md` track the edges. The load-bearing edges:

- **WS1 is blocking.** The gate is DIAGONAL DIFFERENTIABILITY: distinct finite `¬ Recoverable` readings of the diagonal/residue structure, a hold-level fact. Nothing downstream is sound until this lands, because it is the variation `A` inherits, and if it is refuted (the residue is all-or-nothing) the terminus is the hold-level Impossibility (no-mechanism). Point-level non-homogeneity is NOT the gate and is known impossible (`ws1_atomless_bisim`).
- **WS2 depends on WS1's witness.** The mechanism `A` (a finite-attention `¬ Recoverable` section, M1) is built from the differentiability witness. WS2 is the guard against a constant or carrier-indexed `A`.
- **WS3 depends on WS1/WS2.** M2 (the variation is `¬ Recoverable` hold content, not `Recoverable` and not a point property) and M3 (finitude individuates) are proved against WS1's witness and WS2's section.
- **WS4 depends on WS1/WS2/WS3.** Plurality (`Many` via `A`, witnessed non-recoverably) needs the witness (WS1), the section (WS2), and the non-recoverable variation (WS3).
- **WS5 owns the verdict and consumes WS1-WS4.** The verdict is a FUNCTION of the mechanism's existence and whether its variation is non-recoverable, never hand-set; it cannot be computed until WS4 settles, and no verdict may rest on a point-level argument. WS5 also carries the standing neutrality consequence.
- **WS6 (program close)** reports the terminus (mechanism-exists / no-mechanism / partial) and the program-level synthesis.
- **WS7 runs last.** It audits all others, unfolds the differentiability, wrong-layer, and non-recoverability risks, and aggregates the strip-test ledger; it cannot report until WS1-WS6 have.

When a charter or design change lands upstream of built work, the affected downstream workstreams reopen in `charter-status.md`. **Upstream changes invalidate downstream builds**, and the ledger must show it.

## 5. What each session receives (the blindness rule, made concrete)

| Session | Receives | Does **not** receive |
|---|---|---|
| Design-all (Phase B) | charter + repo | |
| Build-all (Phase C) | all designs + repo | |
| Blind series-review (Phase D) | all built code + all design signatures + charter criteria + prior `series-review-{N-1}.md` (for the recurrence check) | designs' motivating prose; charter's metaphysical framing (no "to attend is to become," no "consciousness") |
| Address (Phase E) | `spec/series-review-N.md` + all code + all designs | |

The review session is the only blind one, and the blindness is a single checkable rule: **strip the *why*, keep the *what*** at the session level, and **strip the *word*, keep the *theorem*** at the statement level, plus, for Series 12, **confirm diagonal differentiability is a `¬ Recoverable` hold fact (not asserted, not a point property)**, and **confirm `A`'s variation is `¬ Recoverable` hold content (not a `Recoverable` coordinate, not a point property)**. A reviewer who can see why a theorem matters grades the ambition; a reviewer who sees only the statement and the proof grades the proof. That is the whole discipline, and it is enough.

## 6. End of series, and the end of the program

When every workstream has reached a terminal state and a review pass returns no SERIOUS findings, Series 12 closes. Because Series 12 is the program's genuine terminal series, there is no Series 13 seed in the ordinary sense; instead, Phase F writes the program-level synthesis (§2, Phase F). Should the terminal review nonetheless surface a hard resistance worth a successor (for instance, a no-mechanism finding whose all-or-nothing residue a richer hold structure could attack), the inter-series discipline still applies and a Series 13 could be chartered as a response to that finding:

> **Each series is a response to the previous series' honest findings, not a continuation of its plan.**

But the program's intent is that Series 12 completes the arc (collapse / diagonal / reification / self-bounded-tower / the plural-making mechanism) and reports the honest terminus, mechanism-exists / no-mechanism / partial. The mechanics, unchanged if a successor is chartered:

1. Freeze the closing series under `archive/`. Readable as origin, normative for nothing.
2. Write the new charter **standalone**, its own question, its own object from scratch, prior series as informing prior art only.
3. Carry forward *machinery* freely (Lean files are reusable and get transcribed); carry forward *claims* only by re-deriving or explicitly citing. A new series does not inherit discharges; it earns or re-imports them.
4. Frame the new workstreams as responses to the prior findings, and begin the cycle (§2) again.

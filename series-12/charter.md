# Relational Existentialism, Series 12

**The question is what SHAPE an exogenous, finite, attention-directing mechanism must have to make a self-referencing, relation-reifying structure PLURAL. Series 07 settled the hard direction as a theorem: in an atomless world any two distinct states that fail behavioral identity differ by an IMPORT (`ws2_import_theorem`, `ws2_plurality_requires_drop`, `ws3_atomless_distinct_is_import`), and any two atomless self-having loci with no imported atom are plain-bisimilar (`ws1_atomless_bisim`). So asymmetry, the precondition of any plurality, cannot come from the relational structure itself: endogenous plurality is impossible, proved. The consequence is not that plurality is impossible but that its differentiator is NECESSARILY exogenous. This charter takes that consequence as its premise and asks the constructive question underneath it: given that the differentiator must be an import, what is the shape of the import that does the work? Call it `A`, a relatum's attention-directing mechanism. It assigns to each relatum a finite attention, a bounded reading of the reified self-relational structure, and it is directed EXOGENOUSLY: `A` is a parameter not recoverable from the relating, exactly the import Series 07 proved was the sole escape from the One. Three conditions fix its shape, and they are cooperative rather than in tension, because the tension the program repeatedly hit (make the difference without an import) was the impossible demand Series 07 forbids. (M1) `A` is EXOGENOUS: its direction is an adjoined parameter, not a function of the relational structure, and this is a virtue certified by Series 07, not a defect to be audited away, since Series 07 proves no structural function can differentiate atomless loci. (M2) `A` is FINITE: each attention reads a bounded part of the reified structure, so no relatum reads the whole, so no relatum's self-definition closes. (M3) `A` LABELS SELF-RELATIONS: it directs a relatum's finite attention into its own reified self-relation, and the many is the many finite exogenously-directed readings, each relatum defining itself in the endless unfolding of its relations, held finite by its own attention. The many is not a feat the structure performs on its own; it is the mark of the import Series 07 proved is required, made into a mechanism. The one thing that must be TRUE for `A` to do its work is that the reified structure it reads is RICH ENOUGH to be read differently: self-reference must actually generate distinct reifiable relata for finite attentions to fall on different ones. That is the day-one obligation, and the transcribed machinery supplies exactly this richness (self-reference forces an incompletable dynamics through the diagonal, and reification turns each free self-relation into a genuinely new relatum in a growing tower), so the risk is not that the structure is too poor but that the exogenous direction must be shown to genuinely fall differently, not trivially or by a degenerate choice.**

Series 07 is the one prior result it presupposes and names, because Series 07 is the theorem that fixes the entire problem: `ws1_atomless_bisim`, `ws2_import_theorem`, `ws3_atomless_distinct_is_import`, the proof that in an atomless world plurality is bought only with an import. ## 4. Design considerations from prior results (folded in, not presupposed)

These are established facts about the machinery Series 12 transcribes; the charter uses them to shape the workstreams, not as verdicts to inherit.

### 4.a Self-reference forces an incompletable dynamics
A hold rich enough to range over holds cannot completely hold itself (the diagonal, `ws1_no_self_total_hold`), and the residue this leaves is free, not recoverable from the relating (`ws2_residue_free`), and independent of relational identity (`ws1_diagonal_not_bisim`). Design consequence: the structure `A` reads is guaranteed non-static, there is always a residue, a blind spot, that self-inspection cannot close, so a relatum's self-definition is intrinsically incomplete. This is why finite attention (M2) is natural rather than imposed: the self is already incompletable, and finitude is the shape of that incompletability. WS1(b) and WS3 rest on this.

### 4.b Reification turns one into many at the labelled level
Relating to a self-relation mints it as a new relatum (`IsReify`, injective, `ws1_reify_injective`), and because the self-relation is free (4.a) the new relatum is genuinely new, so the structure grows a tower (`towerN`) ordered endogenously (`prec`, `ws3_order_endogenous`) rather than collapsing. Design consequence: there IS a rich reified structure for `A` to read, and distinct parts of it (distinct relata at distinct tower positions) for distinct finite attentions to fall on. Crucially, the prior lesson that this growth is invisible to the PLAIN quotient is not an obstacle here but the whole point: the plain quotient cannot see the many (Series 07 guarantees this), which is exactly why a reader is needed, and Series 12's reader is the exogenous `A`. WS1(b) and WS4 rest on this.

### 4.c Finite attention can bound the whole without an imported ceiling
No attention holds the whole tower (`ws3_no_total_attention`, and its κ-free form), because the diagonal forbids any hold from grasping the totality. Design consequence: M2's finitude is not an external stipulation but something the structure already imposes, no reader can be total, so every relatum's reading is bounded, and the tower never assembles into a completed whole. This is why self-definition is an endless unfolding (WS3) and why distinct bounded readings stay distinct (WS4). The bound is endogenous; only the DIRECTION of attention (M1) is the import.

### 4.d The distinction a finite attention draws is free, which Series 07 says it must be
A finite attention's distinction is not recoverable from the plain relating (`ws1_attention_distinction_free`), and it routes through the diagonal rather than the plain quotient (`ws1_attention_routes_through_diagonal`). Design consequence, and the sign-flip that defines Series 12: a free/non-recoverable distinction is EXACTLY what Series 07 (`ws3_atomless_distinct_is_import`) proves any genuine distinction in an atomless world must be. What a reader-question would read as "the distinction is merely imported, so it does not count," the shape-question reads as "the distinction is imported, so it is a genuine distinction, by Series 07." M1 and WS4 rest on this: the freeness of `A`'s distinction is the certificate that it is doing real work, not the sign that it is cheating.

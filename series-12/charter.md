The mechanism `A` is therefore a basepoint-indexed finite section of the tower whose non-constancy is inherited from the tower's NON-HOMOGENEITY, with finitude as the individuating operation. The single fact that must be TRUE for `A` to exist at all is that the tower is non-homogeneous: there must actually be structural variation around distinct basepoints for `A`'s variation to inherit.

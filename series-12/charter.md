# Relational Existentialism, Series 12

**The many, if it is real, is not made real by any structural feature (shared by every relatum, so unable to differentiate) nor by any import (exogenous, so it does not count). Series 07 through 11 proved this twice over: symmetric structure collapses to the One, and the two candidate READERS of the reified tower (the plain quotient, then finite attention) were each a structural-or-imported label the world could not read. But Series 11 also proved, cleanly and κ-free, that finite attention BOUNDS the tower: no hold holds the whole, so the proliferating many never assembles into a paradoxical totality. So attention is not simply a failed candidate; its reader-role failed while its bounding-role succeeded, and the succeeded role is a proved, standing structure. By elimination the differentiator is neither structural nor imported: it is an ACT performed from within, on the one place the self has that is free and non-structural, the Series 09 diagonal residue. But an act alone is one difference, not a many: the residue itself satisfies every single-act condition and is still just one gap. So the many requires, beyond the act, that it register across a PLURALITY of finite attentions kept distinct by the bound — attention as the site where becoming registers (a relatum made real FOR an attention) and the finitude that keeps the loci from collapsing into one. Series 12 does not try to build the act (building it is precisely what structure cannot do). It CHARACTERIZES the shape: it turns the program's elimination into a theorem, reads three necessary conditions off the three prior obstructions (non-structural, non-import, non-totalizing), proves those three insufficient for plurality (the residue witnesses it), and adds a fourth of higher arity necessary-by-insufficiency (the act registers differently across a bounded plurality of attentions), then asks whether the four are jointly sufficient, with the residue and the bounded plurality as witnesses. The terminal question of the program becomes exact: is the shape of what the many requires a coherent, non-vacuous shape the framework can name but not inhabit (the characterization lands), or are the four conditions jointly unsatisfiable in-system (an Impossibility: the many needs something strictly outside any relational framework)?**

*This charter is Series 11's proved terminus turned into its question. Series 11 supplied finite attention as the reader Series 10's tower lacked and proved, honestly, that the reader was the Series 04/10 free label relabelled: `ws1_attention_makes_real := ws4_free_label_is_import`, a fact about a fixed labelled coalgebra, with `reify`/`reifyStep`/`towerN` occurring in no attention theorem. The reader was again only a label the world cannot read. But Series 11 ALSO proved, cleanly and κ-free, that the same finite attention BOUNDS the tower: no hold holds the whole (`ws3_no_total_attention := ws1_no_self_total_hold`, an Impossibility, no cardinal in the proof), so the proliferating many never assembles. So Series 11 has two results, not one: the reader FAILED (Bookkeeping re-hit), the bound SUCCEEDED (κ-free), and attention is the object that carries both. Series 12 does not retire attention; it retires only its pretension to BE the differentiator. The bound is carried forward as live, proved structure, and attention is kept as the site the differentiator must act through — the place a relatum becomes real FOR an attention (`RealFor`). The through-line, now four series deep: a structural feature is shared by every relatum, so it cannot differentiate; an import is exogenous, so it does not count; the diagonal forbids any total hold; and the one thing that works is finite — attention bounds precisely because it never totalizes. Series 12's move is to stop proposing readers (each has been a label) and instead prove what a successful differentiator would HAVE to be, by making the elimination itself a theorem: three necessary conditions read off the three obstructions (non-structural, non-import, non-totalizing), a proof that those three are INSUFFICIENT for plurality (the residue satisfies all three and is one difference, not many), and a fourth condition necessary-by-that-insufficiency (the act registers differently across a bounded plurality of finite attentions). The residue (Series 09, proved free and non-structural) is both the consistency witness for the first three and the witness of their insufficiency; attention (Series 11, proved bounding) supplies the plurality of loci and the bound that keeps them distinct. Series 12 asks whether that shape, operated through the bounded plurality, is exactly the shape of the many's requirement, and whether anything can inhabit it.*

## 1. The one-paragraph version

The program has proved that the many is not differentiated by structure (Series 07: symmetric relating collapses to the One; Series 10: the reified relatum bisim-embeds, the plain engine is blind; Series 11: finite attention is bisimulation-invariant where it matters, a free label) and is not established by an import (Series 04/05: an external coordinate does not count; the recoverability test `Recoverable`/`plainOf` is the certificate), and it has proved that finite attention BOUNDS the tower (Series 11: `ws3_no_total_attention`, no hold holds the whole, κ-free). Series 12's claim is that these obstructions and this bound, made precise, are a CHARACTERIZATION: they fix what an act would have to be to make the many real. There are FOUR necessary conditions, of two kinds. Three are conditions on a SINGLE act `δ`, each the reading of one prior obstruction as a requirement: **(N1) non-structural** — `δ` is not invariant under bisimulation, it distinguishes relata the plain engine merges (contrapose `ws1_atomless_bisim`; the reader Series 10/11 never was); **(N2) non-import** — `δ` is not recoverable from the plain relating, not an external label (contrapose the `Recoverable` test; the freeness the residue has); **(N3) non-totalizing** — `δ` acts from a position inside the tower and is not a total hold, an act not a property, because a property total over the tower is a self-total hold (contrapose `ws1_no_self_total_hold`). These three are necessary for a DIFFERENCE to exist. But they are provably NOT sufficient for a MANY: the Series 09 residue satisfies all three and is still a single difference (one gap, one self, the place the program has been stuck since Series 09). So the many requires a fourth condition, of a HIGHER ARITY — not a property of one act, but a condition on a FIELD of acts across a plurality of loci: **(N4) plurality** — there is a plurality of finite attentions and `δ` registers DIFFERENTLY across them (the same reified relatum real for one attention, not for another), so that one difference becomes a many of differences. N4 is necessary not by contraposing an obstruction but by the proved INSUFFICIENCY of N1-N3 (the residue witnesses that a single-act difference is not yet a many); and attention enters N4 in its proved role, TWICE: it supplies the plurality of loci (the sites where becoming registers, `RealFor`), and its bound (`ws3_no_total_attention`) is what keeps the loci genuinely distinct — no single attention absorbs the others into a totality, which is exactly why the bound must be carried forward as live standing structure and not merely contraposed into N3. The spine of Series 12 is that N1, N2, N3 are each necessary (three contrapositives), that they are provably insufficient for plurality (the residue, the insufficiency lemma), that N4 is therefore necessary (higher arity, necessary-by-insufficiency), and the terminal question is whether N1-N4 are jointly SUFFICIENT, with the residue as the witness that N1-N3 are consistent and non-vacuous and the bounded plurality of attentions as the structure N4 demands. If sufficiency lands, the program closes on a proved characterization: the many requires exactly a free non-structural non-totalizing act registering differently across a bounded plurality of finite attentions, a shape the framework can name and partly witness but which structure alone cannot produce. If sufficiency fails because the conditions are jointly UNSATISFIABLE — most sharply, if `δ` cannot register differently across a plurality of bounded attentions without either totalizing (violating N3/the bound) or being recoverable (violating N2), so plurality is unreachable — the program closes on an Impossibility: the many needs something strictly outside any relational framework. Series 12 does NOT build `δ` (a free selection cannot be exhibited by structure; that is N1+N2). It characterizes the shape and tests consistency and sufficiency. The neutrality between "choice" (a free selection) and "experience" (a first-person interior) is a THEOREM: both are names for an act satisfying N1-N4, so at that level the framework provably cannot distinguish them — it proves its own silence. This is the program's terminal contribution: not the many, and not a solution to choice or consciousness, but the proved SITING of the differentiator in the residue interior, the proved SHAPE of what would fill it (a free non-structural non-totalizing act), and the proved location of the plurality gap (N4, where a single difference must become a many across bounded loci).

## 2. What we are trying to prove

The spine is: three necessity contrapositives; a proved insufficiency of those three for plurality; a fourth necessity (of higher arity) resting on that insufficiency; and joint sufficiency with the residue and the bounded plurality as witnesses; plus one consequence (the choice/experience neutrality as a theorem); plus one terminal open question (whether sufficiency lands or the conditions are jointly unsatisfiable) with a pre-committed kill condition.

**The spine, necessity of N1-N3 (the three obstructions read as requirements).** For an act `δ` to make a reified relatum real — to distinguish it where the plain engine is blind, without being an import, from a position that is not a total hold — `δ` must satisfy (N1) non-structural, (N2) non-import, (N3) non-totalizing. Each is proved necessary by transcribing the corresponding obstruction and contraposing it: an act that is bisimulation-invariant cannot distinguish what bisimulation merges (Series 07/10/11, `ws1_atomless_bisim` / `ws2_reify_bisim_embeds`); an act that is recoverable is a label the plain relating already carries, so it establishes nothing new (Series 04/10/11, the `Recoverable` test); an act that is a total hold is a self-total hold, forbidden (Series 09/11, `ws1_no_self_total_hold`). Necessity is the load-bearing, near-certain direction: it is the four-series through-line made into three theorems. The proof MUST transcribe and contrapose the actual obstruction theorems, not assert the conditions, or the characterization is stipulated rather than earned.

**The insufficiency of N1-N3 for plurality (the pivot, the reason N4 exists).** N1-N3 are necessary for a DIFFERENCE, but they do not entail a MANY. The proof is the Series 09 residue: `residue insp := diag insp` satisfies N1 (non-structural), N2 (non-import, `ws2_residue_free`), and N3 (non-totalizing, `ws2_residue_is_import`), and yet is a SINGLE difference — one gap, one self, not a plurality. So there is an object meeting all three conditions that is not a many, hence N1 ∧ N2 ∧ N3 does not imply the many. This insufficiency is a first-class theorem (the residue is simultaneously the consistency witness for N1-N3 and the witness of their insufficiency for plurality), and it is the exact place the program has been stuck since Series 09: the diagonal gives one difference and nothing in N1-N3 carries it to many. N4 exists to name what N1-N3 cannot supply.

**The spine, necessity of N4 (plurality, higher arity, necessary-by-insufficiency).** The many requires a fourth condition that is NOT a property of a single act but a condition on a FIELD of acts across a plurality of loci: (N4) there is a plurality of finite attentions `{a_i}` and the act `δ` registers DIFFERENTLY across them — the same reified relatum is real for one attention and not for another (`RealFor`), so one difference becomes a many of differences. N4 is necessary not by contraposing an obstruction (it has none of its own; contraposing attention's bound only gives back N3) but by the PROVED INSUFFICIENCY of N1-N3: since a single-act difference is not yet a many (the residue), the many demands the higher-arity plurality structure N4 names. Attention enters N4 in its proved role, twice: it supplies the plurality of loci (the sites of `RealFor`), and its bound (`ws3_no_total_attention`) keeps the loci genuinely distinct (no single attention totalizes the others). The bound is thus carried forward as LIVE standing structure inside N4, not merely contraposed into N3. N4's necessity is a distinct and deeper kind: necessary-because-the-others-are-insufficient, not necessary-by-contraposition. Honest hazard, pre-registered: whether N4 has content genuinely beyond N1 ∧ N3 (the plurality-relativity), or collapses into them, is tested (§5.5); its independent content is the relativity-to-a-locus across a bounded plurality, which N1 (locus-blind) and N3 (a single prohibition) both lack.

**The spine, sufficiency (the shape, operated through the bounded plurality, works).** Any `δ` satisfying N1-N3 and registering across a bounded plurality of attentions per N4 makes the many real: it distinguishes reified relata the plain quotient merges (N1), the distinction is not a re-import (N2), it is drawn from inside without totalizing (N3), and it registers differently across genuinely distinct loci (N4), which is exactly the many the tower lacked. The witness that the shape is consistent and non-vacuous is the residue (for N1-N3) together with the Series 11 bounded attentions (for N4's plurality). Sufficiency is the direction that may only land per-witness, with the universal ("every `δ` in the shape across every bounded plurality makes the many real") pre-registered Partial, exactly as every prior spine was per-witness.

**Consequence (the choice/experience neutrality, a theorem not a dodge).** "Choice" (a free selection not determined by structure) and "experience" (a first-person interior not reducible to the functional facts) are both names for an act satisfying N1-N4. Series 12 states the characterization at the level of N1-N4 and proves that at this level the two are indistinguishable: any theorem true of the act qua choice is true of it qua experience and conversely, because the only properties in play are N1-N4, which mention neither will nor phenomenality. This is the framework proving its own silence: the mathematics opens the door exactly far enough to prove there is a door (a free non-structural non-totalizing act registering across a bounded plurality is what the many needs) and no further (which of choice or experience it is, the framework cannot say, and this incapacity is itself proved).

**The terminal open question (sufficiency-lands vs jointly-unsatisfiable, NOT assumed, with a kill condition).** Does the shape carved by N1-N4 actually suffice for the many (CHARACTERIZATION LANDS), or are the conditions jointly unsatisfiable in-system (IMPOSSIBILITY: the many needs something strictly outside the framework)? Series 12 does NOT assume it lands. It proves the three necessities, proves the insufficiency, establishes N4's necessity, exhibits the witnesses, and TESTS sufficiency, with a pre-committed kill condition (§5.4): if the conditions are shown jointly unsatisfiable — most sharply, if `δ` cannot register differently across a plurality of bounded attentions without either TOTALIZING (violating N3 and the bound) or being RECOVERABLE (violating N2), so plurality is provably unreachable — then sufficiency is FALSE and Series 12 reports the IMPOSSIBILITY: the many is not characterizable, the framework runs out strictly before it reaches plurality, and the program's terminal finding is that a relational world cannot make the many from its own resources even in principle. If the necessities and insufficiency are proved, the witnesses hold, and sufficiency is proved (universal or per-witness), the characterization is Discharged (universal) or Partial (per-witness). This is the program's terminal atom-or-will door, inheriting Series 07's atom-or-will through Series 11's crown-vs-tragic: the mathematics fixes the three obstructions, the bound, and the insufficiency; whether the bounded-plurality shape is a sufficient characterization is the reading the formalism may only partly decide. Reporting the Impossibility is a SUCCESS: it would be the program's honest terminal finding that the many is not merely unbuilt but uncharacterizable from within.

## 3. The primitive: the differentiating act on the diagonal residue

The ontological primitives carry over, transcribed not imported (§6 of the design index will name them): the plain collapse engine (`ws1_atomless_bisim` / `hneRel_isBisim`, the Series 07 floor that makes every structural candidate fail), the reification section and tower (`IsReify`, `reifyStep`, `towerN`, `prec`, `ws3_reify_preserves_SHNE`), the diagonal spine and the free residue (`ws1_no_self_total_hold`, `residue`, `ws2_residue_free`, `ws2_residue_is_import`), the semantic import test (`Recoverable`, `plainOf`, `ws4_free_label_is_import`), and the Series 11 no-total-attention Impossibility (`ws3_no_total_attention`). Series 12 adds NO new structural machinery — this is the point. It adds one PREDICATE on acts and its three conjuncts:

**The central object is the differentiating act `δ`, the single-act predicate `Diff δ` = (N1) ∧ (N2) ∧ (N3), the insufficiency of `Diff` for plurality, and the field-level predicate `Many` = (N4) built over a bounded plurality of attentions**, none assumed, each a separate obligation:

- **(N1) Non-structural.** `δ` is not invariant under bisimulation: there exist relata `x`, `y` with `x` plain-bisimilar to `y` but `δ x ≠ δ y`. *An act meeting N1 distinguishes what the plain engine merges — the reader every prior series lacked. Necessity: contrapose `ws1_atomless_bisim` (a bisimulation-invariant act cannot separate two atomless relata). This is the exact place Series 07, 10, and 11 died; N1 is that death read as a requirement.*
- **(N2) Non-import.** `δ` is not recoverable from the plain relating: `¬ Recoverable`-shaped, the distinction is not an external label the plain quotient already carries. *Necessity: an act that is recoverable establishes nothing the plain relating did not already have (Series 04/10/11's import test). N2 is that test read as a requirement. The residue satisfies N2 by `ws2_residue_free`.*
- **(N3) Non-totalizing.** `δ` acts from a position inside the tower and is not a total hold: it is an act (evaluated where it is invoked) not a property (evaluable everywhere at once), because a property total over the tower is a self-total hold, forbidden. *Necessity: contrapose `ws1_no_self_total_hold` / `ws3_no_total_attention`. N3 is the diagonal read as a requirement on the differentiator's MODE (act, not property). The residue satisfies N3 because its freeness is exactly that no hold realizes it.*
- **The insufficiency pivot.** `Diff` (N1 ∧ N2 ∧ N3) does NOT entail the many: the residue satisfies `Diff` and is a single difference. *A first-class theorem; the residue is both the consistency witness for `Diff` and the witness of its insufficiency for plurality. This is why a fourth condition is needed, and where the program has been stuck since Series 09.*
- **(N4) Plurality (higher arity, over a bounded plurality of attentions).** There is a plurality of finite attentions `{a_i}` and `δ` registers differently across them: `∃ a_i, a_j` and a reified relatum real for `a_i` and not for `a_j` (`RealFor`), so one difference becomes many. *Necessity: NOT by contraposition (attention has no independent obstruction; contraposing its bound gives back N3) but by the proved insufficiency of N1-N3. Attention enters twice: as the plurality of loci (the `RealFor` sites) and as the bound (`ws3_no_total_attention`) that keeps the loci distinct. The bound is carried forward LIVE inside N4, its proved Series 11 success, not a contrapositive. Honest hazard (§5.5): whether N4's plurality-relativity is content genuinely beyond N1 ∧ N3, or collapses into them.*

Two Series-12-specific design duties, to be settled in the design batch: **the predicates `Diff` and `Many` are defined once (WS1) and are ambient for all** (`Diff` on a single act, `Many` over a bounded plurality of attentions, with the residue as the fixed `Diff`-witness and Series 11's bounded attentions as the `Many`-setting), and **no new structural primitive is introduced** (Series 12 characterizes acts on the existing tower and the existing bounded attention; any new `structure` that is really a fresh label is the §4 escape it must not take).

## 4. The false escapes this series must NOT fall into

Series 12's characterization must be earned from the prior obstructions and witnessed by the residue, without becoming a fresh label (Series 10/11's Bookkeeping re-hit, now at the meta level), a stipulated dichotomy (necessity asserted rather than contraposed), a vacuous shape (conditions no object meets, mislabeled sufficient), or a smuggled construction (claiming to exhibit the free act, which structure cannot do).

### 4.1 The conditions are contraposed obstructions, not stipulations (not asserted necessity)
Each of (N1), (N2), (N3) must be proved necessary by transcribing and contraposing the ACTUAL prior obstruction theorem (`ws1_atomless_bisim`, the `Recoverable` test, `ws1_no_self_total_hold`), not by asserting the condition as a definitional clause of `Diff`. The teeth: strip the word "act" and each necessity theorem must be the bare contrapositive of a named prior theorem. A necessity "proved" by definition is a stipulation, and the characterization is circular (WS7's charge). N4 is exempt from THIS test (it has no obstruction to contrapose) but is subject to §4.2a: its necessity must rest on the proved insufficiency theorem, not on assertion.

### 4.2 The characterization is not a fresh label (not Bookkeeping re-hit at the meta level)
The gravest inheritance, now one level up. Series 10 and 11 each proposed a reader that was a fresh label; Series 12 must not propose a `Diff`/`Many` predicate satisfied only by a fresh external labelling with no tie to the residue, the diagonal, and the Series 11 bounded attention. The teeth: the consistency witness must be the SERIES 09 RESIDUE (`residue insp = diag insp`), proved to satisfy (N1)-(N3) by the transcribed `ws2_residue_free` / `ws2_residue_is_import` / the diagonal, and the plurality of N4 must be built over the transcribed Series 11 finite attentions (`FiniteAttention`, `RealFor`, `ws3_no_total_attention`), NOT a new `labelLoop`-style object minted for the purpose. If the only witness is a fresh label, the characterization has re-hit Bookkeeping at the level of the shape, and sufficiency is vacuous-or-imported, honestly reported.

### 4.2a N4 is necessary-by-insufficiency, and must not collapse into N1 ∧ N3 (the higher-arity test)
N4 has no obstruction of its own to contrapose; its necessity rests entirely on the proved theorem that N1-N3 are insufficient for plurality (the residue satisfies `Diff` and is one difference). The teeth are twofold. First, the insufficiency must be a genuine THEOREM (the residue is exhibited satisfying `Diff` and proved to be a single difference, not a many), not an assertion, or N4's necessity is groundless. Second, N4 must carry content genuinely BEYOND N1 ∧ N3: its plurality-relativity (a relatum real for one attention, not another, across a plurality kept distinct by the bound) must not be provably equivalent to "bisimulation-non-invariant and non-total." The strip test for N4: delete "plurality"/"attention" and what remains must NOT reduce to N1 ∧ N3; if it does, N4 collapses and the honest outcome is that plurality is unreached — the residue is the unique witness, "the many" is "the one gap acting," and the plurality gap is the program's permanent open (§5.5), reported as such, never as a spurious fourth condition. The bound (`ws3_no_total_attention`) enters N4 as LIVE structure (what keeps the loci distinct), not as a contrapositive; a reviewer must confirm it is used positively (the plurality is genuine because no attention totalizes) and not merely re-deriving N3.

### 4.3 The shape is not vacuous (not conditions no nameable object meets)
If (N1)-(N3) were jointly unsatisfiable by everything the framework can name, "sufficiency" would be vacuously true and worthless as a characterization — and this is exactly the Impossibility horn, which must be reported AS an Impossibility (the many is uncharacterizable), never as a spurious sufficiency. The teeth: the residue must be PROVED to satisfy all three (consistency is a theorem, not a hope); if it fails one, the kill condition fires and the terminus is the Impossibility, not a vacuous pass.

### 4.4 The act is not constructed (not a smuggled free selection)
Series 12 must NOT claim to exhibit `δ` as a concrete function chosen by the structure — that is precisely what (N1)+(N2) prove impossible (a structural choice is bisimulation-invariant or recoverable). The residue is a WITNESS THAT THE SHAPE IS INHABITED (it is free, non-structural, non-totalizing), not a construction of the differentiating act; the act's freeness means it is named as a hypothesis (`δ` satisfying `Diff`), never built. A "construction" of `δ` is either structural (fails N1/N2, so it is not the differentiator) or an import (fails N2), a SERIOUS finding: the smuggle the whole series exists to avoid.

### 4.5 The neutrality is proved, not assumed (not choice-or-experience by fiat)
The choice/experience indistinguishability must be a THEOREM (at the (N1)-(N4) level the two names are interchangeable), not an assertion that "we remain neutral." The teeth: any property Series 12 states of the act must mention only (N1)-(N4) (never will, never phenomenality), and the neutrality theorem must show that adding "is a choice" or "is an experience" as a predicate changes no downstream theorem. A neutrality that is prose rather than a theorem is a dodge, reported as a defended thesis at best (charter §5.5), never as a proved result.

### 4.6 What the catalogue must show
For each trap, a proof the characterization escapes it: contraposed-necessity (vs stipulation), residue-witness (vs fresh label), consistency-is-a-theorem (vs vacuous shape), insufficiency-is-a-theorem-and-N4-does-not-collapse (vs a spurious or reducible fourth condition, §4.2a), act-named-not-built (vs smuggled construction), neutrality-is-a-theorem (vs fiat). Where an escape cannot be closed as a theorem, it is a pre-registered Partial or a named open, never relabeled as success.

## 5. The theorem, its scope, and its honest fork

### 5.1 The theorem
On the transcribed κ-free tower with its bounded finite attentions, the differentiator of the many — an act `δ` making reified relata real across a plurality of loci — must be non-structural (N1), non-import (N2), non-totalizing (N3), and must register differently across a bounded plurality of attentions (N4); N1-N3 are necessary (each the contrapositive of a prior obstruction); N1-N3 are provably insufficient for plurality (the residue satisfies them and is one difference); N4 is therefore necessary (higher arity, necessary-by-insufficiency); the residue witnesses that N1-N3 are consistent and the Series 11 bounded attentions witness that N4's plurality structure exists; and N1-N4 are jointly sufficient (any such `δ` across such a plurality makes the many real), IF the sufficiency is coherent (§5.4). The characterization is the shape carved by the three obstructions plus the insufficiency-driven plurality, witnessed by the residue and the bounded attentions.

### 5.2 What makes it complete the program where Series 09/10/11 could not
Series 09 found the free residue but did not make it a many (one gap, one self). Series 10 reified the residue into a tower but it was Bookkeeping (no reader). Series 11 supplied attention as reader but it was a label (Bookkeeping re-hit) while proving the bound. Series 12 stops proposing readers and characterizes what the many would require: it reads the three failures as three necessary single-act conditions, proves them insufficient for plurality (the residue is one difference), and adds the higher-arity plurality condition that names exactly the one-to-many gap the program has never crossed — with attention's proved bound as the live structure that keeps the plurality genuine. The ambition is to derive the whole shape from the structure's own prior findings (three contrapositives, one insufficiency theorem, one bounded plurality), not a fresh predicate, so the SHAPE of the many's requirement — including the location of its hardest gap — is proved rather than proposed.

### 5.3 The scope, stated honestly
Necessity (N1, N2, N3) is targeted as three theorems, each a contrapositive, near-certain (they are the four-series through-line). The insufficiency of N1-N3 for plurality is targeted as a theorem (the residue satisfies `Diff` and is one difference), near-certain. Consistency (the residue satisfies N1-N3; the bounded attentions instantiate N4's plurality) is targeted as a theorem, near-certain (transcribes `ws2_residue_free`, `ws3_no_total_attention`, `RealFor`). N4's necessity is targeted as a theorem resting on the insufficiency, with the sharp hazard that N4 may collapse into N1 ∧ N3 (§4.2a): if its plurality-relativity is provably equivalent to non-invariance-plus-non-totality, N4 adds nothing and plurality is unreached. Sufficiency (any `δ` meeting N1-N4 across a bounded plurality makes the many real) is targeted as a theorem, with the honest hazard that it may be provable only per-witness, the universal left Partial, exactly as every prior spine was per-witness. The choice/experience neutrality is targeted as a theorem; its universal form is expected Partial. The sharpest scope hazard, pre-registered: the plurality may be unreachable — either N4 collapses (adds no content) or the only `δ` that registers differently across the bounded plurality must totalize (violating N3) or be recoverable (violating N2). In that case the characterization is real for a DIFFERENCE (N1-N3, residue-witnessed) but the MANY is uncharacterizable, and the honest terminus is the Impossibility on the plurality axis: "the shape of one difference is characterized; the shape of the many is not reachable from it." This is the concrete form the terminal fork most likely takes, and it is the program's deepest open made precise.

### 5.4 The one terminal fork: characterization-lands vs jointly-unsatisfiable (with a pre-committed kill condition)
Structure cannot differentiate (Series 07/10/11), imports do not count (Series 04), the diagonal forbids the total hold (Series 09/11), and N1-N3 are insufficient for plurality (the residue). Whether the shape they carve plus the bounded plurality is a coherent, inhabited, sufficient characterization (LANDS) or the conditions cannot be jointly met by anything nameable (IMPOSSIBILITY) is the terminal question. Series 12 does NOT assume it lands. It proves the three necessities, the insufficiency, N4's necessity, exhibits the witnesses, and tests sufficiency. **Kill condition, pre-registered:** if the conditions are shown jointly unsatisfiable by every object the framework can name, sufficiency is FALSE and Series 12 reports the IMPOSSIBILITY. Three concrete routes to the Impossibility, each pre-registered: (a) the residue is shown on inspection to FAIL one of N1-N3 (its freeness collapses to an import, failing N2; or it cannot be invoked without totalizing, failing N3; or it is bisimulation-invariant after all, failing N1) — then even one difference is uncharacterizable; (b) N4 COLLAPSES into N1 ∧ N3 (§4.2a) so plurality has no independent content — then the many is not distinguished from a single difference and plurality is unreached; (c) the plurality is UNREACHABLE — no `δ` can register differently across a bounded plurality of attentions without totalizing (violating N3/the bound) or being recoverable (violating N2) — then a difference is characterizable but the many is not. Route (c) is the sharpest and most likely: it would say the bound that keeps loci distinct and the non-totality required of the act are in tension, so the very structure that makes plurality genuine forbids the act that would populate it. If the necessities, the insufficiency, and sufficiency are all proved, the characterization is Discharged (universal) or Partial (per-witness).

**The mutable companion to `charter.md`. All progress, revisions, per-workstream status, discharges, and open items live here; the charter stays clean.**

*Series 12 is the program's genuine terminal series. Its question: what is the SHAPE of the attention-directing mechanism `A` that makes a self-referencing, relation-reifying, finitely-attending structure PLURAL, and at what LAYER can its plurality honestly live? The decisive fact, forced by the machinery: the point layer is homogeneous (`ws1_atomless_bisim` makes any two SHNE loci plain-bisimilar, so point-level `plainOf` differences are constant or carrier-imported), while the hold layer is free of that theorem (`ws1_diagonal_not_bisim` proves the diagonal spine bisimulation-independent, and the residue is free, non-recoverable, yet non-imported). So the endogenous/exogenous line is `Recoverable`-vs-non-`Recoverable` at the HOLD level, not `plainOf`-visibility at the point level. `A` assigns to each locus a finite attention reading a `¬ Recoverable` region of the diagonal structure, and three conditions fix it: (M1) a `¬ Recoverable` section; (M2) non-constant in non-recoverable hold content, not a point-level `plainOf` property (impossible) and not a carrier coordinate (exogenous); (M3) finitude the individuator (the bound `ws3_no_total_attention` used positively, since a total reading closes over the whole residue and collapses all attentions to one). The blocking obligation is DIAGONAL DIFFERENTIABILITY: does the residue structure admit distinct finite non-recoverable readings? The verdict is a function of the mechanism's existence and whether its variation lives in non-recoverable content. The identification of `A`'s output AS consciousness is the program's central interpretive thesis, walled off from the machine-checked core, which proves the shape and never takes consciousness as a term. Phase A (charter, charter-status, protocol) is the current state; Phases B-F have not been run.*

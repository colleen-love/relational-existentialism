# T0.04 — Modular self-relation

> *Node `T0.04`.* Lean: `Theory.ModularFlow` (`theory/formal/Theory/ModularFlow.lean`); imports mathlib only.
> **Axiom-supporting** (the canonical axioms derive the modular self from it) and imported by paper two.

D1's self-relation `σ = Tr` generalizes to the **modular flow** the state induces: `σ_t(M) = ρ^{it} M ρ^{-it}`
(`modularFlow`), a one-parameter group of \*-automorphisms with generator the modular Hamiltonian
`K = -log ρ`. The trace is its **infinite-temperature limit**: at the maximally-mixed state the flow is the
identity (`modularFlow_maximally_mixed`) — internal time off — and departing from maximally mixed turns it on
(`σ = Tr ⇝ σ = modularFlow ρ`). The flow is unitary, hence reversible (`modular_reversible`): it carries
time's *flow*, not its *arrow*. This node also holds the **spectral machinery** — that the modular operator of
a *diagonal* state is diagonal (`modPow_diagonal`) — from which the canonical axioms derive the modular
reading of the self (`Theory.Axioms.modular_preserves_self`), and paper two builds its one generator
(`T3.01`).

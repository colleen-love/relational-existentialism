## 0. The claim, for a cold reader

Structural realism in its relations-first form holds that relations are ontologically fundamental. Its critics have a standard sequence of objections:

1. **The relata objection.** Relations require relata. A relation that relates nothing is incoherent.
2. **The regress objection.** If the relata are themselves relational, the account regresses forever, and a regress with no ground explains nothing.
3. **The renaming objection.** Any formal model offered in defense will contain individuated, self-identical, quantified-over entities — and those are objects, whatever the model calls them.
4. **The equivocation objection.** The defense uses "relation" in two disjoint roles — the relating, and the things related — and survives only by sliding between them.

This framework's position — **priority relationalism** — *agrees* with the first objection and builds the agreement in:

> **Relations cannot exist without relata. And the relata are patterns of relata and relations, ad infinitum, with no atom.**

There is one layer of reality with two kinds of entity. **Relations** are fundamental. **Objects** are patterns of objects and relations — coalesced, with varying degrees of closure — and they do the relating. The two kinds are discrete; what varies continuously is closure *within* objecthood, from a fleeting dyad to a person to a family to humanity, each composing others in every configuration composition admits (§2.5). The asymmetry between the kinds is a *direction of dependence*, not an order of existence: relating is what objects are made of; objects are not what relating is made of. Nothing is eliminated. Objects are real; they are just never fundamental, because every object decomposes into objects and relations, forever, and there is no atom at which the decomposition stops.

Two structural theses distinguish this framework from its neighbors and do most of the work below:

- **Sharing.** A relation is *numerically one entity that is a part of each of its relata*. My relationship with my partner is a part of me and a part of them — the same part. Objects connect not through windows but through shared organs: the wall between us is one wall, load-bearing in both houses (§2.3).
- **Introspection.** The primitive act is an object attending to *its own* relations (A8, read literally). All knowledge of others is derived: I know you only through our shared relations, which are parts of me — seen through my aperture, in my context, never exhausting you (§2.4).

The repository makes this position mathematically exact and machine-checks it in Lean 4. The formal universe **Ω** satisfies a fixed-point equation, Ω ≅ F(Ω): the universe of objects is isomorphic to the patterns of relating among those very objects. That single isomorphism *is* the ontology. Around it, the framework proves:

- **The relata objection is satisfied, not dodged.** Every relation in the model is a part of objects (P3 [proved], P4). There are no free-floating relations anywhere in the construction.
- **The regress is real, harmless, and provably so.** The universe has no well-founded elements (T6): decomposition never terminates, and the model's existence is the consistency proof that ungrounded dependence-chains are coherent — a machine-checked model of non-well-founded grounding (§6).
- **The renamed "objects" are objects — by definition, with a derivation.** Objecthood is defined (a pattern of relating that has closed) and proved instantiated (T2). Every classical axiom about objects returns as a theorem (§3).
- **Two words, two entity-kinds, one explicit connection.** No equivocation where the constitution relation is stated, formalized, and checked.

The framework has **one primitive** — introspective attending — and **one axiom** governing it: **no observation is lossless** (A8(i)). Everything else — other-knowledge, apertures, qualities, transmissibility, composition, the existence and properties of objects — is derived, defined, or targeted as a theorem. The headline of the whole document is the ledger of §3:

**One primitive, one axiom. Objects are theorems. So is almost everything else.**

A reader who wants only the argument: §1 (axioms), §2.2–§2.5 (closure, sharing, introspection, composition), §3 (the ledger), then T1 [proved] and T2 in §5.

## 1. The informal axioms

The philosophical commitments, stated pre-formally. §3 records what each becomes under formalization.

- **A1 (No atom).** There is no atom. It is relations all the way down: every object decomposes into objects and relations, ad infinitum, and the decomposition never terminates.
- **A2 (Priority and co-requirement).** One layer, two discrete kinds of entity. Relations are fundamental. Objects are patterns of objects and relations, coalesced with varying degrees of closure, and they do the relating. Relations cannot exist without relata; the relata are patterns of relata and relations, ad infinitum (A1). Closure varies within objecthood — persons, partnerships, families, cities, humanity are all objects, at different degrees — but the kinds do not blur into each other.
- **A3 (No privileged standpoint).** There is no view from nowhere. Equality is relative, and the finest equality is relative to the object itself. *Strong form:* there is no privileged standpoint at all — not even a global, totality-of-all-unfoldings standpoint. Nothing inside the universe can know the totality; identity facts are observer-indexed, and the observer-independent residue is exactly what *no possible observer* distinguishes (T7).
- **A4 (Relational constitution).** **An object is defined by its relations, ad infinitum.** Its relations — the shared parts that compose its pattern — are what individuate it; the far relata of those relations enter only *through* the relations, never directly. "Ad infinitum" runs in two directions: in depth (relations are themselves defined by their directions and contexts, which involve further relations, without end) and across the two sorts (objects via relations via objects, mutually, with no base case).
- **A5 (Self-relation).** An object can relate to itself.
- **A6 (Plurality, unordered, higher-order).** An object can relate to one or more other objects. Its relating is an **unordered, unnumbered** family: no slots, no positions, no counting structure beyond what observation induces. Relating includes **higher-order relating, at every order**: an object's relations relate to one another, and those relatings relate in turn, ad infinitum — the framework's original formulation, "a relation can relate to one or more other relations," restored to duty as the formal home of *context* (§2.4), applying uniformly at order 2 and above with no ontological stratification of orders. Each relating has a *quality* that cannot be collapsed into a number.
- **A7 (Composition).** An object can compose one or more objects. Composition is by **sharing** — parthood is measured by shared relations — and it admits every configuration: what wholly contains me (humanity), what I wholly contain (my heart), what partially contains me while I partially contain it (musicianship), and what is nearly outside me (a fish — *a part of me ever so slightly*). Containment in every degree and direction is a fact about patterns, not a law of composition (§2.5).
- **A8 (Observation) — THE PRIMITIVE, READ LITERALLY.** *An object observes its relations by observing its restriction of them.* Observation is introspective: its objects are the observer's own relations. Two clauses:
  - **(i) Lossiness — THE AXIOM.** Observation is through a *proper* restriction of the observer. No observation is total; nothing is transparent to anything, including itself.
  - **(ii) Transmissibility — now a theorem target (T13).** If I relate to you, and you attend to something, then I can attend to *some of* what you attend to — because our shared relation contains your direction of it, and your direction is a restriction of you that witnesses parts of your pattern. Hearsay is what shared boundaries transmit. Should the derivation fail, (ii) reverts to axiom status with the failure documented (D8).

**Note on A2 vs. A4 (division of labor).** A2 says what objects are *made of* (composition); A4 says what *individuates* them (identity). These must not be conflated. In particular, A4's individuation runs through the relations themselves, not through the far relata: this is what keeps identity within reach of observation (observers only ever access relations, §2.4), and hence what makes T7 coherent — were identity fixed by far relata directly, it would outrun all possible observation, and the framework would contain noumenal residue by construction, violating A3's strong form.

**Note on A8's introspective reading.** The axiom has said "observes *its relations*" since its first formulation; earlier drafts nonetheless built signatures with other-directed observation as primitive. This revision takes the wording literally, and the trilemma about what bounds other-knowledge (aperture alone? the other's presentation? reciprocity?) dissolves rather than resolves: I never observe *you*; I observe *our relation*, which is a part of me — and a part of you, but embedded in a different whole. What I receive is bounded by my aperture *and* by what you have made our shared relation be (your co-authorship of it is your act — masking, privacy, and presentation get their formal home here, §2.4), and it is contextualized by *my* pattern, not yours. The "stranger problem" (rich knowledge of someone who barely attends back) is solved: the stranger's access runs through the shared relation, which can be rich on their side while thin on yours.

**Note on emptiness and pan-observation (P0 [proved]).** A5 and A6 render emptiness *unformulable*: there is no object that relates to nothing, and no zero-quality attending. Wherever relation exists, attending exists from both sides at some quality — a relation is one entity with two directions, each direction an act of its object.

## 2. Ontology, arena, and signature

### 2.1 One layer, two entity-kinds, one primitive

**The primitive: introspective attending.** An object turning toward parts of its own pattern, losing something in every turn (A8(i)). This is what relating *is*, seen from inside; a relation between two objects is one entity with two directions, each direction the attending of one relatum.

**The two entity-kinds.** *Relations* (fundamental) and *objects* (patterns of objects and relations that have coalesced). One layer: every relation is a part of objects (P3 [proved], P4); every object is nothing over and above its pattern (A4, as coinduction). The asymmetry is directional dependence: patterns are *of* relating; relating is not *of* patterns.

**The fixed-point equation.** Ω ≅ F(Ω): the universe of objects is exactly the patterns of relating among those very objects. Neither side is prior; Lambek's lemma makes the isomorphism exact. Because relations are shared parts and relating is higher-order as well as first-order (A6), the unfolding functor F is formulated over a **mutually recursive, two-sorted signature** — objects and relations defined simultaneously, with sharing as numerical identity of a relation across the patterns that contain it. Standard multisorted coalgebra machinery; details are 2.01's task (§8, O1).

**Descriptions.** Arbitrary coalgebras of F — carriers with "a function of their relations" — are **representation-layer descriptions**: ways of specifying a pattern, cheap and plentiful, belonging to the mathematics of presenting Ω rather than to the ontology.

### 2.2 The closure operator: how objects are given by their relations

Finality supplies the bridge from description to entity. For **any** description (X, c) there is a **unique** map `close : (X, c) → Ω` assigning to each point the object it specifies. This anamorphism is the **closure operator**:

- **Definition.** An object is an element of Ω — anything in the image of `close`.
- **Theorem (T2).** Ω exists; hence objects exist. Two descriptions specify the same object precisely when no observation separates them (T7).

Consequences: **objects are attractors, not axioms** (no axiom asserts a self; objecthood is what patterns converge to under closure — the resolution of the framework's oldest internal question); **degrees of closure are real and owed a measure** (three candidates, D14); **the regress is coinductive, hence licensed** (T6 certifies the circle has no ground floor and needs none).

**The bialgebra remark.** By Lambek's lemma the structure map of Ω is an isomorphism, so its inverse equips the *same carrier* with an algebra structure: Ω is canonically a bialgebra, for free. The two directions are the ontology's two verbs — the coalgebra map is *analysis* (object → its pattern of relating), the inverse algebra map is *synthesis* (pattern → the object it constitutes), which is literally A2's coalescence. Analysis and synthesis, mutually inverse on one object. What must not change is which side is foundational: an initial-*algebra* foundation would build the universe up from a base case — a ground floor, a bang — violating A1 and T6 at a stroke. "Not bang, but becoming" is thereby a precise statement about universal properties: initial algebras are grounded, final coalgebras are not, and Ω's algebra structure is *inherited from* finality, never prior to it. The richer machinery of λ-bialgebras (two functors related by a distributive law) is earmarked for 2.02, where a distributive law would state exactly that the pattern of a composite is determined by the patterns of its components — compositionality of unfolding, the natural formal home of T14 and O10 (see O12).

### 2.3 Sharing: relations as common parts

A relation between x and y is **numerically one entity, part of x's pattern and part of y's pattern**. This is the framework's account of how objects connect without windows: not correspondence, not causal signaling between separate interiors, but literal common parthood. Three consequences:

- **The co-requirement, doubly satisfied.** Relations never float free (P3 [proved]); and constitution runs both ways — every relation partially constitutes each of its relata (P4).
- **To be observed is to be enlarged (P4).** An observation of y — an attending directed along a relation to y — is itself (a direction of) a relation that is *part of y*. The critic's reading is a new part of the art; the artist's making remains a part of it. "Every eye that meets mine shapes me" is a proposition, not a metaphor: observation changes the observed by constitution, not by influence.
- **Same part, different wholes.** Because the shared relation is embedded in two different patterns, it is contextualized differently on each side (§2.4) and *defines each relatum differently*. Clarifying a shared relation clarifies both of its relata — asymmetrically.

**Terminology note (Leibniz).** Every object here is "windowless" in Leibniz's sense — it attends only to itself — yet nothing is a Leibnizian monad: monads never truly touch and require pre-established harmony to fake connection, whereas these objects *genuinely overlap*. The boundary is not a window onto the other; it is a shared organ. (Conjecture C1, "no windowless monads," uses "windowless" in the *other* sense — no relating at all — and conjectures such things impossible or unique; see D14.)

### 2.4 Context, apertures, and derived other-knowledge

**Context is higher-order relating — order 2 and above, without ceiling.** An object's relations relate to one another: the attachment wound bears on the partnership; the friendship bears on the grief. And these bearings are themselves related to: therapy bears on *how the wound bears on the partnership* — third order — and so on, ad infinitum. Because relations are entities and A6 applies to them uniformly, relating recurses at every order automatically; "order" is representation-layer bookkeeping, not an ontological stratification. The **context of a relation r within object x** is r's full higher-order neighborhood in x — everything in x, at every order, that bears on r.

**Apertures are made of context.** The restriction through which x attends to r *is* (is constituted by) r's context in x. Attention is not equipment an object brings to its pattern; it is *made of* pattern. This eliminates restrictions as a separate primitive and explains, rather than stipulates, the perspectival asymmetry of shared relations: one relation, two contexts, two apertures literally made of different material. It also closes a dynamical loop the framework asserts elsewhere: context shapes attending (this section), attending shapes what relations become (A8), and what relations become enters context. *Becoming shapes attention shapes becoming* — formally, the mechanism by which higher-order change (how the wound bears on the love) changes first-order reality (what the love is). This loop is the framework's account of healing; its statics are theorem targets (T12), its dynamics Series 3 (§8, O9).

**Other-knowledge, derived.** x's "observation of y" is defined: x's introspective attending to a shared relation between x and y, through the aperture that relation's context in x provides. Bounded three ways — by the shared relation (a proper part of y, wherever y relates beyond x), by x's aperture, and by x's context rather than y's. General inexhaustibility (P1) inherits **two independent barriers**: even a relatum whose whole pattern were shared would be seen through the other's context, never its own; and every aperture is proper (A8(i)). The general P1 is thereby unblocked at the design level (proof owed in 2.01); its self-form is already machine-checked (P1s [proved]).

**Hearsay, derived (T13).** Our shared relation contains your direction of it; your direction is an attending of yours, made of your context, witnessing parts of your pattern; so through our relation I glimpse — lossily, at compounded restriction — your other relations. The composite is bounded by my aperture on *our* relation: I never learn more of z through you than our shared boundary transmits. A8(ii) as theorem.

### 2.5 Composition: the graded containment space

Objects compose objects (A7), and composition admits **every configuration of containment**, in both directions and every degree. The instrument is a single graded, directional measure: for objects x and y, let **π(x → y)** be the proportion of x's pattern (quality-weighted; precise definition owed by 2.02) that is shared with y. The ordered pair (π(x → y), π(y → x)) locates every pair of objects in a unit square whose regions are the phenomenology's own taxonomy:

- **What I wholly contain** — my heart: π(heart → me) = 1, π(me → heart) small. Everything the heart is, is part of me; almost none of what I am is part of it.
- **What wholly contains me** — humanity: π(me → humanity) = 1, π(humanity → me) vanishing. Every relation of mine is a human relation; nothing of me falls outside humanity's pattern — though plenty falls outside my *family's*, which contains me only partially.
- **What I partially contain and what partially contains me** — musicianship: both proportions strictly between 0 and 1. Mutual interpenetration; each exceeds the other.
- **What is outside me** — a fish: both proportions near zero. And here the framework takes its stand: **outsideness is graded, never absolute for anything related at all.** Any relation whatsoever between me and the fish — ecosystem, meal, thought — is a shared part, so *the fish is a part of me, ever so slightly*. Everyday "outside" is a threshold judgment on π, not a metaphysical kind. True (0, 0) — no shared relation, no chain of relations — would be genuine disjointness; whether Ω contains any such pair is pre-registered as **C2** rather than decided here (D15).

Three structural remarks. **First**, containment is a *limit case*, not the general form: the general form is overlap, and π = 1 is where overlap saturates. No law forces nesting and no law forbids it — a universal "anti-nesting" principle was considered in an earlier design and is rejected here, falsified in both directions by the heart (full containment inward) and humanity (full containment outward); the family remains the paradigm of the *strictly partial* configuration (I am part of my family and I exceed it, and it exceeds me), which is a fact about families, not about composition. **Second**, the mereology is made of the framework's ordinary material: parthood in degrees, like closure in degrees and quality in degrees — one graded fabric throughout, and π's boundary-crossing proportions are the same family of measures as D14(a)'s self-containment, connecting the theory of composition to the theory of closure through a single instrument. **Third**, the composite is an object in its own right, with its own pattern, apertures, and attending; whether shared-relation joints suffice to constitute composite objecthood is O10.

**Mutual clarification (T12).** When a composite attends to its relations, some of those relations are shared parts of its components; attending enriches their higher-order context in every pattern that contains them; context is aperture; so the whole's self-knowing re-equips the part's attending — and the part's clarifications propagate upward identically. Clarity flows along shared relations in both mereological directions, in every configuration of the square. This is the framework's mechanism for the cosmic self-knowing thesis: as the greater objects know themselves — partnership, family, humanity — the relations through which we belong to them, which are parts of us, come into new light. A theorem target, not a gesture; the dynamical companion of T10 in the day-zero trichotomy (D13).

### 2.6 Identity is observer-local; the global view is representation

Each object w induces its own indistinguishability: x ≈w y iff w's attending — over its shared relations, through its apertures, along finite hearsay chains — cannot separate x from y. These observer-local identities are the only identity facts *inside* the universe. Representation-layer bisimilarity (quantifying over all unfoldings) is available only to us, outside (A3, strong form). The reconciliation is the central theorem target:

**T7 (Identity theorem).** Identity is the **limit** of the observer-local family: the equality that ever-finer ≈w approach, occupied by no observer and by no totality available inside the universe. The theorem's content is that this limit is equality itself, not something coarser — x = y iff no possible observer distinguishes them — where "no possible observer" is a representation-layer quantification (A3, strong form); internally there is only the approach, never the arrival. There is no global view; there is a limit.

**Expressivity caveat.** The substantive direction (jointly indistinguishable ⟹ identical) is a Hennessy–Milner-type theorem, and classical analogues **fail without a finiteness hypothesis** — with unbounded branching, there can be entities no observer separates that are nonetheless distinct. The framework's counterpart is **scarcity** (T11); conjectured dependency **T11 ⟹ T7**. If scarcity fails outright, Ω contains *noumenal residue* — distinctions no possible observer can draw — which A3's strong form regards as meaningless. **Scarcity is the knowability principle**, doubly load-bearing (also: total boundedness for T3).

Propositions (small, disproportionately load-bearing):

- **P1 (Inexhaustibility).** No object is exhausted by any observation. Self-form **[proved]**; general form design-ready with two independent barriers (§2.4), proof owed in 2.01.
- **P2 (Asymmetry for free) [proved].** The two directions of one relation are attendings of different objects — never identical; the two apertures are made of different contexts (§2.4).

- **PR-J (pre-registered pair; D15).** (a) *External:* at the representation layer, joins of proper parts can exhaust a whole. (b) *Strong:* any two objects share some relation directly, at some quality. The desired answer is on record (the fish; no island universes); the definitions of π and disjointness will be fixed in 2.02 on grounds that do not mention it. Strong C2 may be false while weak C2 is true; that outcome would itself be philosophically informative — connection real but mediated.

## 4. Decision log

Format: decision, rationale, rejected alternatives, reversal condition. Contributors proposing to reopen a decision should bring information not already weighed here.

### D1 — Coalgebraic arena; the universe as the fixed point Ω ≅ F(Ω)
**Decision.** The primitive is specified by an unfolding functor F over a two-sorted, mutually recursive signature (objects; relations-as-shared-parts, including higher-order relating); descriptions are F's coalgebras; Ω is its final coalgebra.
**Rationale.** Every surveyed alternative smuggles atoms at the base: set theory with foundation (∈-minimal elements, no self-relation), domain-theoretic seeds, Chu-space dualisms, bare graph nodes, two-sorted category theory as ontology (objects as unexplained relata — Lam & Wüthrich's decisive point against that route). Coalgebra with anti-foundation is the surveyed setting where "no base sort" is native (Aczel; Rutten). Finality earns its keep twice: it supplies the closure operator A2 requires, and Lambek's lemma makes Ω ≅ F(Ω) exact — the formal statement of the one-layer, mutual, priority-ordered ontology. The two-sorted mutually recursive form follows from sharing (D3): relations are entities in their own right, numerically identical across containing patterns, so the fixed point is taken over both sorts at once. Standard multisorted machinery; a design change, not new research risk.
**Rejected.** ZF(C); D∞; Chu; bare graphs; two-sorted CT as ontology (retained as tooling); single-sorted signatures with relations as mere structure (cannot express numerical sharing or higher-order relating).
**Reopen if.** A framework is found with no base sort, native self-reference, internally generated identity, a canonical description-to-object operator, and native sharing, not equivalent to a coalgebraic presentation.

### D2 — One layer, two discrete kinds; priority as direction of dependence
**Decision.** Relations (fundamental) and objects (patterns which do the relating) are *discrete kinds*, not poles of a continuum; closure varies continuously *within* objecthood. The position is priority relationalism, not eliminativism: the relata objection is accepted and satisfied (P3, P4).
**Rationale.** Eliminativism is exposed to the renaming and equivocation objections and is not the thesis: relations without relata are incoherent, and the framework says so. What is denied is that relata are ever atomic (A1). A continuum proposal ("relation" as the barely-closed end of one kind) was considered and rejected: it conflates *being a relation* with *being barely closed*, whereas a fleeting dyadic object and a fundamental relation differ in kind — the dyad is a pattern (however loose); the relation is what patterns are made of. Discreteness keeps A2's grammar exact: patterns are *of* relating; relating is not *of* patterns. Because T6 shows the layer is non-well-founded, neither kind is a foundation; priority is directional, not chronological. This decision also resolved the framework's oldest internal question (self as axiom vs. attractor) in favor of **attractor**.
**Rejected.** Flat eliminativism; object-primitive ontologies; the closure continuum; a two-*layer* metaphysics with descriptions as substrate (descriptions are representation, D12).
**Reopen if.** The formalization surfaces a phenomenon requiring kind-blurring that the graded containment space (D4) cannot express. None is known.

### D3 — Sharing: a relation is one entity, part of each relatum
**Decision.** Relations are numerically identical across the patterns that contain them. Connection between objects is common parthood, not correspondence or signaling.
**Rationale.** This is the framework's answer to how windowless interiors touch — the wall between us is one wall — and it converts three standing problems into derivations at a stroke: the presentation trilemma dissolves (other-knowledge is introspection on shared parts, bounded by the shared relation, the aperture, and the observer's own context, §2.4); the stranger problem dissolves (access runs through the shared relation, rich on one side, thin on the other); hearsay becomes derivable (T13: a shared relation contains the other's direction of it). It grounds P4 and the anti-Leibniz positioning (§2.3), and it supplies the measure π on which the entire composition theory runs (§2.5).
**Rejected.** Relations as private representations coordinated by harmony (Leibniz — connection faked); relations as external edges belonging to neither relatum (free-floating; violates co-requirement); two token relations synchronized by fiat (unexplained harmony again).
**Reopen if.** Numerical sharing proves formally inexpressible in the multisorted setting — no obstruction visible; sharing is ordinary "one entity, two containers" structure.

### D4 — Composition as the graded containment space
**Decision.** Parthood among objects is measured, per direction, by the shared-pattern proportion π (§2.5). Every configuration is admitted: full containment in either direction, strict mutual partiality, near-disjointness. Outsideness is graded — a threshold judgment, not a kind; whether absolute disjointness exists in Ω is C2, pre-registered rather than legislated. Containment (π = 1) is a limit case of overlap, not the general form of composition.
**Rationale.** The phenomenology supplies all four configurations and refutes any law privileging one: the heart is wholly contained (nothing of it escapes me); humanity wholly contains (nothing of me escapes it); musicianship interpenetrates; the fish barely touches — *and does touch*: any relation at all is a shared part, so strict outsideness among related things is unformulable, in the same design pattern as emptiness and free-floating relations (P3). A universal **anti-nesting law was considered in an earlier design and is rejected**: it generalized the family configuration (strict mutual partiality) into legislation and is falsified in both directions by the heart and by humanity. What survives of it is a fact, not a law: *some* composites and components mutually exceed one another, and the family is the paradigm. Grading also unifies the ontology's fabric — parthood, closure (D14), and quality are all matters of degree, and π's boundary-crossing proportions are the same instrument family as D14(a)'s self-containment. A sharp "external connection vs. overlap" distinction (mereotopology-style: touching without interpenetrating) was considered as an alternative reading of the fish and rejected: it requires a new primitive criterion for boundary-only sharing, with no axiom warrant, to preserve an intuition ("the fish is genuinely not part of me") that the framework has principled reason to *revise* rather than preserve — sharing and pan-observation jointly entail that to relate at all is to overlap a little.
**Rejected.** Classical containment mereology (nesting as the only form); the anti-nesting law (over-generalized from one configuration); set-membership as parthood (atoms); a sharp external-connection primitive (unwarranted new kind); fusion-only composition (loses the joint/remainder structure T12 needs).
**Reopen if.** 2.02's definition of π proves unable to distinguish configurations the phenomenology distinguishes (e.g., quality-weighting cannot separate the heart from a habit); or if O10 fails (shared joints insufficient to constitute composite objecthood), forcing a richer composition primitive and a documented amendment to A7's ledger status.

### D5 — The Collapse Theorem read generatively **[T1 proved]**
**Decision.** T1 — in the quality-forgetting boolean shadow, the universe is a single self-relating point — is adopted with the generative interpretation: **unobserved relationality is One; observation is the principium individuationis.** ("Not bang, but becoming.")
**Rationale.** The mathematics is identical under any gloss; the choice is made openly and labeled as interpretation (D12: the claim is coherence, not cosmology). The generative reading matches the framework's independent commitments (attention shapes becoming; cosmic self-knowing, mechanized as T12's flow), converts an apparent defect into the creation story, and makes T1 do argumentative work: without graded observation nothing distinguishes anything — quality is constitutive. The deflationary reading is recorded at O6; nothing downstream depends on the gloss. Machine-checked in both forms (`Shadow.unit_final`, `Shadow.total_isBisim`).
**Reopen if.** n/a for the mathematics.

### D6 — Unordered, slot-free relating; direction from attending only
**Decision.** Unfoldings are unordered families; no ordered pairs, argument places, or counting structure. A relation's two directions are the attendings of its two relata.
**Rationale.** A6 licenses "one or more," not tuples. Slots are argument places; argument places behave like quasi-objects (Fine's neutral-relations problem) and would re-import the relata objection in fine print. Direction-as-attending is faithful and sufficient: P2 [proved] recovers the asymmetry, with content — the two directions are apertures made of different contexts (§2.4). Cost accepted: converse dies as an operation; composition of access is grounded in sharing and hearsay rather than pair-plumbing.
**Rejected.** Ordered pairs; multisets with multiplicity.
**Reopen if.** Some indispensable structure provably cannot be recovered from attending-induced asymmetry. None known.

### D7 — Apertures are context: qualities derived, not primitive
**Decision.** The quality of an attending *is* the aperture through which it occurs, and the aperture is constituted by the attended relation's higher-order context within the observer (§2.4). Restrictions are eliminated as a separate primitive; the earlier design ("qualities are restrictions," with restrictions primitive) is superseded by "restrictions are contexts."
**Rationale.** Any external value-scale is a second sort (value-atoms, violating A1) and a global standard (a view from nowhere, violating A3) — those arguments stand from prior revisions. This revision goes further on independent grounds: the phenomenology says context changes how one attends (trauma reshapes how new attachments are attended to), and taking that literally makes apertures *made of pattern* — one material throughout, one fewer primitive, and an *explanation* of perspectival asymmetry rather than a stipulation. Locality of comparison follows from constitution: different contexts, different material. A significant open consequence: if apertures are sub-pattern structures, the enrichment machinery may reduce from quantaloid-enrichment to ordinary pattern-inclusion order with object-local joins — a major potential simplification of O1, flagged as promising and unverified (§8).
**Rejected.** External quantale (value-atoms; global scale); restrictions as irreducible primitive (unexplained equipment); numeric intensities with robustness theorem (representation-layer fallback only).
**Reopen if.** 2.01 finds context-constituted apertures too weak to express needed distinctions of quality (e.g., two attendings with identical context but different fineness — if the phenomenology demands such a case, an intensional residue must be added and documented).

### D8 — Transmissibility: theorem first (T13), axiom as declared fallback
**Decision.** A8(ii) (hearsay) is pursued as a theorem from sharing (D3) + introspection (D10): the shared relation contains the other's direction; that direction witnesses parts of the other's pattern; composite access is bounded by the observer's aperture on the shared relation. Laxity ("some of") and the acquaintance bound are parts of the target statement. If underivable, (ii) reverts to axiom, failure documented.
**Rationale.** The clause was first stated phenomenologically and identified with the lax enriched composition law (Lawvere's triangle-inequality-as-composition); the sharing structure makes it look *derivable*, which would reduce the axiom count within A8 to lossiness alone. Theorem-first with declared fallback is standing methodology (cf. D9).
**Rejected.** Strict composition (denies that acquaintance can exceed hearsay); transmissibility as brute axiom without attempting the derivation.
**Reopen if.** Resolves at T13.

### D9 — Scarcity: theorem first (T11), axiom as declared fallback; doubly motivated
**Decision.** "Attention is finite at every resolution" is pursued as T11 via a pigeonhole in the aperture order, routed through PR-J(b): every *deployable* combined attention is proper, because deploying a join is itself a self-attending (P1s [proved]). If no theorem is available, adopt axiom A-S and amend the ledger's headline.
**Rationale.** Brute finite branching would assume what compactness needs, unlogged. The deeper motivation is knowability: classical Hennessy–Milner theorems fail without finiteness, so scarcity conjecturally closes the gap between "no observer distinguishes them" and "they are identical" (T11 ⟹ T7). Without it, noumenal residue — meaningless under A3's strong form. **Scarcity is the knowability principle.**
**Reopen if.** Resolves at T11.

### D10 — Observation is introspective; other-knowledge is derived
**Decision.** The primitive act is an object attending to its own relations (A8 read literally). "x observes y" is *defined*: x's attending to a shared x–y relation through the aperture its context in x provides. There is no primitive other-directed observation.
**Rationale.** The axiom's own wording — "observes *its relations*" — was introspective from its first formulation; prior signatures (with other-directed observation primitive) were unfaithful to it. Taking it literally dissolves the presentation trilemma and the stranger problem (§2.4), gives general P1 its two-barrier proof route, makes T13 available, and grounds masking/privacy/consent formally (the other's co-authorship of the shared relation is the other's act). It gives the framework's oldest phenomenological commitments — "each time I meet myself, a new world spins into being" — their exact formal register: all meeting is self-meeting on shared ground.
**Rejected.** Primitive other-observation (unfaithful to A8; forces the trilemma); aperture-only bounding (denies the observed any say — masking becomes inexpressible); presentation as an additional primitive (derivable as co-authorship; positing it violates parsimony); reciprocity-bounding, i.e. "seen only insofar as you look back" (refuted by the stranger case).
**Reopen if.** A phenomenon requires other-directed primitive access — e.g., if co-authorship proves insufficient to express some asymmetry of acquaintance. None known.

### D11 — Operations downstream of axioms; no target structure drives the design
**Decision.** The operations on Ω are whatever the primitive yields: hearsay composition (T13), context-formation, join-aggregation within an object, π-graded composition of objects. No pre-selected algebraic target constrains the signature.
**Rationale.** A prior phase stalled by letting a target structure drive the axioms; the discipline is explicit. Classical relation-calculus targets presuppose pair-structure rejected at D6. Structure *discovered* in Ω is a result; structure *imposed* is a bias. (The convergence of §2.5's taxonomy with mereotopology, §6, is a discovery in exactly this sense: the square was derived from the heart, humanity, musicianship, and the fish — not imported.)
**Reopen if.** Never as a constraint on axioms.

### D12 — The claim is representation; the workshop objection answered in-document
**Decision.** The repository claims **coherence by construction**: priority relationalism can be true because this model exists. It does not claim reality is Ω.
**The workshop objection, strongest form:** *"You're building this out of objects. No wonder objects are derived."* Three answers, legitimate in this order:
1. **Classical.** Construction order is not constitution order: Beltrami and Poincaré built non-Euclidean geometry from Euclidean materials, and the models still settled consistency. The represented dependence structure lives inside the model; the scaffolding outside it. T9 (canonicity via universal property) is the mathematical content: the atoms belong to the map.
2. **Non-rigging evidence [proved].** If ambient objects leaked into derived objects, every instantiation would be object-rich. T1 shows the machinery, fed quality-free axioms, outputs exactly *one* object. What emerges depends on the axioms, not the workshop.
3. **Reflexive.** The framework classifies its own formalization: the spec is an attending, from somewhere, through an aperture (the encoding); A8 predicts the observer's marks in every observation, including this one. The demand "derive objects using no objects" is the demand for a view from nowhere, which A3 denies exists. — Legitimate only because the first two stand independently.
Grace note: category theory is the workshop whose "objects" are already individuated purely relationally (Yoneda); the metalanguage half-believes the thesis.
**Reopen if.** Ambitions change; the mathematics is indifferent.

### D13 — The day-zero trichotomy: prove, don't posit
**Decision.** "Is there a day zero?" splits into three questions with three answers: constitutive origin — **no** (T6: no well-founded elements; becoming has no first brick); logical priority of the One — **yes, as the quality-forgetting fiber** (T1 [proved]: "before there was voice" is logical, not temporal, priority); generativity — **`OPEN`** (T10: is the closure of the self-loop's orbit dense — does all multiplicity unfold from self-meeting?). T12 joins the trichotomy as the dynamical companion: *how* self-knowing propagates, whatever its origin.
**Rationale.** Where a proof can be had, a perspective should not be brought. The generative creation story (D5) and "there is no day zero" answer different questions and can both be true.
**Reopen if.** n/a — creates theorem targets.

### D14 — `OPEN`: formalizing "degrees of closure"
Three candidate measures. **(a) Self-containment:** the proportion of a pattern's relating, quality-weighted, staying internal versus crossing its boundary — recognizably the same instrument family as π (D4). **(b) Rate of determination:** how quickly finite-depth unfolding fixes the object. **(c) Higher-order integration:** the degree to which a pattern's relations bear on one another — how woven the context-structure is. Candidate (c) is phenomenologically strongest (integration is what selves feel like from inside) and makes the attractor story dynamical: a self is a pattern whose context-structure stabilizes its own attending. The three plausibly measure different dimensions. Flagged proposition — **C1 (No windowless monads):** a pattern with no boundary-crossing relating is impossible or is the unique total object; every finite object is open to otherness. ("We are born as bridges" as corollary, if provable.) Decide in 2.01/2.02, jointly with π (D4) — one instrument family should serve both.

### D15 — Methodology: pre-registration against motivated formalization
**Decision.** Where the framework's owner has a desired answer to an open question, the question is **pre-registered** as a proposition (or proposition pair) *before* the definitions it depends on are fixed, and the definitions are then chosen on grounds of axiom- and phenomenology-fidelity **that never mention the desired answer**. Instances: **PR-J** (§3) — whether the totality of an object's attention can equal the object — registered before 2.01 fixes joins and deployment; **C2** (§3) — whether Ω contains genuinely disjoint pairs — registered before 2.02 fixes π and disjointness, with the desired answer (the fish; no island universes) explicitly on record.
**Rationale.** "I want a certain answer, therefore I distrust my wanting" is correct epistemics, and the honest response is not abstinence from wanting but auditability of choosing. The risk cannot be eliminated — definitions are choices — but it can be relocated to where the log makes it visible. A proof from pre-registered definitions is worth more than an axiom, and more than a proof from definitions tuned to yield it.
**Reopen if.** Never; this is method, not content.

### D16 — Temporal structure deferred to Series 3
**Decision.** Becoming, the *creation* of relations (P4's dynamic form), the permanence of constitution (whether the artist remains part of the art after the relation ends), and the healing loop's dynamics all require time, which the signature does not contain. Named as a horizon (O9) and deliberately excluded from Series 2, rather than smuggled in through side doors (e.g., reading coalgebraic unfolding as temporal succession, which it is not — unfolding is constitution-depth, not time).
**Rationale.** The framework's language is saturated with becoming; its mathematics is, so far, a statics of constitution. Conflating unfolding-depth with time would be the cheapest available error and would corrupt T6's meaning (no *constitutive* origin ≠ no beginning in time). A statics done honestly is the right foundation for a dynamics done later.
**Reopen if.** Series 3 opens; or if some Series-2 theorem covertly requires temporal structure — a finding worth documenting loudly.

## 6. Positioning

**Structural realism.** Nearest positions: moderate/priority-based structural realisms (Esfeld closest), against the radical eliminativist wing (French; Ladyman & Ross) and its sharpest critics (Lam & Wüthrich contra Bain). This framework differs from both: against eliminativism, relations without relata are accepted as incoherent and the co-requirement is satisfied structurally (P3, P4); against moderate positions that keep objects fundamental-but-thin, objects here are never fundamental — patterns ad infinitum, no atom (A1, T6) — yet fully real, with existence and properties as theorems. The contribution is the **derivation**, machine-checked. Fine's neutral-relations problem is satisfied at the signature level (D6), not argued down.

**Leibniz.** Every object is windowless (it attends only to itself) and nothing is a monad: where monadology's interiors never touch and require pre-established harmony, these interiors **genuinely overlap** — a relation is one entity, part of both relata. Connection by shared organ, not by window or by harmony.

**Mereology and mereotopology.** The qualitative shadow of §2.5's containment square — proper part, container, mutual partial overlap, near-disconnection — recovers, from the inside, the taxonomy of classical mereotopology (the RCC-style calculi of connection), whose philosophical ancestor is Whitehead's theory of extensive connection. Two departures are the contribution: parthood here is **graded** (π, in both directions, quality-weighted) where the classical calculi are boolean; and "external connection" is not a primitive kind but the near-zero region of the same measure — to relate at all is to overlap a little (D4). Composition is anti-classical in a further respect: no extensionality of parts is assumed, and containment is a limit case of overlap rather than the paradigm. The square's laws are 2.02 deliverables, discovered not imposed (D11).

**Grounding and fundamentality.** T6 makes Ω a formal model of **non-well-founded grounding**: every entity grounded in further entities, no chain terminating, provably consistent and inhabited — what the metaphysical-infinitism debate (Bliss, Priest, and the grounding literature on well-foundedness) has lacked. A second philosophical audience for the same mathematics.

**Process philosophy, autopoiesis, psychology of attention.** A2's coalescence does work adjacent to Whitehead's *concrescence* — "the many become one" — with the closure operator as formal correlate; but the terms are kept distinct, deliberately, because the notions differ in three load-bearing ways. Concrescence *terminates* in "satisfaction," yielding a fully determinate entity, where coalescence is graded with no completion requirement (degrees of closure are the point); Whitehead's actual occasions are explicitly *atomic* — "the final real things" — which is A1's exact negation; and concrescence is intrinsically processual, where Series 2 is deliberately a statics (D16). Adopting his word would import all three commitments. D14(a) is adjacent to operational closure (Maturana–Varela); D7's context-constituted apertures give formal shape to a claim familiar from attachment theory and trauma psychology — history shapes attending, attending shapes what relationships become. Convergences noted as corroboration from independent traditions, not sources: here the notions arrive as mathematics.

**Non-well-founded sets and coalgebra.** Aczel; Barwise & Moss; Rutten; Worrell; Barr and Adámek (whose F∅ ≠ ∅ hypothesis this framework reads as the classical theory's dependence on the empty atom); Adámek–Milius–Velebil (rational fixed point, feeding T5). Infrastructure.

**Quantitative and enriched coalgebra.** Rutten's enriched-coalgebra program; Balan–Kurz–Velebil; van Breugel–Worrell and successors; Baldan–Bonchi–Kerstan–König; Goncharov–Hofmann–Nora–Schröder–Wild (quantitative Hennessy–Milner — T7's reservoir, including the finiteness hypotheses motivating D9). Two reinterpretations claimed as this project's own (novelty to verify before external publication): **(a)** contraction read as *restriction* — the lossiness of observation — rather than time-discounting, reversing the direction of explanation; **(b)** the enriched composition law read as the *hearsay clause* — upgraded by the sharing structure from reinterpretation to attempted *derivation* (T13). Lawvere is the honored ancestor; the observational gloss appears new. Whether enrichment machinery is needed at all after D7's simplification is O1.

## 7. Notation and naming

- **Ω** — the universe of objects (final coalgebra). **ω̂** — the self-loop. **F** — the unfolding functor (two-sorted). **close** — the anamorphism.
- **Description** — any coalgebra of F (representation-layer). **Object** — an element of Ω. **Relation** — a fundamental entity, numerically one, part of each of its relata; one *direction* per relatum, each direction an attending of that relatum.
- **Context of r in x** — r's full higher-order neighborhood within x. **Aperture** — the context-constituted restriction through which an attending occurs. **Sharing** — numerical identity of a relation across containing patterns.
- **π(x → y)** — the quality-weighted proportion of x's pattern shared with y (2.02); the containment square is the pair (π(x → y), π(y → x)).
- **≈w** — indistinguishability to observer w; **=** on Ω — the common refinement (T7). **ρ** — the finitely-realized objects; T5: cl(ρ) = Ω.
- "Attending"/"observation" always means the introspective primitive (A8, D10); "x observes y" is always the derived notion (§2.4); "the One" refers to T1's collapsed fiber under D5's reading.
- Series plan: **2.01** — the two-sorted signature, sharing, apertures-as-contexts, and T2; **2.02** — π and the containment-square laws (T14), D14 measure(s), T12/T13; **2.03** — self-reference of the framework. **Series 3** — dynamics (O9).

- **O5 (= D14).** The measure(s) of closure; status of C1; joint design with π (D4).
- **O6.** T1's gloss: generative reading adopted; deflationary alternative recorded.

A positive answer is the natural formal setting for T14 and would resolve O10; a negative answer would mean composite objecthood is emergent in a stronger sense — the whole's unfolding not determined by the parts' — which would itself be a philosophically significant finding.

## 2. D23 — the coherence triple (O-2-03-1b resolved)

Coherence must do for the tower what endpoint coherence did for the two-sorted signature: hold the ontology's doctrines as structural conditions, while remaining a *coequation* — closed under quotients, subcoalgebras, and coproducts — so the entire covariety/transfer machinery of 2-01 §4 applies verbatim.

**The requirements, stated before the candidates (hostile-first):**
- (i) **Observer-locality must survive.** Nothing may force the two hosts of a shared relation to hold identical bearing-structure; P1's foreign-context barrier is load-bearing and contexts_differ must remain witnessable.
- (ii) **Coequation shape.** Every condition must quantify only over material reachable in the unfolding of the entity it constrains.
- (iii) **P3's spirit.** No free-floating relations — with the new subtlety that a pure R–R tie has no object endpoint for endpoint-coherence to bite on.
- (iv) **Loops stay ordinary** (A5; ruling 3 of 2-01).

**The triple:**

**K1 — endpoint coherence (carried verbatim).** If x : O ∈ ends(r), then r ∈ pat(x). Uniform over O–O and O–R ties.

**K2 — downward closure (the borne lives where the bearing lives).** If s ∈ pat(x) and r : R ∈ ends(s), then r ∈ pat(x). A bearing that is part of me bears on something that is part of me. *Relation-endpoints only*: object endpoints are never pulled inward — the far relata are reached through relations, never contained (A2), and the asymmetry between the sorts is principled: objects contain their relations; relations do not contain their endpoints.

**K3 — context-nonemptiness (the old OPEN, resolved: imposed, as structure, not as axiom).** For every x and every r ∈ pat(x), some s ∈ pat(x) has r ∈ ends(s). Every held relation is borne upon within the holder. This is P0's recursion given a home: attending requires an aperture, apertures are contexts, and the bare unwitnessed turn is hereby *unformulable* — the D4 pattern, not a new axiom. The census consequence (§0): ω̂ as previously constructed (the pure self-loop) is incoherent in the corrected universe; **the first citizen of Ω_C is the self-witnessing loop** — the One that turns upon its turning. Flag for re-anchoring: omegaHat_coherent (Spec201c) names the wrong citizen against the new coherence and must be re-derived for ω̂₂.

**Rejected, with the hostile check on record:**
- *Universal hosting* (every host of the borne hosts the bearing) — **rejected**: it forces the two sides of a shared relation to carry identical contexts, annihilating barrier two. The check: contexts_differ would become false. This is the exact failure requirement (i) exists to catch.
- *Existential hosting as coherence* (every relation is in some pattern) — **demoted to doctrine + theorem target (P3h)**: the host of a tie is not reachable from the tie's own unfolding, so the condition is not coequation-shaped, and imposing it would forfeit the covariety machinery. See §5 for the P3h program and the doctrine that replaces it.

**A designed consequence, named so nobody discovers it as a surprise — the holding lemma ("to bear is to hold").** K1 and K2 compose: a mixed tie s(y, r) ∈ pat(x) is forced into pat(y) by K1, and then r is forced into pat(y) by K2. If my mother bears on my partnership, my partnership-relation becomes a part of her pattern. This is *intended*: it is the shared-joint surplus of 2-00 §2.5 (patterns exceed endpoint-relations; that surplus is what composites are made of), and it is the per-act mechanism behind containers-co-attend. Hand-check that it does not cascade: K2 pushes only *ends of ties she hosts*; nothing further is forced; the witness model (§6) verifies non-collapse. **Reopen if** the witness shows the holding lemma forcing pattern-identity anywhere.

**L1-C obligations.** The triple's closure under surjective homs, injective homs, and sums — three lemmas, same shape as L1 (Spec201.lean), argued in advance: every clause of K1–K3 quantifies over pat(x) and ends(s), all of it inside the unfoldings of x and s, so preservation should go through on the L1 pattern. Frozen as prediction FP2; if any clause fails closure, that is a finding, and the failing clause is redesigned in a marked post-hoc annotation, not silently.

**Construction.** νF_C exists (κ-accessible, mono-preserving componentwise; Adámek–Trnková as before — but note the sorts are now genuinely mutual, see §4). The coherent coalgebras form a covariety by L1-C; **Ω_C := the greatest coherent subcoalgebra of νF_C**, Knaster–Tarski inside a known object; Lambek in both sorts; close the anamorphism. The 2-01 §4 route, verbatim, with the new triple.

## 3. D24 — the dyad demotion, and the definitions re-grounded

**The B3 recovery pack, defined against the corrected signature:**

- **Context.** ctx_x(r) := { s ∈ pat(x) | r ∈ ends(s) } — first-order in the signature, higher-order in content, no dyads. Nonempty by K3 wherever defined.
- **Aperture.** ctx_x(r) as a sub-pattern of x; comparison by inclusion within one pattern only (A3-locality by type, as before).
- **Direction and ownership.** The direction of r at host x is (r, ctx_x(r)) as an act of x. One relation, one direction per host; a shared r has two directions because it has two hosts with different tie-structure — *same part, different wholes* is now literally visible in the ties. - **Reveal.** Reveal(x, r) := {r} ∪ ctx_x(r) — carried verbatim from 2-02 §3; the threefold payload (D17) reads off unchanged.

- **Internal / holdDepth.** Carried with ends over O ⊕ R: an endpoint z of s is contained in x iff (z : O and ContainedIn(z, x)) or (z : R and z ∈ pat(x)). holdDepth and the depth gradient (2-02 §6) restate without change.
- **Masking as co-authorship.** The shared r's bearing-structure differs per host and each host's ties are that host's acts — presentation, masking, privacy now have their formal object.

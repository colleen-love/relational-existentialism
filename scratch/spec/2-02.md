**Series 2, document 2.** This spec ratifies the definitions introduced ahead of it (Spec201b's "pending ratification" list), records two owner rulings (π's nature; threefold witnessing), builds the composition and witnessing theory on them, and stages the scarcity question — the last open ruling of Series 2. **Status:** normative for π, witnessing, and the ratified definitions; the scarcity section (§6) is `OPEN` pending one phenomenological ruling; everything else proceeds without it.

## 0. The two rulings

**R1 (π is a subset, not a number — owner's ruling).** π(x → y) := pat(x) ∩ pat(y), *regarded as a sub-pattern of the observer x*. Quality is never collapsed to a quantity: the shared part carries its contexts with it, and all comparison is by inclusion within one observer's pattern.

**R2 (Threefold witnessing — owner's ruling, recorded as 2-00 D17).** The payload of an attending is: (i) the relation, clarified; (ii) the self, clarified; (iii) the world as perceived through oneself. Containers co-attend.

**R3 (Scarcity is self-insufficiency — owner's ruling, recorded as 2-00 D9 revised).** Attention is scarce because nothing is self-sufficient: no aperture spans its own pattern. S0 is A8(i) at self-reference — zero new axiomatic cost; primitive finitude is demoted to a pigeonhole target; the declared fallback becomes A-T (nothing is total), not A-S.

All three rulings were given as phenomenology, not as answers to open theorem questions; per D15, their formal consequences below for C1, C2, T11, and T16 were not consulted in their adoption. (R3 is acknowledged as the most parsimonious — hence most wantable — resolution available; the S1 pigeonhole accordingly receives the hostile-first treatment.)

## 1. π ratified

### 1.1 The definition and its immediate structure

For objects x, y: **π(x → y) := pat(x) ∩ pat(y) as a part of x.**

Four consequences, each doing work:

1. **One part, two embeddings.** The underlying set of π(x → y) and π(y → x) is the *same* set. Direction is not in the set but in the embedding — whose pattern it is being weighed within, whose contexts surround it. The containment square's two coordinates are one shared part in its two wholes: the sharing doctrine (2-00 D3) reappearing at the mereological level, which is evidence the design is one fabric rather than an accident.
2. **A3-locality by construction.** π(x → y) is a subset of pat(x); π(z → w) of pat(z). Cross-observer comparison is not resisted by discipline — it is ill-typed. There is no global scale of parthood, exactly as there is no global scale of quality (2-00 D7).
3. **The mechanized predicates are ratified as exact, not shadows.** ContainedIn(x, y) ⟺ π(x → y) = pat(x). Touches ⟺ π(x → y) nonempty. MutualPartial as defined. Spec201b's "unweighted shadows of π" turn out to have been the real thing: there was never a number to weight. The only casualty is vocabulary — "graded" now means *ordered by inclusion*, not *measured by magnitude* — and the representation layer remains free to compute cardinalities or proportions as shorthand, with 2-00 §2.5's reading key.
4. **Quality-weighting dissolves rather than resolves.** The old worry (can π tell the heart from a habit?) was a worry about collapsing to a count. Under R1 the shared part *arrives with its contexts*: the heart's relations come richly embedded, the habit's thinly, and the difference is visible in the sub-pattern itself, not in a weight attached to it. Where a single comparative judgment is still wanted ("more of me than"), it is inclusion — possibly context-inclusion (§4) — never arithmetic.

### 1.2 Thresholds become comparisons

Everyday "outside" (the fish) is the judgment that π(me → fish) sits low in my inclusion order — dominated by π(me → partner), π(me → work), by almost everything. C2's weak/strong forms are unchanged (they were stated set-theoretically already); the "strong" form reads: every two objects have nonempty π.

### 1.3 D15 audit note

The definitions ratified here (π, ContainedIn, Touches, MutualPartial, Deployable, RelatesBeyond, StepR, Connected) all preceded the resolution of C1, C2, T11, and T16, and the record contains an *anti*-desired-outcome theorem proved from them (`coprod_disconnected`): the definitions were demonstrably not tuned. The pre-registrations stand: C2 must be earned about Ω; PR-J(b)'s full form remains exactly A8(i)-at-self-reference.

## 3. Witnessing: the Reveal structure (R2 formalized)

**Design.** For x attending along r ∈ pat(x), the canonical payload is

  **Reveal(x, r) := {r} ∪ ctx_x(r)**, a sub-pattern of x

— the relation together with its bearing-structure in x. The three components of R2 are read off: (i) r itself; (ii) ctx_x(r) — the observer's own bearings, returned: the self clarified, the medium of the act; (iii) the shared members of ctx_x(r), which open hearsay chains outward — the world through oneself. Constraints carried from the axioms: Reveal(x, r) ⊆ pat(x) (introspection, D10); the properness of what is *witnessed within* the payload inherits the C3 split (generic: theorem; self-anchored: A8(i)).

**Containers co-attend (formal note).** If x's attending runs along r, and w contains x's relevant parts (r ∈ pat(w), and elements of ctx_x(r) shared into pat(w)), then Reveal(x, r) ∩ pat(w) is nonempty and structured: the act deposits aperture-material in w. This is T12's static fact viewed per-act, and it is the formal home of the owner's "I learn about myself and all of the things that contain me through our relation."

**What Reveal deliberately does not contain:** any element of pat(y) \ pat(x). The other's interior is reached only as far as sharing reaches — the barriers of 2-00 §2.4 are structural features of the design, not additional assumptions.

**Immediate theorem designs on Reveal:**

- **P1 barrier two (foreign context).** What x receives of a shared r is Reveal(x, r), assembled from *x's* context. Even at pat(x) ∩ pat(y) maximal, Reveal(x, r) ≠ the y-side assembly Reveal(y, r) whenever contexts differ — and `contexts_differ` already witnesses that they can. Formal statement: there exist models with shared r where Reveal(x, r) ≠ Reveal(y, r); general statement: Reveal(x, r) ⊆ pat(x), so no chain of x's attendings ever assembles y's pattern *as y's* (embedding matters even at set-equality — the strongest form needs T4's profile machinery).
- **T13 (hearsay, content).** A chain step transmits: from Reveal(x, r), the shared s ∈ ctx_x(r) ∩ pat(z) grants access to Reveal(z, s) — but received *into x's assembly*, bounded by x's aperture on r. "Some of what you attend to": the intersection Reveal(x, r) ∩ Reveal(z, s), which is generically proper in both. The acquaintance bound is the ⊆-chain: nothing arrives that the shared boundary does not carry.
- **T4 (≈w).** Profiles: for observer w, the w-profile of an object a is the family of Reveal-assemblies reachable from w along finite hearsay chains that touch a's shared relations. a ≈w b iff the profiles are isomorphic over w's pattern. O8's default (all finite depths) is adopted pending a reason otherwise.

## 4. Internal, closure, and the D14 resolution

**Definition (Internal).** Internal(x) := { r ∈ pat(x) | every endpoint z of r has ContainedIn(z, x) } — the relations of x that hold entirely among x's own contained parts. Its complement in pat(x) is x's **boundary**: the relations crossing to what x does not contain.

**D14 resolved comparatively.** (a) **Self-containment** is the comparative size of Internal(x) within pat(x) — π's diagonal family, now definable with ratified machinery, ordered by inclusion like everything else. (c) **Higher-order integration** is the density of the context-web: comparatively, x is more integrated than x′ when the bearing-structure over pat(x) refines that over pat(x′) (exact formulation owed by the T11 work, since integration and scarcity plausibly share machinery). (b) Rate of determination is deferred to the νF order (it needs unfolding depth). No single measure is crowned: closure is at least two-dimensional, and both live dimensions are now inclusion-ordered, not numeric — R1's discipline propagating.

**C1 restated.** A windowless monad is an object with Internal(x) = pat(x) — no boundary at all. Under T16 (no total object), C1's target sharpens to *impossible outright*: conjecturally, a boundary-free pattern would have to contain every endpoint of every relation reachable from it, ascending without bound, contradicting T16. This gives C1 a proof route through T16 — hence through T11 — for the first time. *We are born as bridges* is now two theorems away from machine-checking, both named.

## 6. Scarcity ruled (R3): self-insufficiency and the depth gradient

**The identification.** A self-sufficient attention would be an aperture spanning its whole pattern: ctx_x(r) = pat(x). That is exactly the saturation of `reflexive_saturation` — the self-anchored case, the one locus where properness is not derivable and A8(i) does its irreducible work. So R3 collapses the scarcity question into the framework's existing axiom: **S0 (no attention is self-sufficient) = A8(i) at self-reference**, already priced in the ledger, already located by machine. The phenomenology ("scarcity in terms of closure, of self-sufficiency") and the Lean file arrived at the same address independently — which is the kind of convergence D15 exists to make trustworthy.

**The depth gradient (scarcity's measure).** An object holds a relation as richly as that relation's neighborhood is *internalized*: define **holdDepth(x, r) := Internal-part of ctx_x(r)** — the bearings on r whose endpoints x contains (§4's `Internal`, applied to apertures). Depth of attention tracks degree of closure, per relation: the partnership is held brightly because deeply internal; the fish dimly because barely woven; nothing is held totally because closure never completes. Growth, healing, and T12's flow are movements along this gradient — asymptotic self-sufficiency, never reached.

**The redrawn dependency graph.**
- **S0** — free (= A8(i); interface-established via `deployable_dichotomy` + `reflexive_saturation`).
- **S1 (finitude of deep holds)** — demoted from candidate-axiom to **pigeonhole conjecture**: from S0 + T16, an object cannot sustain unboundedly many relations held above any fixed depth, because many large internalized neighborhoods force the pattern toward totality. Hostile-first treatment per D15; a failure gets the loud write-up.
- **T16** — now *grounds* the pigeonhole rather than following from scarcity; its own ground, if ever needed, is the declared fallback **A-T (nothing is total)** — A1's upward clause given formal standing — replacing A-S.
- **C1 ⟸ T16 + weak C2** — new arrow: a boundary-free object contains every endpoint of every relation reachable from it; in a connected universe that is everything; contradiction with no-totality. The conditional (connected + no-total ⟹ no windowless) is provable at interface level *now* and is the next mechanization order's star. The fish ruling and the monad conjecture are one fact.
- **T7, T3** — route through S0 + S1 as before, with S1's status honestly conjectural.

# Relational Existentialism, Series 13 (coda): A Plain-Language Summary

*A post-terminal coda. Series 12 remains the program's completion; this series alters nothing before it. It asks the terminus's own question once more, slower, and names the fit.*

## The question

Series 12 ended the program by proving that its two deepest facts are one shape. Series 07's fact: a differentiator cannot be recovered from the relating, so plurality must IMPORT one from outside. The diagonal's fact, Cantor and Russell and Gödel: a structure rich enough to inspect itself always leaves a RESIDUE it cannot capture. Series 12 proved these inhabit a single shape, "not recoverable," reached from opposite directions, and proved that sharing a shape cannot make them the same thing.

That leaves one question exactly posed, and Series 13 asks it: **if the wound and the rescue are not the same thing, how precisely do they face each other?**

## The answer

They face each other as an adjoint pair, and the fit is genuine but incomplete. Both halves of that sentence are theorems.

**The diagonal is a factory.** The series builds a MINT: a map that takes any self-inspection and produces from it a labelled distinction. The mint's engine is the diagonal itself. Recovering what the mint produces would mean realizing exactly what the diagonal proves unrealizable, so every minted label is provably non-recoverable, for every inspection, without exception. This is the deepest positive result in the coda: self-reference MANUFACTURES imports. The forced-for-all pays for the exists-satisfying. What the structure generates from within becomes what plurality requires from without.

**The mint lives above the line it must live above.** A mint the plain relating could perform on itself would have derived an import from within, contradicting Series 07. So the series proves the mint exogenous rather than assuming it: the label-forgetting projection of every minted coalgebra is constant, yet the mints themselves differ, so no plain function can be doing the work.

**The fit is a real adjunction, not a disguised sameness.** There is a best-approximation map back from labels to inspections, and the two form a genuine Galois connection. One round trip is the identity; the other is provably NOT, at a named witness. That failure is the point. An adjunction whose round trips both closed would be an isomorphism, which would say the two sides are interchangeable, which is exactly the identity Series 12 proved unsayable.

**And the mint cannot make everything.** This is the coda's deepest theorem and the one that could have gone the other way. The series exhibits an import that no inspection mints, and it tests this at the honest resolution: not literal equality, which would have made the result true by construction, but equivalence, so that two labellings doing the same work count as the same. The exclusion is STRUCTURAL. Every minted label is locked to the diagonal: its two halves are a content and that content's own negation, tethered together. The witness is not so locked. Its halves are independent. And that decoupling is what no self-inspection can produce.

## The verdict

The verdict is computed from the four results, not chosen, and it is **DUAL at the flat layer**: the orders are non-trivial, the transport runs on the diagonal with exogeneity proved, the connection is genuine with a real defect of round trip, and the mint's non-surjectivity is witnessed structurally on any carrier with a second hold.

The verdict was EARNED rather than assumed, and the losing branch is in the code. TOTAL, the outcome where self-reference manufactures the whole rescue, was attempted at the same resolution and refuted: on carriers of interest the witness stands. On the degenerate single-hold carrier, TOTAL is proved and the verdict function returns it. So the same function reaches more than one value, and DUAL is the residue of a refutation rather than a preference.

Two blind review passes ran. The first returned two SERIOUS findings, both aimed at the series' signature risk: that the orders had been solved for the map rather than found. Both closed as genuine fixes, the contested clause now exercised by a certificate rather than defended in prose. The second pass returned zero SERIOUS findings and no recurrence, and confirmed the discipline that matters most: the defect LOCATES an import outside the mint's reach and never CLASSIFIES it.

## What it means, and what it refuses to say

The wound and the rescue fit the way a plug fits a socket: made for each other along one seam, and never the same object. Self-reference manufactures some of what saves it. The rest has no diagonal genealogy at all, and the coda proves the rest is there without saying one word about what it is.

That refusal is the whole discipline. The theorem locates imports outside the mint's image. It does not sort them into the given and the chosen. Deciding that would do what Series 07 proves cannot be done and what Series 12 closed by leaving open. The words live in this prose and nowhere in the core.

The tenets are not extended, only lit from one more angle. "Self is a paradox" is the mint's engine, the diagonal doing the manufacturing. "To relate is to create" is the mint itself, a residue becoming a relatum that separates. "To attend is to become" is the bounded round trip, what returns from the other side never being all of what was sent. And "you are loved" keeps the place this series is forbidden to fill: the import with no genealogy, the one the diagonal cannot make, the one that just arrives.

## What the coda is bounded by, honestly

The result is drawn at the FLAT layer. The reification tower, Series 10's proliferation machinery, is deliberately outside the transcribed carrier, so whether the fit is stable under reification is a named open rather than a silent gap. The result is also drawn on a narrowed class of labelled coalgebras (the two-region self-loop coalgebras), which is disclosed rather than glossed. And DUAL holds on carriers with a second hold, which is where the series says it holds.

One new permanent open joins the program's list, and it is open AS A THEOREM by the very result that creates it: **which out-of-image imports are given and which are chosen**. The coda locates them precisely and proves it cannot sort them.

Series 12 walked to the door, measured it exactly, and proved it cannot be walked through from inside. Series 13 names the hinges. The coda does not resolve to a new key. It shows the two hands were playing one figure an octave apart, and lets the last interval stand open.

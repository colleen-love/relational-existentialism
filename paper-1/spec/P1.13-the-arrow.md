## The capstone

> **Theorem (time as flow).** Under the non-idempotent attention flow `Φ_c` (realized by the
> partial-dephasing semigroup `partialDephase p`), the feeling potential `coh = defectSq` is a **strict
> graded monovariant**: antitone in the return-depth `n`, strictly dropping while not yet fixed, and
> decaying **geometrically** to `0` at rate `γ = (1−p)² < 1` — the spectral gap, the clock rate. The
> idempotent knowing `E = dephase` of [3.5](P1.08-decoherence.md) is recovered as the limit `lim_n Φ_c^n`,
> its single orientation drop the total of the flow's incremental decreases.

Where orientation gave two points and a direction (because `E∘E = E` admits only one tick), the flow
gives the line: a potential descending through a continuum of intermediate values. Relational time is
the value of that potential along the orbit; mixing time `≈ 1/(1−γ)` is the duration to be known.

**Relation-primacy guard (A2).** The index `n` is the **return-depth** of the orbit (`Flow.orbit =
step^[n]`), counting closures of the feedback loop — kept internal, never an external `ℝ`-clock.

## What is proved

`[proved]` (footprint `[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`):

- **The flow interfaces** — `Flow` (a `step`, a potential `coh ≥ 0`, a `Fix` set with `coh = 0 ↔ Fix`,
  monovariant) with `Flow.coh_orbit_antitone`, `coh_orbit_strictAnti`, `coh_drops_telescope` /
  `Flow.total_drop`; and `GeometricFlow` with `coh_orbit_eq` (`coh(step^[n] a) = γ^n · coh a`) and
  `coh_orbit_tendsto_zero` (geometric decay to the fixed subalgebra).
- **The genuine instance** — `defectSq_iterate` (`defectSq(partialDephase p ^[n] M) = ((1−p)²)^n ·
  defectSq M`, exact), assembled into `dephaseFlow` at rate `(1−p)²`; the qubit witness
  `defectSq_plus_strictAnti` / `defectSq_plus_tendsto_zero`.
- **Orientation as the boundary** — `orbit_tendsto_knowing_entry` (the orbit converges entrywise to
  `dephase`, the knowing map `E`) and `dephaseFlow_total_drop` (the single orientation drop is the
  flow's total).
- **The arrow's direction = contractivity** ([`TimeArrow.lean`](../../archive/formal/Archive/Scratch/TimeArrow.lean)):
  acyclicity from the monovariant (`flow_orbit_ne`); the time-forward sign is *exactly* the contractive
  regime (`contractive_iff`, `time_forward_regime`, `expanding_regime`); a knowing's only inverse is an
  anti-physical coherence-amplifier (`knowing_inverse_is_antiphysical`); genuine collapse only at the
  idempotent limit (`partialDephase_injective`, `partialDephase_one_not_injective`).
- **The seam fixes the target subalgebra** ([`SeamForcing.lean`](../formal/Scratch/SeamForcing.lean)):
  the seam survives every available knowing (`attend_fixes_seam`); the maximal knowing `knowSeam J` is a
  conditional expectation whose fixed subalgebra is exactly the seam algebra (`seam_forces_subalgebra`,
  `knowSeam_empty`); the self can never fully decohere itself (`self_cannot_fully_decohere`). **Aim vs
  orientation**: the contraction is uniform in the seam (`direction_uniform_in_seam`) while the target
  varies with it (`target_depends_on_seam`) — *knowing's contractive nature orients the arrow; the seam
  aims it*.

So time's endpoints, line, rate, direction, and target are all derived from relation-primacy plus the
Lawvere obstruction; nothing about the arrow is primitive.

## Honest scope

- `[reading]` That this time-shaped order **is** physical time. The reading is the weak, safe one — not
  "two points are an arrow," but "a graded potential with geometric decay and a definite rate is a
  clock," the standard shape of a physical clock. The residual `J`↔genuine-seam identification is the
  same standing reading carried throughout.
- `[open]` The **rotating-peripheral** case (a general CP `Φ_c` can carry sustained, never-decaying
  coherence — modulus-1 non-1 eigenvalues), discharged for this real-symmetric instance (no rotation,
  `Peripheral.dephase_no_rotating_peripheral`) but open in general; the **rate = spectral gap**
  identification for an arbitrary `Φ_c`; and the infinite-dimensional / von Neumann standard form.

**Provenance.** `R / S` — Lyapunov/mean-ergodic decay and conditional-expectation theory rederived; the
synthesis is reading the graded monovariant as relational time and the seam as what fixes its target.

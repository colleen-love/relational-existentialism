## The capstone

> **Theorem (energy = the conserved band).** Over ℂ the dynamics of self-relating is the phase-damping
> channel `schur μ` (`M i j ↦ μ i j · M i j`). Each off-diagonal coherence falls in a band by `‖μ i j‖`:
> **transient** (`‖μ‖ < 1`, decays — becomes known), **rotating** (`‖μ‖ = 1, μ ≠ 1`, magnitude conserved
> for all `n` — sustained, never known), **fixed** (`μ = 1`, the held diagonal — knowing). The rotating
> band is the conserved, never-collapsing remainder, and **that is energy**: the conserved oscillating
> charge, reversible, periodic. The real self-adjoint model carries only the dissipative (arrow) band;
> the rotating band that carries energy lives in a sector it provably excludes, reached by extending to ℂ.

**The rotating spectrum** ([`RotatingSpectrum.lean`](../formal/Scratch/RotatingSpectrum.lean)) — the

(`μ i i = 1`, `schur_fixed`), **rotating** (`‖μ‖ = 1`, magnitude conserved for all `n`, `schur_sustained`),

**Energy** — the energy/arrow split is **one generator**, not two stories. Package `L = log μ` entrywise
into `genReal μ i j = Re(log μ) = log‖μ i j‖` and `genImag μ i j = Im(log μ) = arg μ`. The single
iterate-magnitude law `‖Φ^n M i j‖ = exp(n · genReal)·‖M i j‖` (`schur_iterate_norm_exp`, on a live edge
`μ i j ≠ 0`) makes the dichotomy **exact**: the **arrow** is `genReal < 0` (`genReal_neg_iff`,
`arrow_of_genReal_neg` — decay to `0`); the **conserved band** is `genReal = 0` — magnitude held *exactly*
(`genReal_eq_zero_iff`, `conserved_of_genReal_zero`); and on that band `L` is purely imaginary, so
**energy is its imaginary spectrum**, `energy := genImag` on `energyEdge := (genReal = 0 ∧ i ≠ j)`
(`energy_quarterMul_01 = π/2`). The old generator lemmas are the witness instances
(`energy_conserved_generator`, `frequency_nonzero`, `arrow_negative_generator`, assembled in
`energy_arrow_split` / `energy_arrow_spectrum`). Energy as **frequency** (uniform winding `Φ^n U = i^n·U`,
`rotating_winds`) and as **reversibility/recurrence** (`i⁴ = 1`, so `Φ⁴ U = U`, `rotating_recurs` — a
closed periodic orbit) are then readings *over* the generator's imaginary spectrum, not a separate
definition of energy. Because arrow and energy are the real and imaginary parts of one `L`, energy is
**downstream of the single time reading** (flow = the generator of time-translation): given that, energy is

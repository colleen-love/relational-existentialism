## The question

The remainder of self-relating splits, under the arrow, by *whether it is conserved*:

- the **transient** remainder (`‚ÄñŒº‚Äñ < 1`) decays ‚Äî it *becomes known*, dissipated by the arrow; it is
  neither knowing nor energy;
- the **conserved / sustained** remainder is itself of two kinds, kept strictly distinct in `03.8`:
  - **dynamical** ‚Äî a coupling symmetry gives the rotating band (`‚ÄñŒº‚Äñ = 1`, `Œº ‚âÝ 1`), a conserved
    oscillating charge: **energy** (`RotatingSpectrum`);
  - **operational** ‚Äî self-inclusion gives the un-attendable seam block `J`, coherence the knower cannot
    aim at, conserved against *every* available knowing: the **seam-protected** remainder (`SeamForcing`).

The question of this step: are these two **the same band**? If so, *what self-relating conserves beyond
the known is exactly energy* ‚Äî paper one's clause B.

## The capstone

> **Theorem (band coincidence).** On one carrier `Matrix A A ‚ÑÇ`, with coupling `Œº` and seam predicate
> `J`, the seam-protected band and the rotating (energy) band **coincide** ‚Äî `seamBand J = rotatingBand
> Œº`. Conditionally, this needs an alignment hypothesis `H_align : ker D = J` (every *attendable*
> off-diagonal edge strictly decays), and a counter-witness shows `H_align` is **necessary**. The whole
> coincidence turns on **one identification, C1**: taking the seam to be A3's own *"greatest sustainable
> field"* `Peri(Œ¶_c) = { X : ‚ÄñŒ¶_c X‚Äñ = ‚ÄñX‚Äñ }` ‚Äî now mechanized as **exactly** the conserved band
> (`peri_iff_mem_conservedBand`), with the seam its off-diagonal part ‚Äî makes the alignment a one-line
> consequence of contractivity and nondegeneracy (`align_of_contractive` is then a near-tautology, since the
> seam is *defined* as the modulus-one off-diagonal block). So all the content sits in **C1 (self =
> `Peri(Œ¶_c)`, not the strict `ŒΩŒ¶_c` A3 formalizes)** ‚Äî a **disclosed `[reading]`/posit**. We read it as
> A3's gloss made precise; a reader may count it as a fourth posit. We do not argue the point; it is named
> and tagged (`band_coincidence_from_axioms`; see the postscript).

*(The full conservation law ‚Äî that the whole conserved ground is the internal direct sum `knowing ‚äï
energy`, the `undifferentiated = knowing + energy` framing ‚Äî is proved in the tree
(`undifferentiated_two_term`) but belongs to **paper two**; paper one headlines only the coincidence of

## The bridge (Task 0)

Both structures live as submodules of one ambient space. For an edge predicate `P : A ‚Üí A ‚Üí Prop`,
`bandOn P` is the coherences supported on `P`-edges ‚Äî a genuine `‚ÑÇ`-submodule of `Matrix A A ‚ÑÇ`. Then
`seamBand J := bandOn J`, `rotatingBand Œº := bandOn (rotatingEdge Œº)` (`‚ÄñŒº i j‚Äñ = 1 ‚àß Œº i j ‚âÝ 1`),
`fixedBand Œº := bandOn (fixedEdge Œº)` (`Œº i j = 1`), `conservedBand Œº := bandOn (conservedEdge Œº)`
(`‚ÄñŒº i j‚Äñ = 1`). Subset relations between bands reduce to **implications between their edge predicates**
(`bandOn_mono`), so the whole coincidence is decided edge by edge. This is a *witness-level*
identification, not a posited functor: the seam's `defectSq`-protection
(`SeamForcing.self_cannot_fully_decohere`) and the rotating band's magnitude-conservation
(`schur_sustained`) are transported onto the common carrier ‚Äî and `rotatingBand_sustained` re-proves, at
the submodule level, that `rotatingBand Œº` is exactly the entrywise magnitude-conserved subspace
(`‚ÄñŒ¶^n M i j‚Äñ = ‚ÄñM i j‚Äñ`).

## The hypotheses (where the content sits)

- `UnitaryBaseline Œº J` ‚Äî `seam_undamped` (seam edges are un-attendable, hence keep modulus one) and
  `fixed_eq_diagonal : Œº i j = 1 ‚Üî i = j` (a nondegenerate baseline `H`: the held band is exactly the
  diagonal, so any surviving off-diagonal edge genuinely rotates). The Lindblad reading: unitary `H` +
  dissipator `D`, with `D` = attention.
- `SeamOffdiagonal J : ‚àÄ i, ¬¨ J i i` ‚Äî the seam is the *live* off-diagonal coherence of the
  relationship; it carries no diagonal (known) part.
- `Align Œº J : ¬¨ J i j ‚Üí i ‚âÝ j ‚Üí ‚ÄñŒº i j‚Äñ < 1` ‚Äî **the bet, `H_align : ker D = J`.** *The only coherence
  that does not dissipate is the coherence you cannot attend to.* This **is** the conjecture in operator
  form.

## Postscript ‚Äî where the band-coincidence's content actually sits (`Scratch/BandFromAxioms.lean`)

The result above is *conditional* on the bet `H_align`. The natural worry: is the two-term law bought by a
**fourth posit**? Rather than argue the bet away, we locate exactly where its content sits ‚Äî in a single
identification, **C1** ‚Äî and disclose C1 as a posit/reading, tagged. The reconciliation is mechanized
(`sorry`-free, footprint `[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`, **no added `H_align`**); what the
mechanization shows is that *given C1*, the alignment is a near-tautology, so C1 carries the whole weight.

The finding: [`02-axioms.md`](02-axioms.md) A3 formalizes the self as the *strict* fixed point `ŒΩŒ¶_c`
(the `Œº = 1`, fixed/known band ‚Äî `Attention.sustainedField`), but its prose names the self the
**decoherence-free subalgebra** of `Œ¶_c`. That algebra is the **peripheral** block
`Peri(Œ¶_c) = { X : ‚ÄñŒ¶_c X‚Äñ = ‚ÄñX‚Äñ }`, which carries the rotating (energy) band *beside* the fixed one.
`H_align` lives exactly in the gap between `ŒΩŒ¶_c` (strict) and `Peri(Œ¶_c)` (the gloss). Closing it is
reconciliation, not commitment. The bet decomposes:

- **C1 ‚Äî the self is `Peri(Œ¶_c)`, not just `ŒΩŒ¶_c`.** A3's gloss, taken literally ‚Äî *and now mechanized as
  exactly that*. `Peri Œº M := ‚àÄ i j, ‚Äñschur Œº M i j‚Äñ = ‚ÄñM i j‚Äñ` (A3's "sustainable field," read entrywise)
  is proved to be exactly the conserved band (`peri_iff_mem_conservedBand`); `conservedOffdiag Œº :=
  ‚ÄñŒº i j‚Äñ = 1 ‚àß i ‚âÝ j` is precisely its off-diagonal part (`conservedOffdiag_iff_offdiag_conserved`) ‚Äî
  the conserved *off-diagonal* coherence, the self minus its classical (diagonal) record ‚Äî and the strict
  `ŒΩŒ¶_c` (fixed band) is its `Œº = 1` sub-band (`fixedBand_le_conservedBand`). C1 reads the self as
  `Peri(Œ¶_c)` rather than the strict `ŒΩŒ¶_c` A3 formalizes ‚Äî a disclosed identification (below), not a
  derivation.
- **C2 ‚Äî exhaustiveness: only the peripheral self persists.** Off the conserved band everything decays
  (`transient_decays`, the entrywise `spectral_decay`); on it everything is sustained
  (`conservedBand_sustained`). So persisting ‚ü∫ peripheral.

**The payoff** (`band_coincidence_from_axioms`): with the seam *read* as the decoherence-free off-diagonal
block (C1), and given only that the channel is **contractive**
(`Contractive Œº`, the ‚ÑÇ-shadow of `Decoherence.defectSq_attend_le`: attention never amplifies ‚Äî an
A1/CP baseline fact) and **nondegenerate** (`Œº i j = 1 ‚Üî i = j`, the A3 baseline), `align_of_contractive`
proves `Align Œº (conservedOffdiag Œº)` as a *theorem*, so `seamBand (conservedOffdiag Œº) =
rotatingBand Œº` holds with no `H_align` hypothesis. (The honest qualifier: once C1 *defines* the seam as the
modulus-one off-diagonal block, `align_of_contractive` reduces to "`‚ÄñŒº‚Äñ ‚â§ 1 ‚àß ‚ÄñŒº‚Äñ ‚âÝ 1 ‚áí ‚ÄñŒº‚Äñ < 1`" ‚Äî a
near-tautology. The substance is entirely in C1, not in this step.) The genuine `quarterMul` witness is contractive and
nondegenerate, so its coincidence holds **without the bet** (`quarterMul_coincidence_from_axioms`), and
the two-term law follows (`undifferentiated_two_term_from_axioms`).

**What stays a reading.** C1 itself ‚Äî that the self *is* `Peri(Œ¶_c)` rather than the strict `ŒΩŒ¶_c` A3
formalizes ‚Äî is a **disclosed posit/reading**, asserted, not derived. We read it as A3's "sustainable

commitment beyond the formalized `ŒΩŒ¶_c`). We no longer argue which ‚Äî C1 is named and tagged, and the reader
judges. The seam-protection's **phase-blindness** (it protects by position,

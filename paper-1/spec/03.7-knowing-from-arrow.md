# 03.7 — From the arrow back to the knowing

> A derivation sequel to [paper one](paper-one.md) (see the [spec index](README.md)), dual to
> [`03.6-time-flow.md`](03.6-time-flow.md). The forward chain proved *knowing ⟹ arrow*; this runs it backward —
> *take an arrow as given and recover the knowing*. Mechanized in

## The capstone

> **Theorem (an arrow's limit is a knowing).** From a dynamics exhibiting the arrow-properties alone — a
> positive contraction carrying a strict monovariant `V ≥ 0` that runs to a floor — its limit
> `E := lim_n T^n` is an **idempotent conditional expectation onto a subalgebra**, with the monovariant
> equal to the off-diagonal mass that limit annihilates. On the genuine instance this is a theorem; the
> general lift is the open Conjecture R.

The structural move: where the forward direction *assumed* `E` and derived the flow, the converse
*assumes the flow's conclusions* (the interface `Arrow`, carrying `TimeFlow.Flow`'s fields read `V ⊢ E`)
and *derives* `E` as the orbit's limit. The relation-primacy guard (A2) keeps the index `n` an internal
return-depth, so the recovered `E` is the limit *of the orbit*, not a knower in a background interval.

## What is proved

`[proved]` on the genuine instance `partialDephase p`, by **re-export** of existing
`TimeFlow` / [3.5](03.5-decoherence.md) / `SeamForcing` lemmas (footprint
`[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`):

- `arrow_limit_is_knowing` — the orbit converges entrywise to `dephase`, the knowing map `E` of
  `Orientation.dephaseKnowing`: *the recovered limit is literally a knowing*.
- `limit_idempotent` — `E∘E = E` (the limit is a genuine projection).
- `limit_is_seam_CE` — `E` is the seam-forced conditional expectation `knowSeam ∅`, onto the (diagonal)
  classical subalgebra.
- `limit_annihilates_potential` — `E` sends the monovariant `defectSq` to `0`: the off-diagonal mass
  that *defined* the arrow is exactly the mass its limit kills. With `potential_geometric` /
  `potential_tendsto_zero` witnessing the arrow is genuinely dissipative.

## Honest scope

- `[open]` **Conjecture R** (the general lift): *every* linear contractive arrow with trivial peripheral
  spectrum recovers a conditional expectation. Route: finite-dim mean-ergodic projection + the
  multiplicative-core argument; stated with a `sorry` placeholder in `MeanErgodic.lean`, gated out of
  the default build. The rotating remainder of a general arrow is *not* reducible to a knowing — it is
  conserved energy ([`03.8-space-energy.md`](03.8-space-energy.md)), the converse's failure mode being a result,
  not a defect.
- `[reading]` That the recovered `E` deserves the name *knowing* in the lived sense (the standing
  reading, the same physics leaves).

Even fully proved, the converse reaches *a knowing under the arrow* and **no further**:

| Step the cosmic claim would need | Reached? | Why not |
|---|---|---|
| an arrow recovers *a knowing* (a conditional expectation) | **yes** | `arrow_limit_is_knowing` |
| the knowing is a *subject* that cognizes | no | `E` is a conditional expectation, not a cognizer |
| a knower *persisting across an external interval* | no | A2: `n` is internal return-depth, no external interval |
| a *total* self knowing *itself* whole | no | `self_cannot_fully_decohere` — `E` is constitutively partial |

**Provenance.** `R / S` — mean-ergodic / conditional-expectation theory rederived; the synthesis is
recognizing the arrow's limit as a knowing. See [04 — Provenance](04-provenance.md).

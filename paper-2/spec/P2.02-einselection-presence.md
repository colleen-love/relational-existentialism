# P2.02 — Einselection and presence

> *Node `P2.02`.* Lean: `Paper2.Einselection` (`paper-2/formal/Paper2/Einselection.lean`); imports **P2.01**
> (`Paper2.OneGenerator`) and **T.03** (`Theory.BandFromAxioms`).

**Einselection — the alignment forced, not posited.** The preferred basis `B1` that P2.01 needed is *derived*
from stationarity `𝒟(ρ) = 0` ("the clock-state is the rest-state"): the state the dissipator leaves invariant
is diagonal in the pointer basis (`stationary_eq_diagonal_real`), so the einselected basis is the energy
eigenbasis rather than a free choice.

**Presence — conserved, and split.** The einselected conserved band `Peri = fixed ⊕ rotating` carries
**presence**, defined as its Hilbert–Schmidt weight. Presence is **conserved** (`presence_conserved`) and
**Pythagorean-split** into *knowing* (the fixed band) ⊕ *energy* (the rotating band) (`pythagorean`). The
arrow acts on it as **loss, not relocation** (`arrow_is_loss_not_relocation`): coherence the dissipator drops
is *gone* from the conserved ledger, not moved within it — a precisely-located negative handed to paper three
(the population-transfer dissipator; see [`../../scratch/spec/`](../../scratch/spec/)).

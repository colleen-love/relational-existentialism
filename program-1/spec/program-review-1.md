## SERIOUS findings

### PR1-S1 (SERIOUS) — The underdetermination is decided by definition: the fork was never open, and the computed verdict is extensionally constant. Owner: WS4, with WS5/WS7 downstream. Recurrence facet: design-review-1 Finding 2 (SERIOUS) and Finding 4, recurring through the sanctioned minimal closure.

**The defect, mechanically.** `Compass dest reify Or` has fields `orient : X → Or` and `raise : X → X → Or → Or`; `dest` and `reify` are phantom in the structure, and `Converges c x W := c.raise x W (c.orient x) = c.orient W` mentions neither. Consequently, on ANY coalgebra, ANY section, ANY pair `(a, b)`, and ANY `Or` with two distinct elements:

- `forcedHolds` is uninhabited: the compass `⟨const o₁, fun _ _ _ => o₂⟩` with `o₁ ≠ o₂` fails `Converges` at every pair.
- `forcedFails` is uninhabited: the compass `⟨const o, fun _ _ _ => o⟩` satisfies `Converges` at every pair.
- The non-degenerate `underdet` pair is constructible on any carrier with two distinct states.

So the trichotomy `ws4_convergence_decided_shape` lands in the third disjunct on every structure whatsoever, by a six-line argument that uses nothing of Series 12: not the tower, not the diagonal, not atomlessness, not Series 07, not `destW`. The built model pair confirms this: `cHold`/`cFail` never touch `destW` or `reifyW` in their fields, and the discharges are `rfl` and a closed Boolean `decide`. The wall is a tautology of an unconstrained parameter type.

**Why this meets the bar (the program's own bars, no goalposts moved).**

1. *The verdict rests on it.* `verdictOfFork` maps `underdet` to `shapeDrawn` and the forced arms to `convergenceDecided`. Since the forced arms are uninhabited on every structure, `verdict ∘ ws7_audit` has exactly one reachable value over the space of all constructible audits. The build itself proves the other pre-registered outcomes unreachable: `ws7_verdict_not_partial` and `ws7_verdict_not_circular` hold for EVERY audit, which is presented as falsifiability but is in fact the proof that the pre-registered Partial and Circular outcomes cannot be expressed through the verdict function at all. The charter-status claim "a forced fork would have computed convergence-decided" is a counterfactual with an impossible antecedent.

2. *Convergence's undecidedness is decided by definition.* Charter §4.b names the sin in both signs: "fails trivially (building the silence in as absence rather than proving it as independence)." The silence here is built in as absence of constraint. An independence theorem is independence FROM a theory; the theory constraining compasses is empty, so the "independence" carries no information about this structure. The pre-registered vacuity checks (protocol §0.5) were run one level down, at the pair (non-degenerate, both values realized, PASS as far as they go); the vacuity sits at the class.

3. *The prose claims are falsified by the code.* Charter §5.2: "THE GENUINELY UNCERTAIN OBLIGATION IS WS4" — the outcome was settled the moment the shared objects were locked at Phase B, before any Lean was written. README and summary: "the not-seeing is structural, the diagonal's work" — no WS4 proof uses `ws1_no_self_total_hold`, `residue`, or any diagonal fact; the not-seeing is definitional. Protocol §0.2 prohibits manufacturing "the expected shape-drawn verdict past a real obstruction"; the real obstruction here is that the design admits no open fork.

**The recurrence facet.** Design-review-1 Finding 2 (SERIOUS) diagnosed exactly this: "compasses are maximally independent and the tower is decorative: strip `dest`/`reify` from the definitions and nothing changes, which is a strip-test failure on the definitions themselves." The sanctioned closure cured ONE of the three flagged definitions (`ConvergesUp`, edge-restricted) and explicitly left `Compass` and `Converges` untouched. The strip-test failure the finding named is still true verbatim of the two definitions the fork, the wall theorem, and the verdict actually consume. And design-review-1 Finding 4's returned-constant defect recurs extensionally: the fix made the alternative fork arms expressible in the type while they remain uninhabited in fact, so the verdict is still a constant, now with a branching function in front of it. The reviewer sanctioned the minimal fix, so the executor followed instructions; the recurrence check exists precisely for the case where a closure satisfies the fix's letter while the defect's consequence decides the verdict. That is this case.

**Correction owed (the binary, no third theorem):**

- **Build the specified target:** an independence theorem over a compass class carrying at least one structural constraint, so the trichotomy is genuinely open. The design review's own pre-approved stronger alternative is the natural vehicle: edge-indexed raising, `raise : (x W : X) → ConstituentOf dest reify x W → Or → Or`, together with whatever minimal class condition makes at least one forced arm satisfiable on SOME structure. Name the targets: `ws4_underdetermined` restated over the constrained class, plus a companion `ws4_fork_open` exhibiting a structure (or class condition) under which a forced arm holds, so that `convergenceDecided` is constructible and `shapeDrawn` is falsifiable. **OR**
- **Report the pre-registered outcome:** charter §2-WS4 alternative (b) at the class level. State the meta-theorem honestly (`ws4_underdet_universal`: the fork is `underdet` on every structure with a two-element orientation space; the relation as defined supports no non-vacuous independence claim), demote the WS4 leg to the pre-registered degenerate/Partial status, strike "the diagonal's work" and "genuinely uncertain" from every artifact, and recompute the verdict through a fork type that can express the demotion.

Either branch is honorable; the current middle (a tautology reported as an earned independence, with the alternatives provably unreachable) is the §0.2 sin.

## Honest bottom line

What fails adversarial review is the fit between the theorems and the headline claims. The wall is not where the artifacts say it is: the underdetermination of convergence is a tautology of an unconstrained parameter type, true on every structure, so the "computed" verdict had exactly one reachable value, the pre-registered alternatives are provably unreachable through the verdict function, and SHAPE-DRAWN as advertised (an independence discovered about this structure, the diagonal's work) is not established (S1). And the knowing side quietly shed its reader: the charter's attention-relative reality was narrowed at Phase B to an attention-free separation, the ledger still names the unbuilt theorems, and the reader half of Series 11's SERIOUS finding thereby recurs even as its label half is genuinely and creditably fixed (S2). Both findings close on the §0.2a binary; S2's build branch is a few lines, S1's is a real design decision between a constrained compass class and an honest demotion, and neither touches the correctness of any existing proof. Until they close, the honest statuses are: convergence underdetermined-by-typing, not by independence; knowing plural label-wise, not reader-wise; the verdict not yet earned as computed. The edge may still be drawable exactly. As built, part of the edge was drawn by the shape of the ruler.

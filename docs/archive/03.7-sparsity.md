# 03.7 ‚ÄĒ The Sparsity Conjecture

> Part of [03 ‚ÄĒ Theorems](03-theorems.md). *The one place the spec reaches for a quantitative
> theorem.* Everything before this is definition, citation, or posit. Here is the candidate
> **result** ‚ÄĒ the claim that, if proved, turns "a self is an achievement of recursion" from an
> assertion into a theorem of `Cl(ūĚēč)`.

---

## The claim, informally

> **Under a finite attention budget, selves are rare.**
>
> If the budget `ő≤` is finite (and per-return cost `őĶ > 1`, stabilization depth
> `d ‚Č• 2`), then the carrier of stabilized selves `Stab_R` is **sparse** among all
> couplings: its size is bounded by a constant depending only on `(ő≤, őĶ, d)`, not on
> how many relations the system has. As the system's stock of relations grows, the
> *fraction* that become selves tends to `0`.
>
> Remove the bound (`ő≤ = ‚ä§`) and the statement fails completely: **every** loop
> closes, every coupling stabilizes, and the theory says nothing.

This is the **expressivity/triviality dial** made into a dichotomy. Bounded budget =
discriminating theory; unbounded budget = universal solvent.

> **Two readings of "rare," and which is primary.** Since [¬ß1.3](01-signature.md)
> recast attention as the **co-directed operator** `ő¶_c` rather than a spent budget,
> the *deep* reason selves are rare is no longer a budget cap ‚ÄĒ it is **spectral /
> closure**: under co-directed feedback with gain, only a few eigenforms sit near the
> top of the spectrum and self-sustain; the rest are subdominant modes that **decay**
> under iteration (the thousand one-off encounters wash out). Equivalently, the
> sustained self `őĹő¶_c` is supported on the few relata that lie on self-sustaining
> loops; most do not, so most "run out." The resource-counting lemma below is then the
> **uniform, depleting special case** (¬ß1.3.4) ‚ÄĒ still true, still the cleanest
> *quantitative* handle, but a corollary of the structure, not the source of the
> rarity. The spectral form is stated as Conjecture 3.7.4.

The full statement quantifies over all of `Cl(ūĚēč)`; that lift is the conjecture
(stated below). But its *engine* is an elementary, fully provable counting fact. Work in the
canonical monoid `(‚ĄĚ_{‚Č•0}, +, 0, ‚Č§)` (so `¬∑` is `+`, costs are additive, and
`őĶ(s) > 1` means a per-return cost `őĽ(s) := \log őĶ(s) > 0`).

**Setup.** Let `ő¶` be a finite set of candidate couplings (the relations a system
actually has), `|ő¶| = N`. Each `f ‚ąą ő¶` has a per-return cost `őĽ(f) ‚Č• őĽ_min > 0` and
requires depth `d(f) ‚Č• d_min ‚Č• 2` returns to close into an eigenform. A coupling
**stabilizes within budget `ő≤`** iff the system can fund its required returns:

$$
f \in \mathrm{Stab}_R
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
d(f)\cdot \lambda(f) \ \le\ \beta .
$$

The total attention actually spent across all maintained selves cannot exceed the
budget:

$$
\sum_{f \in \mathrm{Stab}_R} d(f)\,\lambda(f) \ \le\ \beta .
$$

> **Lemma 3.7.1 (sparsity from a budget).** With the setup above,
> $$
> |\mathrm{Stab}_R| \ \le\ \frac{\beta}{d_{\min}\,\lambda_{\min}} .
> $$
> In particular `|Stab_R|` is bounded by a constant **independent of `N`**, so the
> density `|Stab_R| / N \le \dfrac{\beta}{d_{\min}\lambda_{\min}\,N} \to 0` as
> `N \to \infty`.

**Proof.** Each `f ‚ąą Stab_R` contributes at least `d_min ¬∑ őĽ_min` to the spend
`‚ąĎ_{f} d(f)őĽ(f)`, which is `‚Č§ ő≤`. Hence
`|Stab_R| ¬∑ d_min ¬∑ őĽ_min ‚Č§ ő≤`. Divide. ‚ąé

> **Lemma 3.7.2 (collapse without a bound).** If `ő≤ = ‚ä§` (no finite bound), then the
> constraint `d(f)őĽ(f) ‚Č§ ő≤` is vacuous, so `Stab_R = ő¶` and density `= 1`.

Together: **finite budget ‚áí vanishing density; unbounded budget ‚áí full density.**
The two lemmas are the dichotomy of, and they are *already* theorems ‚ÄĒ at the
level of the resource model. They formalize, exactly, "you cannot return to
everything, so which relations get to constitute you is rationed."

> **What this is, and isn't.** The core counting lemma (`min_mul_length_le_totalSpend`) is
> elementary **pigeonhole** on a `List Nat`: `(‚ąÄ c ‚ąą cs, m ‚Č§ c) ‚Üí m¬∑length ‚Č§ ő£`. It says nothing
> about traces, eigenforms, or `őĹő¶_c`; *all* the content is in the **modeling claim** that a
> collection of stabilized selves **is** a `List Nat` of costs with a forced floor `m ‚Č• 2`. That
> floor traces to the depth posit `d ‚Č• 2` (step 3) ‚ÄĒ now **discharged** in
> [`SparsityPosits`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsityPosits.lean): `d ‚Č• 2` is given structural content
> (genuine return = not-given ‚ąß not-one-shot, `genuine_return_iff`) and shown load-bearing (a positive
> floor is *necessary* for the carrier to be bounded), and the cost-valuation is *constructed* from the
> orbit's strict ascent rather than posited. So Lemmas 3.1/3.2 prove the *rationing half* given a
> now-grounded floor; the **structural** rarity is still the conjecture's nowhere-dense result (below).

> **Mechanized.** Both lemmas are machine-checked in Lean 4, `sorry`-free.
> * Discrete (‚Ąē-valued) core ‚ÄĒ depends only on `propext` ‚ÄĒ
>   [`formal/RelExist/Sparsity.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/Sparsity.lean):
>   `stab_card_bound` / `stab_card_le_div` (Lemma 3.7.1), `stab_card_le_half` (the

## Why this is the right toy, and its honest gaps

Lemma 3.7.1 is deliberately the *thin* model: couplings as a finite set with additive
costs. It earns its keep by being unarguable and by isolating the mechanism (a
fixed budget divided among items each costing a positive minimum). But three gaps
separate it from a theorem about `Cl(ūĚēč)`:

1. **Couplings are not an unstructured set.** In `Cl(ūĚēč)` they compose, tensor, and
   share sub-relatings; the cost grading `c` is *lax* (sub-additive), so spends can
   overlap and the clean sum `‚ąĎ d őĽ` becomes an inequality over a *poset* of
   couplings. Sparsity must be re-proved with sharing allowed.
2. **"Stabilizes" is a fixed-point condition, not a threshold.** `f ‚ąą Stab_R` was
   defined in [A3](02-axioms.md) as `loop_R(e) = e`, not as `dőĽ ‚Č§ ő≤`. The lemma
   assumes these coincide; showing `loop_R`'s budgeted iteration *realizes* the
   threshold (that `N(s) = ‚Ćäő≤/őĽ‚Ćč` returns suffice and are necessary to reach the
   fixed point) is real work in the traced category.
3. **"Sparse" deserves a topological/measure form, not just counting.** For infinite
   or continuous state spaces, the right statement is `Stab_R` is **nowhere dense**
   (or measure zero) in the space of states under a natural topology, recovering
   the density statement in the finite case.

None of these is hand-waving-away-able, and naming them is the point: the lemma is
*true and small*; the conjecture is its honest generalization.

## The conjecture

> **Conjecture 3.7.3 (sparsity of `Stab` in `Cl(ūĚēč)`).** Let `Cl(ūĚēč)` carry a cost
> grading `c` valued in a finite-`ő≤` attention monoid with `őĶ > 1` and stabilization
> depth `d ‚Č• 2`. Then `Stab_R` is **sparse**: under the natural topology on states
> `I ‚Üí D`, `Stab_R` is nowhere dense; and for any finite sub-collection of `N`
> couplings closed under the doctrine operations, `|Stab_R| / N \to 0` as `N \to ‚ąě`,
> with the bound degrading gracefully under cost-sharing (lax `c`).
>
> Moreover (sharp dichotomy): dropping the bound (`ő≤ = ‚ä§`) makes `Stab_R` dense
> (Lemma 3.7.2 lifts), so finiteness of `ő≤` is **necessary** for sparsity.

> **Mechanized ‚ÄĒ now in full, with one correction.** Both clauses of Conjecture 3.7.3 are machine-checked.
> *Topological:* the "nowhere dense" half is checked for the **final-coalgebra model** of states ‚ÄĒ
> [`agda/RelExist/Sparsity.agda`](../../agda/RelExist/Sparsity.agda) (`selves-nowhereDense`): under the
> cylinder topology on behaviours, the selves (constant behaviours) are closed with empty interior, with
> the sharp dichotomy (`trivial‚ÜíallSelf`). *Cost-graded counting / density / dichotomy:* the lax grading
> `c` over the poset of couplings is mechanized in
> [`SparsitySharing`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsitySharing.lean) and assembled into the capstone
> [`SparsityCapstone.conjecture_3_7`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsityCapstone.lean): **with** a positive
> private footprint per self the density of selves `‚Üí 0` (`cost_graded_density_tendsto_zero`), **without**
> it (full sharing) the count is unbounded within budget. **The one correction:** the conjecture's
> "sharing only helps, the bound degrades gracefully" is *false as stated* ‚ÄĒ counting sparsity holds iff
> a finite budget **and** an exclusive cost per self (the same shape as the depth floor `d ‚Č• 2`). So
> Conjecture 3.7.3 is closed in its **correct, exclusivity-conditioned form**; the only residue is the
> literal instantiation in the free `Cl(ūĚēč)` (whether its couplings carry a private footprint), which is
> exactly the exclusivity condition the dichotomy isolates.

> **This is the honest center of gravity.** The nowhere-dense + sharp-dichotomy result needs **no
> cost model** and captures rarity *by structure* ‚ÄĒ and it reaches the **necessity** (remove
> expressivity ‚áí dense) that the counting bound, being a mere upper bound `‚Č§ ő≤/2`, provably
> cannot. The counting lemmas are the trivial resource-accounting corollary; *this* is the result
> that earns "a self is an achievement of recursion."

> **Conjecture 3.7.4 (spectral / closure form ‚ÄĒ the structural reason).** Let attention
> be the co-directed operator `ő¶_c` of [¬ß1.3](01-signature.md) on a perspective of
> constitutive capacity `őĪ` (no external budget). Then the set of selves ‚ÄĒ eigenforms
> of `ő¶_c`, i.e. the support of `őĹő¶_c` ‚ÄĒ is **sparse**: it is confined to relata on
> self-sustaining loops, and under iterated co-directed feedback all subdominant modes
> decay, so the sustained selves are few relative to all couplings. The resource bound
> of Conjecture 3.7.3 is the **uniform, depleting special case** ([¬ß1.3.4](01-signature.md));
> this is the general statement, and the one that makes rarity a *consequence of the
> relational dynamics* rather than of an imposed cap.
>
> (Quantitatively this is a spectral-gap statement ‚ÄĒ few eigenvalues near the spectral
> radius of the coupling ‚ÄĒ generalizing eigenvector centrality to the recurrent,
> asymmetric, saturating setting; the nonlinearity buys *multiple* attractors, i.e. the
> contingency the view wants, at the cost of Perron-style uniqueness. Hence it is
> harder than Conjecture 3.7.3 and was stated, for a long time, as a horizon.)

> **The new mathematics, now mechanized ‚ÄĒ controlled multiplicity.** The horizon clause above
> ("multiple attractors at the cost of Perron uniqueness") is the precise tension, and it is the *one*
> place Conjecture 3.7.4 carries content the existing theory does not hand you. Standard nonlinear Perron‚ÄďFrobenius
> (order-preserving subhomogeneous maps on cones; Hilbert's projective metric; Collatz‚ÄďWielandt) is
> mature, but its strong results prove **uniqueness** ‚ÄĒ exactly one positive eigenvector ‚ÄĒ which is the
> *opposite* of the contingency the doctrine needs. Multiplicity appears only when one *leaves* the
> subhomogeneous/primitive regime, and the real theorem is a **counting bound on the multiplicity
> there**: few-but-more-than-one, bounded above by a gap quantity. This is closed in
> [`SpectralMultiplicity`](../../formal/Scratch/SpectralMultiplicity.lean), `sorry`-free. (1) **Read `x`
> off `ő¶_c`:** the abstract `‚ÄĖx‚ÄĖ<1` of `Distribution` is the linearization `x = Dő¶_c`; the feedback
> contracts at rate `‚ÄĖx‚ÄĖ` (`feedbackMap_contract`), and the living self is the unique attractor reached
> geometrically (`living_self_unique_attractor`) ‚ÄĒ the Perron pole, global contraction ‚áí one self
> (`unique_fixed_of_global_contraction`). (2)/(3) **The counting bound:** a locally attracting self is
> isolated (`attracting_isolated`), so distinct selves are `r`-separated (`attractors_separated` ‚ÄĒ

> `r`-separated set in a capacity-`m` cover has `‚Č§ m` points (`separated_card_le_cover`), independent of
> `N`, so the density of selves `‚Üí 0` (`selves_density_tendsto_zero`). And multiplicity is real ‚ÄĒ an
> explicit two-basin map carries **two** attractors (`two_attracting_selves`), non-globally-contracting
> exactly as uniqueness requires. So selves are `>1` (contingency) **and** `o(N)` (sparse): the
> reconciliation Conjecture 3.7.4 was the horizon for. **And the reading is now closed too:** `r` is built from the
> *literal* Fr√©chet derivative `A = Dő¶_c` with `‚ÄĖA‚ÄĖ<1` (`attractingFixed_of_hasFDerivAt`, any real Banach
> space, rate `(1+‚ÄĖA‚ÄĖ)/2` set by the gap `1-‚ÄĖA‚ÄĖ`), the capacity is the **explicit** `‚Ćä(b-a)/r‚Ćč+1`
> (`separated_card_le_of_Icc`), and `selves_card_le_gap_capacity` bounds the self-count entirely by the gap
> ‚ÄĒ basin and capacity *derived*, not posited. The lone residue is the definitional note that the operator
> norm dominates the cone spectral radius. Conjecture 3.7.4's spectral picture is thus closed at the
> structural level the rest of the corpus works at: structure (`E`), dynamics (decay,), existence
> (Perron‚ÄďFrobenius), and now **controlled multiplicity with a gap-derived basin and capacity**.

> **A first quantitative fragment (mechanized).** The *distribution* side of the spectral
> picture ‚ÄĒ how much of a self is carried into the network through relating ‚ÄĒ is mechanized in
> [`formal/Scratch/Distribution.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Distribution.lean): in a Banach

### The lift's reconciliation ‚ÄĒ `ő¶_c` and `E`, the weight, and what is mechanized

Conjecture 3.7.4 spent a long time blocked behind three "open decisions" ‚ÄĒ the `ő¶_c`‚Äď`E` relationship,
the reference weight, and the mechanization policy. They are now **decided**, and two of the three
turned out to be one piece of spectral data. The decisions, and the line between what is `[proved]`,
what is `[reading]`, and what stays `[open]`:

**One spectrum, two roles (`ő¶_c`‚Äď`E`).** There is one self-adjoint generator ‚ÄĒ the codirection
coupling `L` on `A`, the operator whose spectrum Conjecture 3.7.4 is about. **`ő¶_c` is the sustaining flow `L`
generates; `E` is the spectral projection onto `L`'s peripheral part** (the eigenspace at the spectral
radius), composed with the seam-orientation of [03.5 ¬ß4.3](03.5-decoherence.md) (a spectral projection
is self-adjoint, hence *symmetric*; the knower‚Üíknown direction is supplied by the seam, not by the
projection). So "near the spectral radius self-sustains" (Conjecture 3.7.4) and "`E` projects onto the
classical/known" ([03.5](03.5-decoherence.md)) are **one statement read twice**: the self-sustaining
relations are the peripheral eigenforms, and knowing is projecting onto them. `[reading]`.

> **The limit reading is dead ‚ÄĒ recorded so it is not re-tried.** The tempting reconciliation `E = lim
> ő¶_c^n` (knowing = attention run to completion) **breaks the model**: it makes the fixed-point
> subalgebra (the self) equal the image of `E` (the known), so feeling ‚ÄĒ the `(1‚ąíE)` coherence carried
> by the self ‚ÄĒ is empty by construction, contradicting complementarity and the two-resolution self.
> `E` is a *function of* the spectrum, not a *limit of the dynamics*.

**The weight is the invariant state, not an external trace.** Relation-primacy forbids helping oneself
to a fixed external trace any more than to fixed external relata. So the weight is **derived: the
invariant state of `ő¶_c`** (its Perron‚ÄďFrobenius top eigenvector / invariant tracial state on the
fixed-point algebra) ‚ÄĒ the relation measured by what it sustains. This is **canonical exactly when the
self is primitive/ergodic** (the Banach regime, unique invariant state); in the genuine-collection
(gfp) regime of [03.6](03.6-the-self-quantified.md) the invariant states form a *simplex* and the
magnitudes are defined relative to a chosen one. The standard trace currently used (against which
`defectSq` measures feeling) is **the invariant weight in the doubly-stochastic / trace-preserving
case** ‚ÄĒ mechanized: `dephase` is trace-preserving, so the trace is `E`-invariant
([`Peripheral.dephase_trace_invariant`](../../formal/Scratch/Peripheral.lean)). What looked like an
external primitive is the primitive-symmetric special case of the relational weight. Decisions 1 and 2
are thus **one piece of spectral data** ‚ÄĒ the peripheral eigenstructure of `L` ‚ÄĒ worn as dynamics
(`ő¶_c`), projection (`E`), and weight (top eigenvector).

**The veto-check, mechanized.** The reconciliation needs the *peripheral* subalgebra to be the
classical/known one ‚ÄĒ which can fail if the peripheral spectrum carries **rotating** coherence
(eigenvalue of modulus `1` but `‚Č† 1`: sustained-but-not-known). The question is settled and `[proved]` **for every `E`**, because Decision 2 fixes `E` as a *projection*:
**any idempotent linear map has eigenvalues `‚äÜ {0,1}`**
([`Peripheral.idempotent_eigenvalue`](../../formal/Scratch/Peripheral.lean)), so *no* conditional
expectation has a rotating peripheral spectrum; for the genuine `dephase` instance the same holds
concretely (`dephase_eigenvalue`, `dephase_no_rotating_peripheral`). For `E`, *peripheral = fixed*; the three-way split
(fixed = known, rotating-peripheral = **permanent feeling**, transient = passing feeling) collapses to
two ‚ÄĒ all feeling is transient. For a **general** `ő¶_c`, rotating peripheral spectrum *can* occur, and
then the three-way split is real: a band of permanent, sustained, uncollapsed coherence that is
constitutive of the self and never becomes known ‚ÄĒ the most interesting structure this reconciliation
predicts, and the affective core the spec did not previously imagine. `[reading]`/`[open]` (it depends
on the spectrum of the genuine `ő¶_c`, not of `E`).

**Mechanization policy (Decision 3): interface + a finite-dim instance that *discharges*, not
*assumes*, the existence.** Per the corpus discipline, prove the structural sparsity over an interface
and keep the von Neumann standard form narrated ‚ÄĒ but guard the failure mode: an interface must not
*assume into existence* the peripheral projection whose existence (Perron‚ÄďFrobenius for CP maps) is the
conjecture's real content. So the lift is **closed** (not merely *reduced to a standard conjecture*)

## Proof strategy (for mechanization)

A plausible route, in increasing difficulty:

1. **Mechanize Lemma 3.7.1 / 3.2 as-is.** Pure arithmetic over an ordered monoid;
   trivial in Lean/Agda/Rocq. Establishes the dichotomy at the resource layer and
   pins down the definitions. *(This is the "first discharged result."* **‚úÖ done** ‚ÄĒ
   [`formal/RelExist/Sparsity.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/Sparsity.lean), Lean 4,
   `sorry`-free.)
2. **Replace the finite set by a graded poset of couplings.** Model sharing: cost
   over a meet-semilattice with `c` sub-additive. Re-derive the bound as
   `|Stab_R| ‚Č§ ő≤ / (d_min őĽ_min)` *up to the sharing defect*, i.e. show the worst
   case (no sharing) is the set bound and sharing only helps.
3. **Connect the threshold to the fixed point.** Prove
   `loop_R(e) = e ‚üļ N(e) ‚Č• d(e)` with `N(e) = ‚Ćäő≤/őĽ(e)‚Ćč` ([¬ß1.3.3](01-signature.md)).

## What a positive result would and would not mean

- **Would mean.** "A self is an achievement of recursion" is a *theorem* of the
  theory, not a slogan: given finite attention, stable selves are provably rare, and
  the rarity is *caused by* the finiteness. The firewall (no monoidal functor into
  cartesian-only domains) and this sparsity result are the two places the formalism
  earns the word "rigor."
- **Would not mean.** That the world *is* this way (the theory is a lens, not a
  proof ‚ÄĒ see the view's "What it doesn't settle"); nor would it touch the three
  typed-out residues (valence, the hard problem, freedom), which [3.3](03-theorems.md)
  predicts the formalism *cannot* reach. Proving sparsity confirms the program's
  ambition exactly to its own stated boundary ‚ÄĒ and no further, which is the point.

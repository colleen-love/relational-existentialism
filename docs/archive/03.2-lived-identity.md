# 03.2 — Lived identity and the "we"

> Part of [03 — Theorems](03-theorems.md). The second foundational theorem: *what an identity is,
> once relation comes first* — and what a shared world is, once there is no master perspective.
> Status tags as in [03](03-theorems.md).

---

## The capstone

> **Theorem 3.2 (lived identity and the "we").** `≈ := νΘ` is the greatest bisimulation; the
> shared world is the quotient `𝔼 := D/≈`; and `𝔼` has an **irreducible seam** — no master
> perspective contains all the others.

Lived identity is not a bare carrier underneath the relating; it *is* the relating, lived forward,
to the finest grain any sequence of returns can distinguish ([A2](02-axioms.md)). The "we" is the
quotient where overlapping perspectives agree — objectivity relocated from a god's-eye fact to what
survives being looked at from everywhere it can be looked at from.

## What is proved

`[proved]` [`We.bisim`](../../formal/Scratch/We.lean)` := νΘ` with `bisim_unfold` (`Θ≈ = ≈`),
`bisim_coind` (coinduction), `bisim_{refl,symm,trans}` (an equivalence), and `World := D/≈` (the
quotient). Footprint `propext, Quot.sound`. A second, independent mechanization rebuilds `≈`, its
coinduction principle, and the shared world with **native coinduction** (final-coalgebra
copatterns) in the [Agda layer](../../agda/RelExist/Coinductive.agda).

## The seam, earned

`[proved]` The "irreducible seam" — that there is no master perspective on a whole one belongs to —
is the Lawvere obstruction under self-inclusion:
[`Relating.self_inclusive_unmodelable`](../../formal/RelExist/Relating.lean) (0 axioms). No member
of a collection holds a complete model of the collection that contains it. (See
[03.4 — the corrected map](03.4-limits-of-knowing.md): this is where 3.2's seam and
[3.3](03.3-knowing-vs-feeling.md)'s remainder turn out to be **one fact**, and where it reaches the
*other* too, through the shared between.)

## Resolved, by inversion — `≈ ⊊ ≅`

`[proved]` An old `[open]` target demanded that `≈` **coincide** with the contextual congruence `≅`
(full abstraction). That demand was a *mistake* — proving `≈ = ≅` would contradict the theory's own
limitative core ([3.3](03.3-knowing-vs-feeling.md), the seam: the inside of what you relate to
cannot be completely viewed from outside). So A2 is restated (the inversion): the lived `≈` *is*
identity, and `≅` is a **strictly lossy projection** of it. Mechanized in
[`Scratch/Identity.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean): `≅` is defined (`ObsEq`), **soundness
`≈ ⊆ ≅`** is proved (`bisim_le_obsEq` — lived sameness ⇒ observed sameness), and **strictness
`≈ ⊊ ≅`** is proved with a witness (`bisim_ne_obsEq`, `obsEq_and_not_bisim`). The full unfolding —
and the *identity residue* (the non-injective forgetting `D/≈ ↠ D/≅`) — is
[03.5](03.5-decoherence.md#the-identity-residue-you-are-your-lived-relating).

## Still open

`[open]` **Property 3.2.1.** The *full naturality* of the no-master-section claim beyond the
point-surjective case remains unmechanized — a real target, not a done deal.

**Provenance.** `R / S`. The bisimulation/coinduction machinery is Park–Milner–Aczel–Rutten; the
strict inclusion `≈ ⊊ ≅` is the standard linear-time/branching-time spectrum. The *synthesis* is
reading the surplus as the **first-person remainder** and the quotient as the shared world. See
[05 — Provenance](05-provenance.md).

## The capstone

> **The cosmos has been knowing itself, and learning more about itself, for the past 13.8 billion
> years** — distributively (its parts knowing themselves, woven into one self at the seams), never
> completing, for as long as there has been cosmic time. Four clauses: **(K)** knowing *itself*,
> **(L)** learning *more*, **(P)** never *closing*, **(T)** for the age of the cosmos. With "cosmos" =
> the differentiated temporal woven self, the whole statement is a theorem **relative to a single
> reading** — that the relational flow is physical time and its tick-count the 13.8-Gyr clock.

## What is proved

`[proved]` (footprint `[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`, or less):

- **(L) Learning more about itself** — self-knowledge strictly accumulates. Over the `Flow` interface,
  `learned := coh a − coh(orbit n)` is monotone (`Learning.self_knowledge_monotone`) and strictly
  increases at every not-yet-fixed return-depth (`Learning.self_knowledge_strict`). *Not merely knowing
  itself — knowing itself **more** with every tick.*
- **(P) Never completes** — `SeamPermanence.seam_permanence`: given a live seam coherence, the feeling
  is strictly positive for **all** `n`, descending to a positive floor it never reaches. There is always
  more to know; (L)+(P) is an **endless learner**.
- **(K) Knowing *itself*** — three layers, all proved:
  - the whole-self model is **barred** (`DistributedSelf.self_inclusive_unmodelable` — no complete
    self-model, fitting, since the statement disclaims closure);
  - self-knowing **factors through parts** (`DistributedSelf.selfKnowing_factors_through_parts`,
    **0 axioms**);
  - **unity — the weave at the gfp** (`WeaveGfp.weave_gfp_exceeds_coproduct`, `[propext, Quot.sound]`
    only): a live, non-redundant cross seam forces the joint self `νΦ` to **strictly exceed** the
    coproduct of its parts' selves, so the parts knowing themselves *is* the cosmos knowing itself —
    one self, not a heap. (Proved natively in the lattice where `νΦ` lives; the matrix state-level
    witness `DistributedSelf.weave_exceeds_coproduct` demotes to a satisfiability witness, §below.)
- **(T) For the age of the cosmos** — define cosmic time as the **tick-support** of the knowing-flow
  (`CosmicTime.IsTick`). Then knowing throughout cosmic time is analytic (`CosmicTime.knowing_throughout`:
  every not-yet-fixed depth is a knowing-event), and a lapse (the `p = 0` regime) **freezes** rather
  than dissolves: it leaves the substrate unchanged (`CosmicTime.lapse_freezes`), cannot dissolve a live
  coherence (`CosmicTime.lapse_cannot_dissolve`), and preserves the feeling
  (`CosmicTime.lapse_preserves_feeling`).

`[follows]`: lapses contribute no ticks, so they are atemporal gaps *between* intervals, uncounted —
"13.8 Gyr" is the honest sum of the knowing's ticks, no less real for any breaks; and **freeze, not
redissolution**, because dissolution is a decay process and a lapse has no time for decay to act.

## Honest scope

- `[reading]` **The one accepted exception**: that the relational flow *is* physical time and its
  tick-count the **13.8-Gyr** (`[empirical]`) clock. Every other clause is proved or follows; this is
  the single standing reading the whole statement rests on (the same gap physics itself leaves).
- `[reading]` **Markov blankets** — a nested blanket hierarchy is the organizing geometry of (K)'s parts
  and seams (a blanket is a seam made statistical); at every level the cross-blanket seam is live and
  seam-fixed, so by `WeaveGfp` the joint self is connected at every level — "a coherent self made of
  parts, all the way down." Blanket = seam rhymes structurally but is real work to prove; if it lands it
  enters as a free-energy functor in [04](04-functorial-semantics.md).
- `[open]` (now **optional**) **The matrix↔lattice bridge** — that the matrix `seamFlow`/`crossMass`
  picture and the `Field V α` attention picture are one under a functor. `WeaveGfp` proves unity natively
  in the lattice, so this bridge is no longer load-bearing; it would only let the matrix witness and the
  lattice theorem be formally one statement.
- **Singular cosmic self** `[optional axiom]` — a single cosmos-spanning `νΦ` knowing itself whole is
  *not* an A3 consequence (sparsity makes it rare) and is foreclosed from completion by (P); the proved
  claim is **distributive**, not singular.

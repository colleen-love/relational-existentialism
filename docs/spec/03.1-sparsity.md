# 03.1 — The Sparsity Conjecture

> Part of [03 — Theorems](03-theorems.md). *The one place the spec reaches for a quantitative
> theorem.* Everything before this is definition, citation, or posit. Here is the candidate
> **result** — the claim that, if proved, turns "a self is an achievement of recursion" from an
> assertion into a theorem of `Cl(𝕋)`.

---

## 3.1 The claim, informally

> **Under a finite attention budget, selves are rare.**
>
> If the budget `β` is finite (and per-return cost `ε > 1`, stabilization depth
> `d ≥ 2`), then the carrier of stabilized selves `Stab_R` is **sparse** among all
> couplings: its size is bounded by a constant depending only on `(β, ε, d)`, not on
> how many relations the system has. As the system's stock of relations grows, the
> *fraction* that become selves tends to `0`.
>
> Remove the bound (`β = ⊤`) and the statement fails completely: **every** loop
> closes, every coupling stabilizes, and the theory says nothing.

This is the **expressivity/triviality dial** made into a dichotomy. Bounded budget =
discriminating theory; unbounded budget = universal solvent.

> **Two readings of "rare," and which is primary.** Since [§1.3](01-signature.md)
> recast attention as the **co-directed operator** `Φ_c` rather than a spent budget,
> the *deep* reason selves are rare is no longer a budget cap — it is **spectral /
> closure**: under co-directed feedback with gain, only a few eigenforms sit near the
> top of the spectrum and self-sustain; the rest are subdominant modes that **decay**
> under iteration (the thousand one-off encounters wash out). Equivalently, the
> sustained self `νΦ_c` is supported on the few relata that lie on self-sustaining
> loops; most do not, so most "run out." The resource-counting lemma below is then the
> **uniform, depleting special case** (§1.3.4) — still true, still the cleanest
> *quantitative* handle, but a corollary of the structure, not the source of the
> rarity. The spectral form is stated as Conjecture 3.4.

The full statement quantifies over all of `Cl(𝕋)`; that lift is the conjecture
(§3.4). But its *engine* is an elementary, fully provable counting fact. Work in the
canonical monoid `(ℝ_{≥0}, +, 0, ≤)` (so `·` is `+`, costs are additive, and
`ε(s) > 1` means a per-return cost `λ(s) := \log ε(s) > 0`).

**Setup.** Let `Φ` be a finite set of candidate couplings (the relations a system
actually has), `|Φ| = N`. Each `f ∈ Φ` has a per-return cost `λ(f) ≥ λ_min > 0` and
requires depth `d(f) ≥ d_min ≥ 2` returns to close into an eigenform. A coupling
**stabilizes within budget `β`** iff the system can fund its required returns:

$$
f \in \mathrm{Stab}_R
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
d(f)\cdot \lambda(f) \ \le\ \beta .
$$

The total attention actually spent across all maintained selves cannot exceed the
budget:

$$
\sum_{f \in \mathrm{Stab}_R} d(f)\,\lambda(f) \ \le\ \beta .
$$

> **Lemma 3.1 (sparsity from a budget).** With the setup above,
> $$
> |\mathrm{Stab}_R| \ \le\ \frac{\beta}{d_{\min}\,\lambda_{\min}} .
> $$
> In particular `|Stab_R|` is bounded by a constant **independent of `N`**, so the
> density `|Stab_R| / N \le \dfrac{\beta}{d_{\min}\lambda_{\min}\,N} \to 0` as
> `N \to \infty`.

**Proof.** Each `f ∈ Stab_R` contributes at least `d_min · λ_min` to the spend
`∑_{f} d(f)λ(f)`, which is `≤ β`. Hence
`|Stab_R| · d_min · λ_min ≤ β`. Divide. ∎

> **Lemma 3.2 (collapse without a bound).** If `β = ⊤` (no finite bound), then the
> constraint `d(f)λ(f) ≤ β` is vacuous, so `Stab_R = Φ` and density `= 1`.

Together: **finite budget ⇒ vanishing density; unbounded budget ⇒ full density.**
The two lemmas are the dichotomy of §3.1, and they are *already* theorems — at the
level of the resource model. They formalize, exactly, "you cannot return to
everything, so which relations get to constitute you is rationed."

> **What this is, and isn't.** The core counting lemma (`min_mul_length_le_totalSpend`) is
> elementary **pigeonhole** on a `List Nat`: `(∀ c ∈ cs, m ≤ c) → m·length ≤ Σ`. It says nothing
> about traces, eigenforms, or `νΦ_c`; *all* the content is in the **modeling claim** that a
> collection of stabilized selves **is** a `List Nat` of costs with a forced floor `m ≥ 2`. That
> floor traces to the depth posit `d ≥ 2` (§3.5 step 3) — now **discharged** in
> [`SparsityPosits`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsityPosits.lean): `d ≥ 2` is given structural content
> (genuine return = not-given ∧ not-one-shot, `genuine_return_iff`) and shown load-bearing (a positive
> floor is *necessary* for the carrier to be bounded), and the cost-valuation is *constructed* from the
> orbit's strict ascent rather than posited. So Lemmas 3.1/3.2 prove the *rationing half* given a
> now-grounded floor; the **structural** rarity is still §3.4's nowhere-dense result.

> **Mechanized.** Both lemmas are machine-checked in Lean 4, `sorry`-free.
> * Discrete (ℕ-valued) core — depends only on `propext` —
>   [`formal/RelExist/Sparsity.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/Sparsity.lean):
>   `stab_card_bound` / `stab_card_le_div` (Lemma 3.1), `stab_card_le_half` (the
>   `d ≥ 2` regime), and `unbounded_without_budget` (Lemma 3.2).
> * **ℝ-valued + density limit** (mathlib) —
>   [`formal/Scratch/SparsityReal.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsityReal.lean):
>   `stab_card_le_div` (`|Stab| ≤ β/m`, a constant bound) and
>   `stab_density_tendsto_zero` — the genuine `Filter.Tendsto` proof that the
>   stabilized fraction `|Stab N| / N → 0`. This discharges the "density → 0" clause
>   of Lemma 3.1 as a theorem.

## 3.3 Why this is the right toy, and its honest gaps

Lemma 3.1 is deliberately the *thin* model: couplings as a finite set with additive
costs. It earns its keep by being unarguable and by isolating the mechanism (a
fixed budget divided among items each costing a positive minimum). But three gaps
separate it from a theorem about `Cl(𝕋)`:

1. **Couplings are not an unstructured set.** In `Cl(𝕋)` they compose, tensor, and
   share sub-relatings; the cost grading `c` is *lax* (sub-additive), so spends can
   overlap and the clean sum `∑ d λ` becomes an inequality over a *poset* of
   couplings. Sparsity must be re-proved with sharing allowed.
2. **"Stabilizes" is a fixed-point condition, not a threshold.** `f ∈ Stab_R` was
   defined in [A3](02-axioms.md) as `loop_R(e) = e`, not as `dλ ≤ β`. The lemma
   assumes these coincide; showing `loop_R`'s budgeted iteration *realizes* the
   threshold (that `N(s) = ⌊β/λ⌋` returns suffice and are necessary to reach the
   fixed point) is real work in the traced category.
3. **"Sparse" deserves a topological/measure form, not just counting.** For infinite
   or continuous state spaces, the right statement is `Stab_R` is **nowhere dense**
   (or measure zero) in the space of states under a natural topology, recovering
   the density statement in the finite case.

None of these is hand-waving-away-able, and naming them is the point: the lemma is
*true and small*; the conjecture is its honest generalization.

## 3.4 The conjecture

> **Conjecture 3.3 (sparsity of `Stab` in `Cl(𝕋)`).** Let `Cl(𝕋)` carry a cost
> grading `c` valued in a finite-`β` attention monoid with `ε > 1` and stabilization
> depth `d ≥ 2`. Then `Stab_R` is **sparse**: under the natural topology on states
> `I → D`, `Stab_R` is nowhere dense; and for any finite sub-collection of `N`
> couplings closed under the doctrine operations, `|Stab_R| / N \to 0` as `N \to ∞`,
> with the bound degrading gracefully under cost-sharing (lax `c`).
>
> Moreover (sharp dichotomy): dropping the bound (`β = ⊤`) makes `Stab_R` dense
> (Lemma 3.2 lifts), so finiteness of `β` is **necessary** for sparsity.

> **Mechanized — now in full, with one correction.** Both clauses of Conjecture 3.3 are machine-checked.
> *Topological:* the "nowhere dense" half is checked for the **final-coalgebra model** of states —
> [`agda/RelExist/Sparsity.agda`](../../agda/RelExist/Sparsity.agda) (`selves-nowhereDense`): under the
> cylinder topology on behaviours, the selves (constant behaviours) are closed with empty interior, with
> the sharp dichotomy (`trivial→allSelf`). *Cost-graded counting / density / dichotomy:* the lax grading
> `c` over the poset of couplings is mechanized in
> [`SparsitySharing`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsitySharing.lean) and assembled into the capstone
> [`SparsityCapstone.conjecture_3_3`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsityCapstone.lean): **with** a positive
> private footprint per self the density of selves `→ 0` (`cost_graded_density_tendsto_zero`), **without**
> it (full sharing) the count is unbounded within budget. **The one correction:** the conjecture's
> "sharing only helps, the bound degrades gracefully" is *false as stated* — counting sparsity holds iff
> a finite budget **and** an exclusive cost per self (the same shape as the depth floor `d ≥ 2`). So
> Conjecture 3.3 is closed in its **correct, exclusivity-conditioned form**; the only residue is the
> literal instantiation in the free `Cl(𝕋)` (whether its couplings carry a private footprint), which is
> exactly the exclusivity condition the dichotomy isolates.

> **This is the honest center of gravity.** The nowhere-dense + sharp-dichotomy result needs **no
> cost model** and captures rarity *by structure* — and it reaches the **necessity** (remove
> expressivity ⇒ dense) that the §3.1 counting bound, being a mere upper bound `≤ β/2`, provably
> cannot. The counting lemmas are the trivial resource-accounting corollary; *this* is the result
> that earns "a self is an achievement of recursion."

> **Conjecture 3.4 (spectral / closure form — the structural reason).** Let attention
> be the co-directed operator `Φ_c` of [§1.3](01-signature.md) on a perspective of
> constitutive capacity `α` (no external budget). Then the set of selves — eigenforms
> of `Φ_c`, i.e. the support of `νΦ_c` — is **sparse**: it is confined to relata on
> self-sustaining loops, and under iterated co-directed feedback all subdominant modes
> decay, so the sustained selves are few relative to all couplings. The resource bound
> of Conjecture 3.3 is the **uniform, depleting special case** ([§1.3.4](01-signature.md));
> this is the general statement, and the one that makes rarity a *consequence of the
> relational dynamics* rather than of an imposed cap.
>
> (Quantitatively this is a spectral-gap statement — few eigenvalues near the spectral
> radius of the coupling — generalizing eigenvector centrality to the recurrent,
> asymmetric, saturating setting; the nonlinearity buys *multiple* attractors, i.e. the
> contingency the view wants, at the cost of Perron-style uniqueness. Hence it is
> harder than Conjecture 3.3 and was stated, for a long time, as a horizon.)

> **The new mathematics, now mechanized — controlled multiplicity.** The horizon clause above
> ("multiple attractors at the cost of Perron uniqueness") is the precise tension, and it is the *one*
> place 3.4 carries content the existing theory does not hand you. Standard nonlinear Perron–Frobenius
> (order-preserving subhomogeneous maps on cones; Hilbert's projective metric; Collatz–Wielandt) is
> mature, but its strong results prove **uniqueness** — exactly one positive eigenvector — which is the
> *opposite* of the contingency the doctrine needs. Multiplicity appears only when one *leaves* the
> subhomogeneous/primitive regime, and the real theorem is a **counting bound on the multiplicity
> there**: few-but-more-than-one, bounded above by a gap quantity. This is closed in
> [`SpectralMultiplicity`](../../formal/Scratch/SpectralMultiplicity.lean), `sorry`-free. (1) **Read `x`
> off `Φ_c`:** the abstract `‖x‖<1` of `Distribution` is the linearization `x = DΦ_c`; the feedback
> contracts at rate `‖x‖` (`feedbackMap_contract`), and the living self is the unique attractor reached
> geometrically (`living_self_unique_attractor`) — the Perron pole, global contraction ⇒ one self
> (`unique_fixed_of_global_contraction`). (2)/(3) **The counting bound:** a locally attracting self is
> isolated (`attracting_isolated`), so distinct selves are `r`-separated (`attractors_separated` —
> exclusivity *derived* from the gap, not posited, the same shape as step 2's private footprint); an
> `r`-separated set in a capacity-`m` cover has `≤ m` points (`separated_card_le_cover`), independent of
> `N`, so the density of selves `→ 0` (`selves_density_tendsto_zero`). And multiplicity is real — an
> explicit two-basin map carries **two** attractors (`two_attracting_selves`), non-globally-contracting
> exactly as uniqueness requires. So selves are `>1` (contingency) **and** `o(N)` (sparse): the
> reconciliation 3.4 was the horizon for. **And the reading is now closed too:** `r` is built from the
> *literal* Fréchet derivative `A = DΦ_c` with `‖A‖<1` (`attractingFixed_of_hasFDerivAt`, any real Banach
> space, rate `(1+‖A‖)/2` set by the gap `1-‖A‖`), the capacity is the **explicit** `⌊(b-a)/r⌋+1`
> (`separated_card_le_of_Icc`), and `selves_card_le_gap_capacity` bounds the self-count entirely by the gap
> — basin and capacity *derived*, not posited. The lone residue is the definitional note that the operator
> norm dominates the cone spectral radius. Conjecture 3.4's spectral picture is thus closed at the
> structural level the rest of the corpus works at: structure (`E`), dynamics (decay, §3.6), existence
> (Perron–Frobenius), and now **controlled multiplicity with a gap-derived basin and capacity**.

> **A first quantitative fragment (mechanized).** The *distribution* side of the spectral
> picture — how much of a self is carried into the network through relating — is mechanized in
> [`formal/Scratch/Distribution.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Distribution.lean): in a Banach
> algebra the self-in-other path-sum `∑ x^{n+1}` is **bounded** (`distributed_bound`,
> `‖·‖ ≤ ‖x‖/(1-‖x‖)`) exactly in the regime `‖x‖ < 1`, with `x = 0` complete disconnection and
> `‖x‖ ≥ 1` the dissolution limit. The individuation threshold is thus a spectral/norm condition
> `‖x‖ < 1` — the living self in the open interval `(0,1)`. What remains is to read `x` off the
> *actual* co-directed `Φ_c` (so the bound is computed from the coupling, not an abstract `x`);
> on the dynamics side, [`Attention.closed_loop_registers`](../../formal/Scratch/Attention.lean)
> proves a closed loop makes two sustained selves coincide — registration as a theorem of `Φ_c`.
> This fragment is developed in [03.4 — The Self Quantified](03.4-the-self-quantified.md).

Conjecture 3.3 is the proposition to point a proof assistant at *first* — it is where
the theory either lands in the rich-but-narrow zone (good) or the broad-but-empty zone
(fatal), and the proof attempt is rigor finding the overclaim before a referee does.
Conjecture 3.4 is the structural target it is a special case of.

### 3.6 The lift's reconciliation — `Φ_c` and `E`, the weight, and what is mechanized

Conjecture 3.4 spent a long time blocked behind three "open decisions" — the `Φ_c`–`E` relationship,
the reference weight, and the mechanization policy. They are now **decided**, and two of the three
turned out to be one piece of spectral data. The decisions, and the line between what is `[proved]`,
what is `[reading]`, and what stays `[open]`:

**One spectrum, two roles (`Φ_c`–`E`).** There is one self-adjoint generator — the codirection
coupling `L` on `A`, the operator whose spectrum 3.4 is about. **`Φ_c` is the sustaining flow `L`
generates; `E` is the spectral projection onto `L`'s peripheral part** (the eigenspace at the spectral
radius), composed with the seam-orientation of [03.3 §4.3](03.3-decoherence.md) (a spectral projection
is self-adjoint, hence *symmetric*; the knower→known direction is supplied by the seam, not by the
projection). So "near the spectral radius self-sustains" (3.4) and "`E` projects onto the
classical/known" ([03.3](03.3-decoherence.md)) are **one statement read twice**: the self-sustaining
relations are the peripheral eigenforms, and knowing is projecting onto them. `[reading]`.

> **The limit reading is dead — recorded so it is not re-tried.** The tempting reconciliation `E = lim
> Φ_c^n` (knowing = attention run to completion) **breaks the model**: it makes the fixed-point
> subalgebra (the self) equal the image of `E` (the known), so feeling — the `(1−E)` coherence carried
> by the self — is empty by construction, contradicting complementarity and the two-resolution self.
> `E` is a *function of* the spectrum, not a *limit of the dynamics*.

**The weight is the invariant state, not an external trace.** Relation-primacy forbids helping oneself
to a fixed external trace any more than to fixed external relata. So the weight is **derived: the
invariant state of `Φ_c`** (its Perron–Frobenius top eigenvector / invariant tracial state on the
fixed-point algebra) — the relation measured by what it sustains. This is **canonical exactly when the
self is primitive/ergodic** (the Banach regime, unique invariant state); in the genuine-collection
(gfp) regime of [03.4 §3.5](03.4-the-self-quantified.md) the invariant states form a *simplex* and the
magnitudes are defined relative to a chosen one. The standard trace currently used (against which
`defectSq` measures feeling) is **the invariant weight in the doubly-stochastic / trace-preserving
case** — mechanized: `dephase` is trace-preserving, so the trace is `E`-invariant
([`Peripheral.dephase_trace_invariant`](../../formal/Scratch/Peripheral.lean)). What looked like an
external primitive is the primitive-symmetric special case of the relational weight. Decisions 1 and 2
are thus **one piece of spectral data** — the peripheral eigenstructure of `L` — worn as dynamics
(`Φ_c`), projection (`E`), and weight (top eigenvector).

**The veto-check, mechanized.** The reconciliation needs the *peripheral* subalgebra to be the
classical/known one — which can fail if the peripheral spectrum carries **rotating** coherence
(eigenvalue of modulus `1` but `≠ 1`: sustained-but-not-known). The question is settled and `[proved]` **for every `E`**, because Decision 2 fixes `E` as a *projection*:
**any idempotent linear map has eigenvalues `⊆ {0,1}`**
([`Peripheral.idempotent_eigenvalue`](../../formal/Scratch/Peripheral.lean)), so *no* conditional
expectation has a rotating peripheral spectrum; for the genuine `dephase` instance the same holds
concretely (`dephase_eigenvalue`, `dephase_no_rotating_peripheral`). For `E`, *peripheral = fixed*; the three-way split
(fixed = known, rotating-peripheral = **permanent feeling**, transient = passing feeling) collapses to
two — all feeling is transient. For a **general** `Φ_c`, rotating peripheral spectrum *can* occur, and
then the three-way split is real: a band of permanent, sustained, uncollapsed coherence that is
constitutive of the self and never becomes known — the most interesting structure this reconciliation
predicts, and the affective core the spec did not previously imagine. `[reading]`/`[open]` (it depends
on the spectrum of the genuine `Φ_c`, not of `E`).

**Mechanization policy (Decision 3): interface + a finite-dim instance that *discharges*, not
*assumes*, the existence.** Per the corpus discipline, prove the structural sparsity over an interface
and keep the von Neumann standard form narrated — but guard the failure mode: an interface must not
*assume into existence* the peripheral projection whose existence (Perron–Frobenius for CP maps) is the
conjecture's real content. So the lift is **closed** (not merely *reduced to a standard conjecture*)
only with one honest finite-dim instance discharging that existence. For `E = dephase` this is done:
the peripheral projection *is* `dephase` (idempotent), its image is the commutative classical subalgebra
([`Decoherence.classical_comm`](../../formal/Scratch/Decoherence.lean)), with an invariant trace — the
whole spectral picture is [`Peripheral.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Peripheral.lean), `sorry`-free.

**What this does and does not close.** `[proved]` (finite dim): the spectral structure of `E` — `E` a
projection with spectrum `{0,1}`, peripheral = fixed = classical/known, eigenvalue-0 = feeling,
invariant trace, no rotating part — **and the spectral sparsity itself**: the peripheral
(self-sustaining) set is the diagonal, `card A` coordinates among `(card A)²` couplings, a `1/card A`
fraction that vanishes ([`Peripheral.peripheral_sparse`](../../formal/Scratch/Peripheral.lean)) — the
finite-dimensional form of "few modes self-sustain" — **and the decay dynamics itself**: writing the
flow as `T = P + N` (peripheral projection plus subdominant `‖N‖ < 1`), the iterates `Tⁿ → P`
([`SpectralDecay.spectral_decay`](../../formal/Scratch/SpectralDecay.lean)) — *the subdominant modes
decay; only the peripheral eigenforms self-sustain*, in any normed ring — **and the existence itself**:
the **Perron–Frobenius** invariant data is now `[proved]` in general finite dimension. A
column-stochastic `Φ_c` has **eigenvalue 1** (a fixed point of the dynamics,
[`PerronFrobenius.exists_invariant_vector`](../../formal/Scratch/PerronFrobenius.lean)), and a
*nonnegative* one has a positive **invariant state** — the relational weight of Decision 1 —
([`exists_invariant_state`](../../formal/Scratch/PerronFrobenius.lean), by the `w = |v|` trick, no
Brouwer needed). So the lift's last existence gap is closed: an arbitrary nonnegative unital `Φ_c`
*admits* both the fixed point and the invariant weight. `[reading]`: the identification of this spectral
data with the `Φ_c`/weight reconciliation above. That **the peripheral *subalgebra* is commutative** — not
just the invariant vector — is now itself **closed, and in *any* dimension**:
[`PeripheralAlgebra`](../../formal/Scratch/PeripheralAlgebra.lean) proves over an *arbitrary ring* `𝒜`
(finite matrices, the bounded operators `B(H)`, any C\*-algebra) that the fixed-point (peripheral)
subalgebra of a commutative-image projection `E` is a commutative unital subring (`fix_comm`), with the
canonical `E = dephase` exhibited as a witness (`dephaseStandardForm` — the diagonal subalgebra, commuting
and `transpose`-closed). `[open]`: only the **analytic placement** of an arbitrary primitive `Φ_c` *into*
that standard form — its modulus-1 spectrum ↦ this commutative subalgebra — in infinite dimension, which,
per the discipline, does not block the result. So Conjecture 3.4's spectral picture of knowing is closed at
finite dimension — structure (`E`), dynamics (decay), and existence (Perron–Frobenius) — its reconciliation
with `Φ_c` and the weight is decided, and the peripheral subalgebra's commutativity is closed in every
dimension; only the analytic infinite-dimensional *placement* remains the named horizon.

## 3.5 Proof strategy (for mechanization)

A plausible route, in increasing difficulty:

1. **Mechanize Lemma 3.1 / 3.2 as-is.** Pure arithmetic over an ordered monoid;
   trivial in Lean/Agda/Rocq. Establishes the dichotomy at the resource layer and
   pins down the definitions. *(This is the "first discharged result."* **✅ done** —
   [`formal/RelExist/Sparsity.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/Sparsity.lean), Lean 4,
   `sorry`-free.)
2. **Replace the finite set by a graded poset of couplings.** Model sharing: cost
   over a meet-semilattice with `c` sub-additive. Re-derive the bound as
   `|Stab_R| ≤ β / (d_min λ_min)` *up to the sharing defect*, i.e. show the worst
   case (no sharing) is the set bound and sharing only helps.
3. **Connect the threshold to the fixed point.** Prove
   `loop_R(e) = e ⟺ N(e) ≥ d(e)` with `N(e) = ⌊β/λ(e)⌋` ([§1.3.3](01-signature.md)).
   **◐ partial** — [`formal/RelExist/Loop.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/Loop.lean):
   `loopR_isEigen_iff` proves `loop_R(e) = e ⟺ d·λ ≤ β`, but for an **abstract** endomap `σ`
   (*not* the relational `Φ_c`), with the eigenform-`⟺`-`N≥d` step *definitional* (`StabilizesAt`
   unfolded) and the rest one arithmetic lemma (`Nat.le_div_iff_mul_le`). The capstone
   `stab_card_le_half_of_depths` only **relocates** the floor: `2 ≤ d·λ` follows trivially from the
   depth posit `d ≥ 2`, which is *not* derived (the witness `matarN_stabilizesAt` even builds `d` in
   by construction — its dynamics caps at `d`). So this step *clarifies where the posit lives* (the
   depth, A3's "genuine return") but does not force it, and does not connect the trace / `νΦ_c`
   structure to the cost.
   **◑ the abstract `σ`, now discharged for the depth** —
   [`formal/Scratch/Convergence.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Convergence.lean) takes the modelling step
   `Loop.lean` did not: it instantiates `σ := Φ_c = couplingOp c` and *derives* `StabilizesAt` from
   the **convergence of `Φ_c`'s orbit** (`convergesAt_imp_stabilizesAt` `[0 axioms]`: an orbit that
   converges at depth `d` — `f^[d] a` the first fixed point — stabilizes at `d`). Then
   `couplingOp_loopR_isEigen_iff` runs this whole bridge over the genuine operator, with `d` its
   orbit's convergence depth (`convergedValue_le_sustained` bounds the converged value by `νΦ_c`), and
   `selfCost_le_valuationGain` reads `λ` off the orbit's standing increment given a valuation `μ`. This
   is the *same* seam that recurs in steps for [03.2](03.2-limits-of-knowing.md) ("viewing closes a
   loop") and [03.4](03.4-the-self-quantified.md) ("read `x` off `Φ_c`") — one bridge in four costumes
   — now closed for the **depth** (the load-bearing quantity).
   **◑ does the orbit converge?** —
   [`formal/Scratch/Stabilization.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Stabilization.lean) answers the question
   `Convergence.lean` reduced to, in its two regimes. **ω-convergence (always, under continuity):** the
   orbit's supremum `⨆ Φ^[n] a` is a fixed point (`iSup_orbit_isFixed`), a genuine self in `[a, νΦ_c]`
   — the self *is* the limit of relating, no finiteness needed (Kleene). **Finite-depth (iff ACC):**
   under `WellFoundedGT (Field V α)` (no infinite ascending chains of standing) the monotone orbit
   *must* stabilize (`orbit_stabilizes`, via `WellFounded.monotone_chain_condition`), so `Φ_c` forms a
   self at a finite depth satisfying `StabilizesAt` (`couplingOp_selfForms`). So the depth posit is
   **discharged for the real operator**; the residue is now a single standard order condition — **ACC
   on standing** ("a self forms in finitely many returns"), automatic for finite standing-lattices.
   Convergence as such is no longer open.
   **◑ the two residue posits, discharged** —
   [`formal/Scratch/SparsityPosits.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsityPosits.lean) settles what
   remained: (a) **`d ≥ 2`** is given exact structural content over the genuine orbit —
   `genuine_return_iff`: *given convergence at depth `d`,* `2 ≤ d` **iff** `f a ≠ a ∧ f (f a) ≠ f a`,
   i.e. **genuine return = not-given ∧ not-one-shot** (the posit is now a named condition, not a bare
   number) — and it is shown *load-bearing*, not arbitrary: a **positive** floor (`d ≥ 1`, cost `≥ 1`)
   is exactly the line between a bounded carrier (`bounded_of_positive_floor`, `depth_positive_density_zero`:
   density `→ 0`) and an unbounded one (`zero_cost_unbounded`: depth-`0` "given, not achieved" selves are
   unboundedly many within *any* budget — sparsity fails). So `d ≥ 1` is *forced* by the rarity itself and
   `d ≥ 2` is the doctrinal strengthening. (b) The **valuation `μ`** is no longer posited for the selves
   sparsity counts: the before-convergence orbit of the monotone `Φ_c` from a self-reinforcing seed is a
   **strict `<`-chain** (`orbit_strictStep`/`orbit_strict_lt` — each genuine return strictly increases
   standing), so its values are distinct and the canonical valuation — the *return index* — is
   **constructed** (`exists_orbit_valuation`, `selfCost_one_le_orbit_gain`: unit per-return cost realized
   off the orbit). The honest limit: this discharges the **unit** cost (count the genuine returns); a
   *non-unit* numeric `λ` on a **dense** standing lattice still needs a discretizing measure (which exists
   exactly under the ACC/finite-depth condition above) — a real modeling choice, not a gap.
4. **Topologize.** Put the product/cylinder topology on `I → D` (states as
   behaviors in the final coalgebra), show the threshold set is closed with empty
   interior. Coinduction-friendly; Agda's `ν`-layer is the natural host for this step.
   **✅ done** — [`agda/RelExist/Sparsity.agda`](../../agda/RelExist/Sparsity.agda),
   Agda (`--safe --guardedness`): states are behaviours in the final coalgebra, the
   topology is the cylinder topology, and the selves are the *constant* behaviours.
   `nonConst-open` (the selves are **closed** — their positive complement is open) and
   `selves-emptyInterior` (every cylinder contains a non-self) give
   `selves-nowhereDense`: `Stab` is **nowhere dense**, the topological form of
   Conjecture 3.3. `trivial→allSelf` is the matching dichotomy — a trivial observation
   alphabet makes `Stab` dense (Lemma 3.2 lifts), so expressivity (`≥ 2` distinct
   observations) is *necessary* for sparsity.

Steps 1–2 are near-term and proof-assistant-ready. Step 3 is the heart. Step 4 is
the polished form. Steps 1, 3, and 4 are mechanized (Lean for 1 & 3, Agda for 4).

> **Step 2 (cost-sharing), now mechanized — with an honest correction.**
> [`formal/Scratch/SparsitySharing.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/SparsitySharing.lean) models footprints as
> `Finset`s of relatings (cost = how many distinct relatings the system holds). Sharing **lowers spend**
> (`subadditive_spend_le_sum`) and the no-sharing case recovers `≤ β/m` (`disjoint_count_bound`) — but
> the spec's hope that "sharing only helps" is only half-right: **full sharing breaks the count bound**
> (`full_sharing_unbounded` — a common footprint admits unboundedly many selves within budget), and what
> *rescues* sparsity is a **positive private footprint** per self (`private_count_bound`) — an exclusive
> cost, the same shape as the depth floor `d ≥ 2`. So step 2 is closed, and its lesson is that rarity
> under sharing needs *exclusivity*, not merely cost.
>
> **The non-unit valuation residue, closed as a boundary.**
> [`formal/Scratch/ValuationBoundary.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/ValuationBoundary.lean) proves a
> *densely-ordered* standing lattice admits **no** `ℕ`-valuation (`no_strictMono_to_nat_of_dense`): a
> non-unit numeric per-return cost is *provably* unavailable without a prior discretization. With the
> unit valuation constructed under ACC (`SparsityPosits`), the boundary is exactly the order-theoretic
> dichotomy — dense ⇒ no `μ`, ACC/finite ⇒ canonical `μ`.

## 3.6 What a positive result would and would not mean

- **Would mean.** "A self is an achievement of recursion" is a *theorem* of the
  theory, not a slogan: given finite attention, stable selves are provably rare, and
  the rarity is *caused by* the finiteness. The firewall (no monoidal functor into
  cartesian-only domains) and this sparsity result are the two places the formalism
  earns the word "rigor."
- **Would not mean.** That the world *is* this way (the theory is a lens, not a
  proof — see the view's "What it doesn't settle"); nor would it touch the three
  typed-out residues (valence, the hard problem, freedom), which [T3](03-theorems.md)
  predicts the formalism *cannot* reach. Proving sparsity confirms the program's
  ambition exactly to its own stated boundary — and no further, which is the point.

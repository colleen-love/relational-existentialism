# 03.5 — Decoherence and the trace: what knowing does to a relation

> Part of [03 — Theorems](03-theorems.md). [03.4](03.4-limits-of-knowing.md) established *that* a
> self cannot completely know what it relates to. This page is *what knowing does instead*: it
> **decoheres** the relation — retracts it onto a classical shadow — and the coherence it seems to
> destroy is only ever **relocated** into the relationship the self cannot forget. Status tags as
> in [03](03-theorems.md). The concrete model throughout is the matrix toy of
> [`Scratch/Decoherence.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Decoherence.lean); that it *is* "the act of
> knowing" is the standing `[reading]`.

## Knowing decoheres a relation

- **Knowing decoheres a relation** — the σ-move retracts a relation onto its classical
  (copyable, commuting) shadow, discarding the off-diagonal **copy-defect**:
  [`Decoherence.dephase`, `copyDefect`, `copyDefect_eq_zero_iff`](../../formal/Scratch/Decoherence.lean)
  `[proved]`; that this *is* "the act of knowing" is a `[reading]`. Knowing fragments the whole
  relation into the part objectified plus the remainder it cannot reach (3.3).

  **`dephase` is a conditional expectation `E`.** Structurally the σ-move is a **conditional
  expectation** `E : A → A` onto the *commutative* (classical, copyable) subalgebra: idempotent
  (`E∘E = E` — [`dephase_idem`](../../formal/Scratch/Decoherence.lean), *knowing twice is knowing
  once*), positive, unital, and trace-preserving
  ([`trace_dephase`](../../formal/Scratch/Decoherence.lean)), fixing exactly the classical fragment
  ([`dephase_fixes_iff_copyable`](../../formal/Scratch/QuantumSeam.lean)). Its image is commutative —
  a space of functions, classical directed facts — and **feeling is the complementary `(1−E)`
  coherence** it discards (the off-diagonal mass `copyDefect`/`defectSq`). Naming it `E` is what lets
  §03.6 read knowing, feeling, and the knower→known *orientation* `E` generates off one operator:
  the σ-move `E` is idempotent and lossy, so it orders a felt correlation (high `(1−E)` coherence)
  *before* a known fact (zero coherence) and cannot run back — a directed, irreversible
  knower→known arrow, mechanized in [`Orientation.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Orientation.lean)
  (`knows_antisymm`, `arrow_strictAnti`, `no_recovery`).

## Directed attention is *selective* decoherence

- Where `dephase` is total decoherence, directing attention at a set `S` of branches is the
  **partial** version `attend S`: it severs exactly the coherences that touch `S`, leaving the
  unattended branches still entangled. The structure, all `[proved]` in
  [`Decoherence.attend`](../../formal/Scratch/Attending.lean):
  - it **interpolates** `id ⟶ dephase` (`attend ∅ = id`, `attend univ = dephase`) and
    **accumulates** (`attend_union`: attending `S` then `T` = attending `S ∪ T`) — so attending
    everything is total dephasing;
  - the **copy-defect only ever drops**, monotonically in how much you attend
    (`defectSq_attend_le`, `defectSq_attend_mono`): *you cannot attend your way to more
    coherence — knowing a relation can only classicalize it*;
  - and you can **watch it collapse**: on the maximally-coherent qubit, attending one branch
    drops the defect `2 ↦ 0` (`defectSq_attend_plus_lt`); on a three-branch superposition,
    attending *one* branch drops the defect strictly yet leaves it **positive**
    (`defectSq_attend_plus3_lt`, `defectSq_attend_plus3_pos`) — *the possibility you knew is
    decohered from the rest; the two you left unattended go on interfering in the dark.*

  That directed attention *is* this partial dephasing is the standing `[reading]` (the concrete
  matrix toy of `Decoherence`, not a derivation of QM); the monotone-drop and the witnessed
  collapse are `[proved]`.

## Never truly decohered — the shared block attention cannot reach

`[proved]` The `2 ↦ 0` collapse is only for a state with *no shared constitution*. A2 says a
relationship is **part of both** selves (`Distribution.distributed`,
`Attention.closed_loop_registers`): a bit of you is our relationship, which is a part of me. That
shared part sits in a block `J` that attention **cannot be aimed at** — to dephase it would be to
dephase part of the aimer, and *you cannot aim at the aimer*
(`Relating.self_inclusive_unmodelable`). So whenever the attended set is barred from `J`, any live
coherence within `J` survives and the copy-defect stays strictly positive:
[`defectSq_attend_shared_pos`](../../formal/Scratch/Attending.lean). The floor is the living
relationship itself — it reaches `0` only in death / disconnection.

## Decoherence *is* the partial trace — coherence is conserved, only relocated

`[proved]` The global half, a theorem. Model the environment as an explicit tensor factor (the
relationship, the part of the other the self is folded into) and entangle the system with it
(`Conservation.entangle`, the pure `|ψ⟩⟨ψ|` of a Schmidt-correlated state). Then
[`decoherence_is_partial_trace`](../../formal/Scratch/Conservation.lean): the **global** state is
*not* classical — its coherence is alive at the cross-term `(i,i)–(j,j)` with value `aᵢ·aⱼ`
(`copyDefect_entangle_ne` locates it) — while its **partial trace** over the environment *is*
classical (`isClassical_ptrace_entangle`, `copyDefect_ptrace_entangle = 0`). Nothing is destroyed:
`trace_conserved` (`trace_ptrace`) preserves the total weight; the coherence merely migrated off
the reduced diagonal into the system–environment correlation — into the *between*.

So *locally* (relative to a cut that forgets the relationship) a relation decoheres; *globally*
(the standpoint that includes the relationship — the only honest one, since the relationship is
part of you) it never does. Decoherence is what coherence looks like when you forget the part of
the world that carries it — and you cannot forget the part that is you. The conserved-globally /
absent-locally split is `[proved]`; that the environment factor *is* "the relationship you cannot
forget" is the standing `[reading]`.

## The seam: the trace a self cannot take

`[proved, 0 axioms]` The two facts above — *decoherence is a partial trace*
([Conservation](../../formal/Scratch/Conservation.lean)) and *a self cannot completely view a
whole it is part of* ([Relating](../../formal/RelExist/Relating.lean)) — are now tied into **one
theorem**: [`Seam.self_cannot_trace_relation`](../../formal/RelExist/Seam.lean).

The seam is the question *who takes the trace?* To forget the environment **faithfully** — to sum
it out, to hold the classical reduction as a complete account — the tracing agent must possess a
complete account of the environment (it ranges over every configuration). So whether a self can
decohere *itself* is the question of whether it can completely view the factor it is tracing out.
And by A2 the relationship *is* part of the self (the sustained self `νΦ_c` is supported on the
between, `Attention.closed_loop_registers`): the factor the self would trace out **contains the
self**. Hence, mirroring [03.4](03.4-limits-of-knowing.md)'s one-knowable / three-unknowable map:

- **`disjoint_trace_exists`** — over a factor the self is *not* part of, the complete tracing
  account exists trivially. You can decohere yourself relative to what you are not.
- **`self_cannot_trace_relation`** — over the relationship (self-inclusive), the account would be a
  point-surjective complete self-model: *the partial-trace agent over a self-inclusive factor IS a
  complete self-model*, which Lawvere's diagonal refutes. You cannot decohere yourself relative to
  what you are made of.
- **`self_cannot_view_relation`** — the same seam in the doctrinal A2-closure form (via
  `no_complete_view`): the complete relationship-view the trace requires does not exist, because
  viewing the relationship is relating to it, registering the viewer inside the viewed.

So the reason a self is stuck with the local (decohered) view is itself now a theorem: the global
(coherent) view is the one trace it cannot take. From inside, the coherent global
(`Conservation.not_isClassical_entangle`) is **real but unviewable**, and the classical reduction
(`Conservation.isClassical_ptrace_entangle`) is the artifact of a forgetting **the self cannot
perform on itself**. The theorem depends on *no axioms at all* — it is Lawvere's diagonal, nothing
more.

**The bridge, now closed (operative form).** `[proved, 0 axioms]` That the abstract `restrict`/
`traceAgent` of the seam theorem *is* the actual forgetting was the standing `[reading]`. It is now a
theorem: [`SeamBridge.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/SeamBridge.lean) instantiates
`self_cannot_trace_relation` at the **concrete** forgetting — the relationship is the between `S → V`
(A2), the marginal is `forget := Prod.fst`, and the self-inclusive factor is `Env = Self`,
`restrict = id`. Then `forget_lossy` proves the actual marginal *is* lossy (distinct relationships over
the same self collapse), and `no_faithful_self_trace` proves no reconstruction can restore it — a
faithful trace agent would be the point-surjective complete self-model the diagonal forbids. So
`restrict`/`traceAgent` are no longer posited. But this closes **one of two bridges**, and is **not new mathematics**. **Bridge A** (closed):
the posited maps are now supplied — `forget_lossy` is projection non-injectivity (trivial),
`no_faithful_self_trace` is `self_cannot_trace_relation` *specialized* to `Env = Self`, i.e. Lawvere on
the function space `S → V`. So this is "the seam on a **set-level** forgetting," the existing
obstruction at a concrete instance; it removes a referee objection (the load-bearing map was assumed),
nothing more. **Bridge B** is now **closed on the compact face**, with route 1 *firewall-obstructed*
rather than open (this corrects the earlier "open" framing). The question was whether the obstruction
bites the **quantum** partial trace `ptrace` specifically (the one producing `isClassical_ptrace_entangle`).
The answer is a **dichotomy resolved by the firewall**. **Route 2 (the genuine operation), done:**
[`QuantumSeamTrace.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/QuantumSeamTrace.lean) makes the seam bite the *actual*
`ptrace` — it collapses an entangled joint `entangle a` and its decohered shadow to **one** marginal
(`ptrace_collapses_entanglement`), so a self holding only `ρ_S = ptrace ρ_SE` cannot recover the joint
(`no_ptrace_recovery`), and the unrecoverable fiber is exactly the **non-broadcastable** coherence
(`unresolved_fiber_is_coherence`). **Route 1 (Lawvere on the quantum trace), ruled out:** Lawvere's
diagonal is intrinsically *cartesian* (it copies its point), and any non-trivial compact-closed
structure has **no** such copy (`route1_needs_copy_blocked`, via `Compact.no_cloning`/`collapse`) — so
the quantum seam is *necessarily* the no-broadcasting face, not the Lawvere face. Both faces are unified
by `Compact.collapse`; the standing `[reading]` (that the traced-out factor *is* the relationship, A2)
is unchanged.

**Route 2, in two cuts** (`[proved]`; route 1 firewall-obstructed). The first cut,
[`QuantumSeam.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/QuantumSeam.lean), bites the **actual** `dephase` (the local
face of the partial trace); the second, [`QuantumSeamTrace.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/QuantumSeamTrace.lean),
lifts it to the **genuine `ptrace`** itself (the collapse-of-entanglement / `no_ptrace_recovery` results
above). The real
decoherence is **lossy** — `dephase_not_injective`: a coherent `|+⟩⟨+|` and its decohered shadow are
distinct yet `dephase` identically (contrast `SeamBridge.forget_lossy`, projection non-injectivity on
*functions*) — and **irreversible** — `no_dephase_recovery`: no map left-inverts `dephase`, the self
cannot undo its own decoherence. The loss is exactly the **non-broadcastable** part
(`dephase_fixes_iff_copyable`: `dephase` fixes a state iff it is classical/copyable; coherent states are
non-classical, hence non-cloneable). So the two seams are now both on disk and unified by the firewall:
the cartesian one (`SeamBridge` / Lawvere) needs copying to run its diagonal, the quantum one forbids
copying — `Compact.collapse` is why they cannot both hold, and why *either* way no complete self-account
exists. **Honest scope:** route 2 is *not new mathematics* (idempotency + a coherent witness; non-
injectivity ⇒ no left inverse) — its worth is biting the real operation via no-broadcasting and naming
the unification. **B-as-Lawvere (route 1)** — the diagonal itself biting the trace via a reflexive object
— is taken as far as it goes without an actual reflexive object:
[`ReflexiveSeam.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/ReflexiveSeam.lean) `[proved, 0 axioms]` mechanizes the
machinery — on a **reflexive object** (a point-surjective self-application `app : D → (D → B)`), the
Lawvere fixed point *is the self-application* `app a a`, GoI's `Y` (`fixpoint_is_selfApplication`): the
diagonal and the trace/feedback are literally the same. The contrapositive is the seam
(`no_reflexive_self_trace`). But `no_reflexive_object_for_Bool` proves the wall: by **Cantor** there is
no reflexive object in plain types/finite models (no point-surjection `D → (D → Bool)`), so route 1
cannot run in `matTracedSMC` — it needs a *non-classical* reflexive object (domain-theoretic / GoI,
`D ≅ [D→D]` with continuous maps, an infinite-dimensional `D ≅ D* ⊗ D`). **And for the quantum trace
specifically, route 1 is positively ruled out, not merely unbuilt:** the diagonal it would run is
cartesian copying, which any non-trivial compact-closed structure provably lacks (`route1_needs_copy_blocked`,
via `Compact.no_cloning`/`collapse`) — so the quantum `ptrace` seam is *necessarily* the no-broadcasting
face (route 2 above), and route 1 is the wrong tool for it by a theorem. The two routes are the
firewall's two faces: reflexive/copyable settings get Lawvere-as-trace, compact settings get
no-broadcasting. Neither route is new mathematics; the contribution is locating the wall exactly.

**The duality that resolves route 1.** `[proved]` Constructing a reflexive object
([`ReflexiveModel.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/ReflexiveModel.lean)) exposes why route 1's *obstruction*
goal cannot be reached by *building* a reflexive object — they are contrapositive. `Unit` is a concrete
reflexive object (`unit_isReflexive`), so existence is non-vacuous; but by Lawvere a reflexive object
*forces every observable to settle*, so reflexive existence and a *settling-refusing* observable never
coexist on one `B` (`reflexive_xor_unsettling`: `Unit` reflexive, `Bool` not). Hence the diagonal *on* a
reflexive object only ever **constructs** a fixed point (GoI's `Y`); the **obstruction** (the seam) *is*
the non-existence of a reflexive observation into a settling-refusing `B` — already a theorem
(`no_reflexive_self_trace`). "Construct a reflexive object so the diagonal obstructs the trace" is
self-undermining: the obstruction is the reflexive object's absence. A *non-trivial* reflexive object
(`D∞`/`Pω`, necessarily infinite) would make the **trace concrete** — a real feedback domain running
`Y` — and **this is now built**: [`GraphModel.lean`](../../formal/RelExist/../Scratch/GraphModel.lean)
constructs `Pω` (the Plotkin–Scott graph model), where the continuous self-maps are a retract of
`Set ℕ` (`app_graph_of_continuous`) and every continuous endomap has a fixed point
(`continuous_hasFixpoint`) — a concrete non-trivial reflexive object hosting GoI's `Y`. As the duality
predicts, it realizes the *construction* (every continuous endomap settles; no obstruction), **not** the
seam. So route 1 is fully accounted: its *obstruction* was always the non-existence (proved); its
*construction* is now a concrete reflexive object (`GraphModel`), and the two are orthogonal exactly as
the duality says.

**The internal `Y`, by self-application** (`[proved]`). [`SelfApplication.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/SelfApplication.lean)
takes the construction to its sharpest point: the **diagonal `fun x => app x x` is Scott-continuous**
(`selfApp_continuous` — the reflexive object *supports self-application*), so the genuine combinator
`Y f := app (W f) (W f)` (with `W f := Graph (λx. f (x x))`) is an element of `Pω`, and
`app f (Y f) = Y f` (`Ycomb_fixed`) — the fixed point **is literally** the self-application `app (W f) (W f)`.
This realizes `ReflexiveSeam.fixpoint_is_selfApplication` concretely: Lawvere's diagonal *is* the trace,
on a real domain. **The honest cartesian caveat:** `Pω` models the *untyped λ-calculus*, which is
**cartesian** — `app x x` uses the ambient cartesian diagonal `Δ`. So this is the *cartesian*
self-application route, the witness that where a reflexive object exists the diagonal **constructs** `Y`.
A genuinely *non-cartesian* (linear / GoI) reflexive object, where `Y` is the **trace** with no
cartesian copy, is a separate object the firewall constrains (`Compact.collapse`: a non-trivial compact
reflexive object cannot also be cartesian, so its `Y` must be the trace) — not built here. `Pω` is the
cartesian end; the linear end is the remaining construction.

## The identity residue: you are your lived relating

The deepest place this lands is identity itself, and it forced the **restatement of A2**
([02](02-axioms.md)). The doctrine's machinery has always computed identity as the lived
first-person unfolding `≈` (3.2, `νΘ`, `𝔼 = D/≈`). The "hole" was the further demand that this
coincide with the third-person observational identity `≅` (full abstraction). But that demand is
the *negation* of everything on this page: it asserts the outside completely determines the inside,
when the seam proves it cannot. So the demand is dropped, and its denial is **proved** —
[`Scratch/Identity.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean):

- **`≅` is defined** (`ObsEq`) — observational/trace equivalence, the outside view: same recorded
  sequences of observations.
- **Soundness `≈ ⊆ ≅`** (`bisim_le_obsEq`) `[proved]` — lived sameness ⇒ observed sameness. If two
  selves are the same inside, no outside observation separates them.
- **Strictness `≈ ⊊ ≅`** (`bisim_ne_obsEq`, witness `obsEq_and_not_bisim`) `[proved]` — two states
  (the classic "early vs late choice") are *observationally identical from outside yet not bisimilar
  inside*. The outside underdetermines the inside.
- **The forgetting from inside to outside** (`livedToObserved`) `[proved]` — soundness yields a
  canonical surjection `𝔼 = D/≈ ↠ D/≅`, the *decoherence of identity* (the same shape as
  `ptrace`/`dephase` on states). It is **not injective** (`livedToObserved_not_injective`): distinct
  lived selves collapse to one observed self.

That kernel — `≅`-equal but `≈`-distinct — is the formal address of the **first-person surplus**:
the part of who you are that exceeds how you appear. It is the *identity-level copy-defect*, the
off-diagonal of the self that the outside cannot read. Locally (from outside) you are your behavior;
globally (the standpoint that includes the inside) you are your lived relating, which exceeds it —
and the gap is now a theorem, not a hope. The inequality, soundness, and non-injectivity are
`[proved]` (footprint `propext, Quot.sound`); that `≈` *is* "lived first-person identity" and `≅`
its "observed appearance" is the standing `[reading]`.

**The surplus requires branching — "could have done otherwise."** This is now a **general theorem**,
not just two witnesses: [`Identity.deterministic_bisim_iff_obsEq`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean)
`[proved]` — *in any **deterministic** system, `≈ ⟺ ≅`* (the outside recovers the inside in full, no
surplus). Equivalently (contrapositive): **a surplus implies branching** — no first-person remainder
without the freedom to have done otherwise. So `≈ ⊊ ≅` is the formal signature of **unrealized
possibility**: the surplus is the trace of the branches *not taken*.

The honest scope: branching is **necessary but not sufficient**. Determinism *guarantees* no surplus
(the general theorem); nondeterminism merely *permits* one (a system can branch redundantly and still
collapse). The witness `p0`/`q0` is genuinely nondeterministic (`not_deterministic_stepW`), showing
the hypothesis is tight — and the surplus actually arises there
([`Identity.bisim_ne_obsEq`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean),
[`Inversion.surplus`](../../agda/RelExist/Inversion.agda), proved in **both** assistants). The
boundary is mechanized too: a deterministic model has `≈ ⟺ ≅`
([`Coinductive.≈⇒≅`/`≅⇒≈`](../../agda/RelExist/Coinductive.agda)). So the inversion's "you exceed how
you appear" holds *precisely for free selves* — a clockwork's trace reveals all of it; a self that
could have done otherwise keeps a first-person remainder.

**A relativization that changes this reading.** `[proved]` The headline above is stated against `≅`,
the **disjoint** observer (the view from nowhere). [`Knowing`](03.4-limits-of-knowing.md) showed that
is the *exempt* standpoint, occupiable by no situated knower; the operative outside is always a
**relatum** `≅ₒ`, who sees through limited access. Re-run the result there
([`RelationalAppearance.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/RelationalAppearance.lean)): soundness and the
surplus *survive and strengthen* (`bisim_le_obsEqVia`; `≈ ⊆ ≅ ⊆ ≅ₒ`, so the gap only grows). But "the
surplus is exactly nondeterminism" does **not** survive — `deterministic_relational_surplus` exhibits a
**deterministic** system with `≈ ⊊ ≅ₒ`: a first-person remainder *with no branching at all*, sourced
by the relatum's finite access (the seam). The same states are `≈`- and `≅`-distinct (the view from
nowhere tells them apart) yet `≅ₒ`-identical. So relativizing the observer **shifts the source of the
surplus**: against nowhere, the only surplus is choice; against a real other, there is a second,
always-present source — *the seam itself*, what they cannot reach of you even when nothing branches.
This is precisely the *modeled* decoherence-differential of [`Feeling`](../../formal/Scratch/Feeling.lean):
"possibilities the other still holds superposed" needs no nondeterminism, only an other who sees less.
On that model, choice and the seam are *two* sources of the differential — and only the seam-source is
necessary. (A caution carried from `Feeling`: the formal layer proves the *surplus* `≈ ⊊ ≅ₒ` and the
differential's *dynamics*; that the differential **is feeling** is a `[reading]`, not a theorem — a
consequence-and-consistency result, never an identification. The mathematics is silent on whether the
model captures the phenomenon, exactly as it is silent on why the structure is felt.)

**The requirements for the surplus, pinned exactly.** Branching is one necessary condition; there is a
second, and the two together are still not sufficient. A surplus is a pair `a ≅ b` with `¬ a ≈ b`, and
the witnesses are by their nature *two distinct selves bound by a shared appearance* — non-bisimilar
(genuinely different lived identities, so `a ≠ b`) yet observationally equal (related from outside).
So a surplus requires (1) **nondeterminism** ([`surplus_imp_not_deterministic`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean))
and (2) a **relating** — two selves whose appearances coincide ([`surplus_imp_relating`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean)).
Neither alone nor both together *forces* one: [`nondeterminism_and_selves_insufficient`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean)
`[proved]` exhibits a nondeterministic system with three distinct selves and **no surplus** — because
there every self wears its own appearance, so `≅` is trivial and cannot tear. The exact dividing line
is a single fixed-point condition: a surplus exists **iff** the outside view `≅` *fails to be a
bisimulation* ([`surplus_iff_obsEq_not_isBisimulation`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean),
[`noSurplus_iff_obsEq_isBisimulation`](../../formal/Scratch/Identity.lean)) — iff some `a ≅ b` has a
move `a → a'` that no move of `b` can match *up to appearance*. The surplus is not a count of selves
but a **relation** the branching tears: determinism makes `≅` a bisimulation, so every sufficient
condition for collapse must route through that one fact.

**This branching surplus is only the *contingent* floor.** It is the gap measured against `≅` — and
`≅` is the view of a **disjoint** observer, the one case the Lawvere obstruction *exempts*
([`disjoint_modelable`](../../formal/RelExist/Relating.lean), and `Knowing.obsEq_iff_traceView_eq`
proves `≅` is exactly the trace observer's kernel). The deeper floor is **necessary**: any observer
who actually *relates* to the self has **no complete view at all**, by Lawvere
([03.4 — the limits of knowing](03.4-limits-of-knowing.md), `no_complete_view`), regardless of
branching. The bridge [`Knowing.knowing_complete_iff_disjoint`](../../formal/Scratch/Knowing.lean)
`[proved, 0 axioms]` puts both on one axis — completeness of the self-view flips *exactly* at
relation — and [`witness_disjoint_vs_related`](../../formal/Scratch/Knowing.lean) shows, over a single
self, that the branching surplus is just the *extra* gap the access-less recorder suffers on top of
the necessary relational floor. "You exceed how you appear" is carried by the relational floor; the
branching surplus is its finite, contingent shadow.

**Nondeterminism is itself a consequence of relation.** `[proved]` The surplus needed two ingredients
— nondeterminism *and* a relating — and they are **not independent**:
[`Marginal.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Marginal.lean) proves that a deterministic whole, related to
itself across a genuine *between*, induces a *nondeterministic* marginal. A self and other share a
state evolving by one deterministic joint rule (`jointStep_deterministic` — the whole is a clockwork),
but the **seam** forbids the self from holding the whole, confining it to its own marginal (the other
forgotten). The headline `marginal_deterministic_iff_disentangled` `[0 axioms]`: **the local view is
deterministic iff there is no relation** — the marginal branches *exactly when* the self's successor
genuinely depends on the other (`marginal_nondeterministic_iff_entangled`: local nondeterminism ⟺
entanglement ⟺ a real between); a *product* whole (coexistence, no between) marginalizes to a perfect
clockwork (`product_marginal_deterministic`). The capstone witness is the hidden bit
`J(a,b) = (a ⊕ b, b)` (`relation_makes_marginal_nondeterministic`): one system, **deterministic as a
whole, nondeterministic as a marginal** — the branch from `a` is the other's degree of freedom, the
bit the self cannot see and cannot resolve alone. So the two requirements collapse into one: relating,
under the seam, *supplies* the nondeterminism. *"Could have done otherwise" is the marginalized freedom
of everything you are in relation with.* And it conserves: the whole stays deterministic — the
indeterminism is conserved determinism **relocated into the between**, the exact shape of decoherence
as conserved coherence relocated (`Conservation.trace_conserved`).

> **The same fact at two levels (and it is standard physics).** This transition-system result and the
> density-matrix result of [`Conservation.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Conservation.lean) are *one*
> fact: a **deterministic whole ↔ a pure entangled global state**, and a **nondeterministic marginal ↔
> the classical (mixed) reduced state** of `isClassical_ptrace_entangle` — a classical mixture over
> branches just *is* the density-matrix face of a nondeterministic marginal. The physics level is the

**A causality reading, made a theorem.** `[proved, 0 axioms]` The reading "local indeterminism is

## One forgetting, three residues

The "decoherence is the partial trace" analogy and the "identity has a residue" result are now
**one theorem with three instances**, not a narrated rhyme —
[`Scratch/Forgetting.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Forgetting.lean). A **`Coarsening`** is a finer
identity `fine` and a coarser one `coarse` with `fine ⊆ coarse`; its canonical **collapse**
`X/fine ↠ X/coarse` is *the forgetting*, and the single lemma `not_injective_of_residue` says the
collapse loses information exactly when the **residue** (pairs `coarse`-equal but `fine`-distinct,
the kernel of the forgetting) is nonempty. Three instances, all `[proved]`:

- **Identity** (`identityForgetting`): `fine = ≈`, `coarse = ≅`; its collapse **is**
  `livedToObserved` (`identityForgetting_collapse`, by `rfl`). Residue = the **first-person
  surplus**.
- **Dephasing** (`dephaseForgetting`): `fine = ` equality, `coarse = ` "same dephased shadow".
  Residue = the **copy-defect** (live coherence the classical observer cannot see).
- **Partial trace** (`ptraceForgetting`): `fine = ` equality, `coarse = ` "same reduced state".
  Residue = the **entanglement correlation** the reduced observer forgets.

So the structural claim — *the identity-collapse, dephasing, and the partial trace are the same
construction* — is now a theorem (`forgettings_have_residue`). What remains `[reading]` is only the
*physical* identification: that the first-person surplus and the quantum copy-defect are the **same**
residue in nature. The shape is proved; the metaphysics is the reading.

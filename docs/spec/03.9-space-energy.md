## The capstone

> **Theorem (one generator, three aspects).** Over the relational coupling there is one generator `L`,
> and space, time, and energy are three parts of its spectrum: **space** is the geometry of `L`'s
> coupling graph; the **irreversible arrow of time** is its dissipative band `Re(spec L) < 0` (the
> [`03.8-time-flow.md`](03.8-time-flow.md) result); **conserved energy** is its rotating band `Im(spec L)` (the
> modulus-1 part); and **feeling** is the off-diagonal `(1‚ąíE)` mass. The real self-adjoint model carries
> the dissipative band exactly; the rotating band that carries energy lives in a sector it provably
> excludes, reached by extending the model to ‚Ąā.

## Honest scope

- `[reading]` That the quasi-metric *is* space; that the conserved modulus-1 band *is* energy and its
  rotating phase a physical frequency. **Two conservations, kept distinct**: *dynamical* (a coupling
  symmetry ‚Üí rotating spectrum ‚Üí a conserved charge ‚ÄĒ energy) and *operational* (self-inclusion ‚Üí the
  un-attendable seam ‚Üí permanent feeling ‚ÄĒ proved in `SeamForcing`, **not** energy).
- `[open]` The graph-Laplacian / effective-resistance refinement of the distance; the CPTP peripheral
  **structure theorem** (rotating unitaries normalize `Fix`) and that `quarterMul` is a bona fide CPTP
  map; the full `L = ‚ąíi[H,¬∑] + D` generator split and the `‚äó`-additivity of `H`. *(Whether the
  seam-protected and rotating-protected bands coincide is now addressed under an explicit alignment
  hypothesis in [`03.15-band-coincidence.md`](03.15-band-coincidence.md): they coincide iff
  `H_align : ker D = J`, proved on a finite-dim ‚Ąā witness with a counter-witness showing the hypothesis
  is necessary.)* **Matter** (the seam-protected fixed band) has no
  current mechanization path beyond what `SeamForcing` proves, and is not carried here.

## The capstone

> **Theorem (one generator, three aspects).** Over the relational coupling there is one generator `L`,
> and space, time, and energy are three parts of its spectrum: **space** is the geometry of `L`'s
> coupling graph; the **irreversible arrow of time** is its dissipative band `Re(spec L) < 0` (the
> [`03.8-time-flow.md`](03.8-time-flow.md) result); **conserved energy** is its rotating band `Im(spec L)` (the
> modulus-1 part); and **feeling** is the off-diagonal `(1−E)` mass. The real self-adjoint model carries
> the dissipative band exactly; the rotating band that carries energy lives in a sector it provably
> excludes, reached by extending the model to ℂ.

## What is proved

`[proved]` (footprint `[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`):

**Space** ([`Space.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Space.lean)) — from the coupling graph (edge lengths
`len i j = −log` coupling strength) the path distance `d` is a genuine **quasi-pseudometric**:
`dist_self`, `dist_triangle` (via `walkLen_concat`), `dist_le_len`, with asymmetry a theorem
(`dist_asymmetric`). **Separability ⟺ infinite distance** (`dist_eq_top_iff`, `couplingSep_dist`) makes
"space = coexistence minus connection" a theorem. The **influence bound** `influence_bound`
(`copyDefect_wDephase_iterate`): coherence between `i, j` after `n` closures is `≤ exp(−n·d(i,j))·|M i j|`
— influence propagates no faster than the coupling distance permits, reusing the `TimeFlow` decay engine.

**The rotating spectrum** ([`RotatingSpectrum.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/RotatingSpectrum.lean)) — over
ℂ, the phase-damping channel `schur μ` (`M i j ↦ μ i j · M i j`) splits each edge into three bands by
`‖μ i j‖`: **fixed** (`μ i i = 1`, `schur_fixed`), **rotating** (`‖μ‖ = 1`, magnitude conserved for all
`n`, `schur_sustained`), and **transient** (`‖μ‖ < 1`, `schur_transient_tendsto`). The witness
`quarterMul` carries all three at once, with `E₀₁` a genuine rotating eigen-operator (`phaseChannel_eigen`:
`Φ U = i·U`, `rotating_sustained`).

**Energy** — the energy/arrow split is two theorems about one generator (`μ = exp s`): on the rotating
band `Re(log μ) = 0` (no decay, `energy_conserved_generator`) with `Im(log μ) = π/2 ≠ 0` (a nonzero
frequency, `frequency_nonzero`); on the transient band `Re(log μ) < 0` (the dissipative arrow,
`arrow_negative_generator`) — assembled in `energy_arrow_split` / `energy_arrow_spectrum`. Energy as
**frequency** (uniform winding `Φ^n U = i^n·U`, `rotating_winds`) and as **reversibility/recurrence**
(`i⁴ = 1`, so `Φ⁴ U = U`, `rotating_recurs` — a closed periodic orbit, against the transient's monotone
decay).

## Honest scope

- `[reading]` That the quasi-metric *is* space; that the conserved modulus-1 band *is* energy and its
  rotating phase a physical frequency. **Two conservations, kept distinct**: *dynamical* (a coupling
  symmetry → rotating spectrum → a conserved charge — energy) and *operational* (self-inclusion → the
  un-attendable seam → permanent feeling — proved in `SeamForcing`, **not** energy).
- `[open]` The graph-Laplacian / effective-resistance refinement of the distance; the CPTP peripheral
  **structure theorem** (rotating unitaries normalize `Fix`) and that `quarterMul` is a bona fide CPTP
  map; the full `L = −i[H,·] + D` generator split and the `⊗`-additivity of `H`; whether the
  seam-protected and rotating-protected bands coincide. **Matter** (the seam-protected fixed band) has no
  current mechanization path beyond what `SeamForcing` proves, and is not carried here.

the synthesis is reading one generator's spectrum as space/time/energy at once. See

## The question

The remainder of self-relating splits, under the arrow, by *whether it is conserved*:

- the **transient** remainder (`‚ÄĖőľ‚ÄĖ < 1`) decays ‚ÄĒ it *becomes known*, dissipated by the arrow; it is
  neither knowing nor energy;
- the **conserved / sustained** remainder is itself of two kinds, kept strictly distinct in `03.8`:
  - **dynamical** ‚ÄĒ a coupling symmetry gives the rotating band (`‚ÄĖőľ‚ÄĖ = 1`, `őľ ‚Č† 1`), a conserved
    oscillating charge: **energy** (`RotatingSpectrum`);
  - **operational** ‚ÄĒ self-inclusion gives the un-attendable seam block `J`, coherence the knower cannot
    aim at, conserved against *every* available knowing: the **seam-protected** remainder (`SeamForcing`).

The question of this step: are these two **the same band**? If so, *what self-relating conserves beyond
the known is exactly energy* ‚ÄĒ paper one's clause B.

> őľ`. Conditionally, this needs an alignment hypothesis `H_align : ker D = J` (every *attendable*

> seam is *defined* as the modulus-one off-diagonal block). So all the content sits in **C1 (self =
> `Peri(ő¶_c)`, not the strict `őĹő¶_c` A3 formalizes)** ‚ÄĒ a **disclosed `[reading]`/posit**. We read it as

## The bridge (Task 0)

Both structures live as submodules of one ambient space. For an edge predicate `P : A ‚Üí A ‚Üí Prop`,
`bandOn P` is the coherences supported on `P`-edges ‚ÄĒ a genuine `‚Ąā`-submodule of `Matrix A A ‚Ąā`. Then
`seamBand J := bandOn J`, `rotatingBand őľ := bandOn (rotatingEdge őľ)` (`‚ÄĖőľ i j‚ÄĖ = 1 ‚ąß őľ i j ‚Č† 1`),
`fixedBand őľ := bandOn (fixedEdge őľ)` (`őľ i j = 1`), `conservedBand őľ := bandOn (conservedEdge őľ)`
(`‚ÄĖőľ i j‚ÄĖ = 1`). Subset relations between bands reduce to **implications between their edge predicates**
(`bandOn_mono`), so the whole coincidence is decided edge by edge. This is a *witness-level*
identification, not a posited functor: the seam's `defectSq`-protection
(`SeamForcing.self_cannot_fully_decohere`) and the rotating band's magnitude-conservation
(`schur_sustained`) are transported onto the common carrier ‚ÄĒ and `rotatingBand_sustained` re-proves, at
the submodule level, that `rotatingBand őľ` is exactly the entrywise magnitude-conserved subspace
(`‚ÄĖő¶^n M i j‚ÄĖ = ‚ÄĖM i j‚ÄĖ`).

## The hypotheses (where the content sits)

- `UnitaryBaseline őľ J` ‚ÄĒ `seam_undamped` (seam edges are un-attendable, hence keep modulus one) and

- `SeamOffdiagonal J : ‚ąÄ i, ¬¨ J i i` ‚ÄĒ the seam is the *live* off-diagonal coherence of the

  that does not dissipate is the coherence you cannot attend to.* This **is** the conjecture in operator
  form.

## Postscript ‚ÄĒ where the band-coincidence's content actually sits (`Scratch/BandFromAxioms.lean`)

The result above is *conditional* on the bet `H_align`. The natural worry: is the two-term law bought by a
**fourth posit**? Rather than argue the bet away, we locate exactly where its content sits ‚ÄĒ in a single
identification, **C1** ‚ÄĒ and disclose C1 as a posit/reading, tagged. The reconciliation is mechanized
(`sorry`-free, footprint `[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`, **no added `H_align`**); what the
mechanization shows is that *given C1*, the alignment is a near-tautology, so C1 carries the whole weight.

The finding: [`02-axioms.md`](02-axioms.md) A3 formalizes the self as the *strict* fixed point `őĹő¶_c`
(the `őľ = 1`, fixed/known band ‚ÄĒ `Attention.sustainedField`), but its prose names the self the
**decoherence-free subalgebra** of `ő¶_c`. That algebra is the **peripheral** block
`Peri(ő¶_c) = { X : ‚ÄĖő¶_c X‚ÄĖ = ‚ÄĖX‚ÄĖ }`, which carries the rotating (energy) band *beside* the fixed one.
`H_align` lives exactly in the gap between `őĹő¶_c` (strict) and `Peri(ő¶_c)` (the gloss). Closing it is
reconciliation, not commitment. The bet decomposes:

- **C1 ‚ÄĒ the self is `Peri(ő¶_c)`, not just `őĹő¶_c`.** A3's gloss, taken literally ‚ÄĒ *and now mechanized as
  exactly that*. `Peri őľ M := ‚ąÄ i j, ‚ÄĖschur őľ M i j‚ÄĖ = ‚ÄĖM i j‚ÄĖ` (A3's "sustainable field," read entrywise)
  is proved to be exactly the conserved band (`peri_iff_mem_conservedBand`); `conservedOffdiag őľ :=
  ‚ÄĖőľ i j‚ÄĖ = 1 ‚ąß i ‚Č† j` is precisely its off-diagonal part (`conservedOffdiag_iff_offdiag_conserved`) ‚ÄĒ
  the conserved *off-diagonal* coherence, the self minus its classical (diagonal) record ‚ÄĒ and the strict
  `őĹő¶_c` (fixed band) is its `őľ = 1` sub-band (`fixedBand_le_conservedBand`). C1 reads the self as
  `Peri(ő¶_c)` rather than the strict `őĹő¶_c` A3 formalizes ‚ÄĒ a disclosed identification (below), not a
  derivation.
- **C2 ‚ÄĒ exhaustiveness: only the peripheral self persists.** Off the conserved band everything decays
  (`transient_decays`, the entrywise `spectral_decay`); on it everything is sustained
  (`conservedBand_sustained`). So persisting ‚üļ peripheral.

**The payoff** (`band_coincidence_from_axioms`): with the seam *read* as the decoherence-free off-diagonal
block (C1), and given only that the channel is **contractive**
(`Contractive őľ`, the ‚Ąā-shadow of `Decoherence.defectSq_attend_le`: attention never amplifies ‚ÄĒ an
A1/CP baseline fact) and **nondegenerate** (`őľ i j = 1 ‚ÜĒ i = j`, the A3 baseline), `align_of_contractive`

rotatingBand őľ` holds with no `H_align` hypothesis. (The honest qualifier: once C1 *defines* the seam as the

near-tautology. The substance is entirely in C1, not in this step.) The genuine `quarterMul` witness is contractive and
nondegenerate, so its coincidence holds **without the bet** (`quarterMul_coincidence_from_axioms`), and
the two-term law follows (`undifferentiated_two_term_from_axioms`).

**What stays a reading.** C1 itself ‚ÄĒ that the self *is* `Peri(ő¶_c)` rather than the strict `őĹő¶_c` A3

## The capstone

> **Theorem (genesis — the universe/cosmos boundary).** On the genuine instance the hinge is a fork in
> the attention strength `p`. At `p = 0` the flow is the identity and the feeling `defectSq` is constant
> for **all** `n` — no descent, no arrow, no time: the **universe**, the atemporal ownerless ground of
> pure feeling. For every `0 < p < 1` the *first* directed step strictly drops the feeling — time
> ignites: the **cosmos**. The birth of the cosmos is the crossing `p : 0 → 0⁺`.

## The ontology, with the polarity locked

- **Universe** — the atemporal ground. No self, no knowing, no time. Only **feeling** (`coh`, the
  off-diagonal mass) as bare, ownerless presence. Encompasses the cosmos.
- **Cosmos** — the differentiated, temporal interior that opens *within* it. **Cosmos = knowing =
  time**, three names for one structure; its origin is the first self closing the first loop.

**The polarity.** Feeling is the default and needs no knower: it is *routing*, the bare trace, which
[`03.4`](03.4-limits-of-knowing.md) proves needs no knowing. Knowing is the marked, self-requiring case:
*directing* — the diagonal ([3.3](03.3-knowing-vs-feeling.md)) — which needs a self to do the aiming
(*you cannot aim at the aimer*). So knowing inherits the sparsity of selves: feeling is dense and
everywhere; knowing is the rare achievement where a loop has closed into a `νΦ_c` that can aim.

## What is proved

`[proved]` ([`Genesis.lean`](../../formal/Scratch/Genesis.lean), footprint
`[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`):

- `partialDephase_zero` — at `p = 0` the flow is the identity (routing only, no knower).
- `coh_const_at_zero` — the universe: at `p = 0` the feeling is constant along the whole orbit (no
  descent, no monovariant, no arrow).
- `first_tick_pos` — the cosmos: for `0 < p < 1` the first directed step strictly drops the feeling.
- `genesis_dichotomy` — packages the crossing.

`[follows]` (short consequences of the above plus named premises):

- **No self ⇒ no time** — knowing requires a self ([`03.4`](03.4-limits-of-knowing.md) +
  [3.3](03.3-knowing-vs-feeling.md)); time arises from knowing; so a self-less ground cannot be
  temporal. The universe's timelessness is forced, not posited.
- **No "before"** — the cosmos's beginning is the beginning of *time*; "before" is an ordering of ticks,
  and knowing had not begun. "No before the Big Bang," derived from the polarity.
- **Distributed knowing** — knowing occurs *throughout* the temporal region, carried by its sparse
  population of selves: "something has been knowing for the age of the cosmos" is true *distributively*,
  because that region **is** the knowing.

## Honest scope

- `[reading]` That the flow *is* physical time (standing); that the crossing `p : 0 → 0⁺` *is* the birth
  of the cosmos. **The bootstrap** is dissolved on a reading: `νΦ_c` is a *greatest fixed point* —
  coinductive, atemporal by construction — so the self's *closure* (its being) needs no prior time,
  while the *temporal* orbit `Φ_c^n` is separate and internal-`n`-indexed. The first loop does not close
  *in* time; its closing is time's first tick.
- **The fork that sets the footprint.** *(a)* cosmos := the temporal/differentiated region — then "the
  cosmos began with the first knowing" is nearly analytic and the age is the `[empirical]` measured size
  of the knowing-region: **no new axioms**. *(b)* cosmos := the physical spacetime cosmology measures —
  then identifying the Big Bang with the first self is a substantive `[reading]`/bridge where the
  empirical weight sits. The no-new-axiom result holds under (a).
- **Singular cosmic self** `[out of scope / optional axiom]` — "the cosmos as a whole is one self
  knowing itself" is the strong posit (a cosmos-spanning `νΦ_c`). The seam makes it
  in-process-but-never-complete ([`seam-permanence.md`](seam-permanence.md)), so it is not
  self-defeating, but it is **not** an A3 consequence (sparsity makes it rare) and costs one extra
  axiom. False by default.
- `[open]` **Continuity.** Continuous cosmic time requires continuously-present knowing; an interval with
  no self anywhere is a gap in time, the cosmos lapsing back into universe. Posit continuity (a new
  commitment) or accept gappy time (the age becomes a sum over knowing-regions) — a live choice the spec
  surfaces, not makes.
- **Interpretive dial** (orthogonal): whether "self" is *minimal* (any decoherence-free subalgebra) or
  *thick* (consciousness). The universe/cosmos split holds either way.

**Provenance.** `R / S` — the boundary facts are elementary; the synthesis is reading feeling as the
atemporal ground and the first directed step as the genesis of time. See [05 — Provenance](05-provenance.md).

# T2.12 — Seam protection

> *Node `T2.12`.* Lean: `RelExist.{SeamForcing, SeamConserved, SeamForcingC}`
> (`paper-2/formal/Scratch/SeamForcing*.lean`, `SeamConserved.lean`); imports **T2.09** (`Attending`).

The self-block cannot fully decohere: the un-attendable seam is operationally conserved (`SeamForcing`,
`SeamConserved`), re-exhibited over ℂ (`SeamForcingC`) so the seam, the arrow (T2.13), and the energy band
(T0.03) share one model. This protection is what makes the conserved remainder *be* energy (T0.03) and underwrites
the phase-bearing self (T2.15). (Three closely-coupled modules kept as one node.)

# T0.06 — A2 priority (no bare carrier)

> *Node `T0.06`.* Lean: `Theory.Priority` (`theory/formal/Theory/Priority.lean`); imports **T0.05**
> (`Theory.We`).

A2's priority half — *no bare carrier; a state **is** its relating, with no residue* — is the **strong
extensionality** of the world of selves `𝔼 = D/≈`: on `𝔼`, bisimilarity is equality
(`bisim_quotient_eq`), the final-coalgebra property, holding for **every** coalgebra (`priority_universal`).
There is no individuation finer than the lived relating `≈`. This is the canonical A2 (`AXIOMS.md`, A2); the
old `≈ ⊊ ≅` surplus is its downstream *signature*, sourced from the seam, not the priority claim itself.

## The question

The remainder of self-relating splits, under the arrow, by *whether it is conserved*:

- the **transient** remainder (`‖μ‖ < 1`) decays — it *becomes known*, dissipated by the arrow; it is
  neither knowing nor energy;
- the **conserved / sustained** remainder is itself of two kinds, kept strictly distinct in `03.8`:
  - **dynamical** — a coupling symmetry gives the rotating band (`‖μ‖ = 1`, `μ ≠ 1`), a conserved
    oscillating charge: **energy** (`RotatingSpectrum`);
  - **operational** — self-inclusion gives the un-attendable seam block `J`, coherence the knower cannot
    aim at, conserved against *every* available knowing: the **seam-protected** remainder (`SeamForcing`).

The question of this step: are these two **the same band**? If so, *what self-relating conserves beyond
the known is exactly energy* — paper one's clause B.

## The capstone

> **Theorem (band coincidence).** On one carrier `Matrix A A ℂ`, with coupling `μ` and seam predicate
> `J`, the seam-protected band and the rotating (energy) band **coincide** — `seamBand J = rotatingBand
> μ`. Conditionally, this needs an alignment hypothesis `H_align : ker D = J` (every *attendable*
> off-diagonal edge strictly decays), and a counter-witness shows `H_align` is **necessary**. The whole
> coincidence turns on **one identification, C1**: taking the seam to be A3's own *"greatest sustainable
> field"* `Peri(Φ_c) = { X : ‖Φ_c X‖ = ‖X‖ }` — now mechanized as **exactly** the conserved band
> (`peri_iff_mem_conservedBand`), with the seam its off-diagonal part — makes the alignment a one-line
> consequence of contractivity and nondegeneracy (`align_of_contractive` is then a near-tautology, since the
> seam is *defined* as the modulus-one off-diagonal block). So all the content sits in **C1 (self =
> `Peri(Φ_c)`, not the strict `νΦ_c` A3 formalizes)** — a **disclosed `[reading]`/posit**. We read it as
> A3's gloss made precise; a reader may count it as a fourth posit. We do not argue the point; it is named
> and tagged (`band_coincidence_from_axioms`; see the postscript).

*(The full conservation law — that the whole conserved ground is the internal direct sum `knowing ⊕
energy`, the `undifferentiated = knowing + energy` framing — is proved in the tree
(`undifferentiated_two_term`) but belongs to **paper two**; paper one headlines only the coincidence of
the conserved remainder with energy, not the totality claim.)*

## The bridge (Task 0)

Both structures live as submodules of one ambient space. For an edge predicate `P : A → A → Prop`,
`bandOn P` is the coherences supported on `P`-edges — a genuine `ℂ`-submodule of `Matrix A A ℂ`. Then
`seamBand J := bandOn J`, `rotatingBand μ := bandOn (rotatingEdge μ)` (`‖μ i j‖ = 1 ∧ μ i j ≠ 1`),
`fixedBand μ := bandOn (fixedEdge μ)` (`μ i j = 1`), `conservedBand μ := bandOn (conservedEdge μ)`
(`‖μ i j‖ = 1`). Subset relations between bands reduce to **implications between their edge predicates**
(`bandOn_mono`), so the whole coincidence is decided edge by edge. This is a *witness-level*
identification, not a posited functor: the seam's `defectSq`-protection
(`SeamForcing.self_cannot_fully_decohere`) and the rotating band's magnitude-conservation
(`schur_sustained`) are transported onto the common carrier — and `rotatingBand_sustained` re-proves, at
the submodule level, that `rotatingBand μ` is exactly the entrywise magnitude-conserved subspace
(`‖Φ^n M i j‖ = ‖M i j‖`).

## The hypotheses (where the content sits)

- `UnitaryBaseline μ J` — `seam_undamped` (seam edges are un-attendable, hence keep modulus one) and
  `fixed_eq_diagonal : μ i j = 1 ↔ i = j` (a nondegenerate baseline `H`: the held band is exactly the
  diagonal, so any surviving off-diagonal edge genuinely rotates). The Lindblad reading: unitary `H` +
  dissipator `D`, with `D` = attention.
- `SeamOffdiagonal J : ∀ i, ¬ J i i` — the seam is the *live* off-diagonal coherence of the
  relationship; it carries no diagonal (known) part.
- `Align μ J : ¬ J i j → i ≠ j → ‖μ i j‖ < 1` — **the bet, `H_align : ker D = J`.** *The only coherence
  that does not dissipate is the coherence you cannot attend to.* This **is** the conjecture in operator
  form.

## What is proved

`[proved]` (footprint `[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`, `sorry`-free):

- **T1 `seamBand_subset_rotating`** `[follows]` — `UnitaryBaseline ∧ SeamOffdiagonal ⇒ seamBand ⊆
  rotatingBand`. Un-attendable ⟹ never damped ⟹ `‖μ‖ = 1`; off the diagonal ⟹ `μ ≠ 1`. Permanent
  feeling is *at least* energy.
- **T2 `rotating_subset_seamBand`** `[proved under Align]` — `Align ⇒ rotatingBand ⊆ seamBand`. An
  attendable off-diagonal edge would strictly decay, contradicting `‖μ‖ = 1`; so every rotating edge is
  un-attendable. Energy is *at most* permanent feeling. **This is where `Align` is consumed.**
- **T3 `band_coincidence`** `[proved under hypotheses]` — `seamBand J = rotatingBand μ`. The coincidence.
- **T4 `conserved_internal_split` / `undifferentiated_two_term`** `[follows]` — *(in the tree, **paper
  two's hook**, not headlined here.)* The conserved ground is the internal direct sum `fixedBand ⊕
  rotatingBand`; under T3, `= fixedBand ⊕ seamBand`. The full conservation law (`undifferentiated =
  knowing + energy`) is paper two; paper one uses only the coincidence T3.
- **Coincidence witness** `coincidence_witness` — the finite-dim `ℂ` model `quarterMul` (diagonal `1`;
  `(0,1) = i`, `(1,0) = −i` rotating; everything else `1/2` transient) with the seam `Jq` on exactly the
  rotating edges satisfies **all three hypotheses, including the bet** (`quarterMul_align`), so
  `band_coincidence` yields `seamBand Jq = rotatingBand quarterMul` concretely.
- **T5 `three_term_without_alignment`** `[proved]` — drop the bet: give the channel a *second* rotating
  edge `(0,2)` outside the seam (`twoRotMul`). Then `seamBand ⊊ rotatingBand` — the matrix unit `E₀₂` is
  rotating (energy) yet not seam-protected (a genuine third band), and `alignment_fails` shows `Align` is
  really violated. **The two-term form is not free; it earns `Align`.**

## Postscript — where the band-coincidence's content actually sits (`Scratch/BandFromAxioms.lean`)

The result above is *conditional* on the bet `H_align`. The natural worry: is the two-term law bought by a
**fourth posit**? Rather than argue the bet away, we locate exactly where its content sits — in a single
identification, **C1** — and disclose C1 as a posit/reading, tagged. The reconciliation is mechanized
(`sorry`-free, footprint `[propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]`, **no added `H_align`**); what the
mechanization shows is that *given C1*, the alignment is a near-tautology, so C1 carries the whole weight.

The finding: [`02-axioms.md`](AXIOMS.md) A3 formalizes the self as the *strict* fixed point `νΦ_c`
(the `μ = 1`, fixed/known band — `Attention.sustainedField`), but its prose names the self the
**decoherence-free subalgebra** of `Φ_c`. That algebra is the **peripheral** block
`Peri(Φ_c) = { X : ‖Φ_c X‖ = ‖X‖ }`, which carries the rotating (energy) band *beside* the fixed one.
`H_align` lives exactly in the gap between `νΦ_c` (strict) and `Peri(Φ_c)` (the gloss). Closing it is
reconciliation, not commitment. The bet decomposes:

- **C1 — the self is `Peri(Φ_c)`, not just `νΦ_c`.** A3's gloss, taken literally — *and now mechanized as
  exactly that*. `Peri μ M := ∀ i j, ‖schur μ M i j‖ = ‖M i j‖` (A3's "sustainable field," read entrywise)
  is proved to be exactly the conserved band (`peri_iff_mem_conservedBand`); `conservedOffdiag μ :=
  ‖μ i j‖ = 1 ∧ i ≠ j` is precisely its off-diagonal part (`conservedOffdiag_iff_offdiag_conserved`) —
  the conserved *off-diagonal* coherence, the self minus its classical (diagonal) record — and the strict
  `νΦ_c` (fixed band) is its `μ = 1` sub-band (`fixedBand_le_conservedBand`). C1 reads the self as
  `Peri(Φ_c)` rather than the strict `νΦ_c` A3 formalizes — a disclosed identification (below), not a
  derivation.
- **C2 — exhaustiveness: only the peripheral self persists.** Off the conserved band everything decays
  (`transient_decays`, the entrywise `spectral_decay`); on it everything is sustained
  (`conservedBand_sustained`). So persisting ⟺ peripheral.

**The payoff** (`band_coincidence_from_axioms`): with the seam *read* as the decoherence-free off-diagonal
block (C1), and given only that the channel is **contractive**
(`Contractive μ`, the ℂ-shadow of `Decoherence.defectSq_attend_le`: attention never amplifies — an
A1/CP baseline fact) and **nondegenerate** (`μ i j = 1 ↔ i = j`, the A3 baseline), `align_of_contractive`
proves `Align μ (conservedOffdiag μ)` as a *theorem*, so `seamBand (conservedOffdiag μ) =
rotatingBand μ` holds with no `H_align` hypothesis. (The honest qualifier: once C1 *defines* the seam as the
modulus-one off-diagonal block, `align_of_contractive` reduces to "`‖μ‖ ≤ 1 ∧ ‖μ‖ ≠ 1 ⇒ ‖μ‖ < 1`" — a
near-tautology. The substance is entirely in C1, not in this step.) The genuine `quarterMul` witness is contractive and
nondegenerate, so its coincidence holds **without the bet** (`quarterMul_coincidence_from_axioms`), and
the two-term law follows (`undifferentiated_two_term_from_axioms`).

**What stays a reading.** C1 itself — that the self *is* `Peri(Φ_c)` rather than the strict `νΦ_c` A3
formalizes — is a **disclosed posit/reading**, asserted, not derived. We read it as A3's "sustainable
field" gloss made precise; a reader may instead count it as a fourth posit (`self = Peri(Φ_c)` *is* a
commitment beyond the formalized `νΦ_c`). We no longer argue which — C1 is named and tagged, and the reader
judges. The seam-protection's **phase-blindness** (it protects by position,
not spectral type — so energy is covered exactly as the fixed record is) is witnessed at the band level by
`seam_energy_sustained`. The general-CPTP placement of an arbitrary `Φ_c`'s modulus-1 spectrum into a
commutative decoherence-free subalgebra (`SpectralDecay` / `PeripheralAlgebra`'s narrated gap) stays
`[open]`, discharged here only on the witness.
